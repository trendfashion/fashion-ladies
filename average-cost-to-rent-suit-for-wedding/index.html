Average Cost To Rent A Suit For Wedding Everything You Need To Know

Written by Robby Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Weddings are a memorable occasion that brings joy and happiness to everyone involved. However, planning a wedding can be quite stressful and overwhelming. One of the most important things to consider is the attire of the groom and groomsmen. The cost of renting a suit for a wedding can be a major pain point for many couples. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will break down everything you need to know about the average cost to rent a suit for a wedding.

The cost of renting a suit for a wedding varies depending on several factors such as location, the number of suits needed, the style of the suit, and the duration of the rental. However, on average, the cost to rent a suit for a wedding can range from $150 to $300. This cost typically includes the jacket, pants, shirt, tie, and shoes. Some rental companies may offer additional accessories such as cufflinks or pocket squares for an extra fee.

To save money on your wedding suit rental, consider renting from a company that offers package deals for groomsmen. This can help you to negotiate a lower price and save money overall. Additionally, consider renting during the off-season or on weekdays as rental prices tend to be lower during these times.

In summary, the average cost to rent a suit for a wedding can range from $150 to $300. To save money, consider renting from a company that offers package deals or renting during the off-season or on weekdays.

Factors Affecting the Cost to Rent a Suit for a Wedding

The cost to rent a suit for a wedding can vary depending on several factors. Here are some of the factors that can affect the cost:

Location

The location of your wedding can impact the cost to rent a suit. Rental prices tend to be higher in major cities and tourist destinations compared to smaller towns.

Number of Suits Needed

The number of suits needed can impact the cost to rent a suit. Rental companies may offer discounts for larger orders, so consider renting all the suits from the same company to save money.

Style of the Suit

The style of the suit can also impact the cost to rent a suit. Higher-end suits with more modern designs may cost more to rent than traditional suits.

Our Experience with Renting a Wedding Suit

When my brother got married, I was the best man and needed to rent a suit for the occasion. We decided to rent from a local rental company that offered a package deal for groomsmen. This package included the suit, shoes, shirt, and tie for a total cost of $250. We were able to negotiate a lower price for the groom's suit since we rented all the suits from the same company.

Tips for Renting a Suit for a Wedding

Here are some tips to keep in mind when renting a suit for a wedding:

1. Rent from a company that offers package deals for groomsmen.

By renting all the suits from the same company, you may be able to negotiate a lower price and save money overall.

2. Rent during the off-season or on weekdays.

Rental prices tend to be lower during the off-season or on weekdays, so consider renting during these times to save money.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I rent a suit for just a few hours?

A: Yes, most rental companies offer hourly rates for suit rentals. However, keep in mind that the rental price may still be high even for a few hours.

Q: Do rental companies offer alterations?

A: Yes, most rental companies offer alterations for an extra fee. However, it's best to rent a suit that fits well to avoid the need for alterations.

Q: How far in advance should I rent a suit for a wedding?

A: It's best to rent a suit at least 2-3 months in advance to ensure availability and to allow time for alterations if needed.

Q: Can I buy a suit instead of renting?

A: Yes, you can buy a suit instead of renting. However, buying a suit can be more expensive than renting and may not be worth the investment if you don't plan on wearing it again.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the average cost to rent a suit for a wedding can range from $150 to $300. To save money, consider renting from a company that offers package deals or renting during the off-season or on weekdays. Remember to take into account the factors that can affect the cost when planning to rent a suit. By following these tips and keeping these factors in mind, you can find the perfect suit for your wedding at an affordable price.
