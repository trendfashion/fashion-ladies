Q: How many baby clothes do I need?

Q: What sizes should I buy for my newborn?

A: Newborns typically wear size 0-3 months, but it's always a good idea to have a few smaller and larger sizes on hand just in case.

Q: What should I look for in baby clothes?

A: Look for clothes that are made from soft, breathable fabrics, easy to put on and take off, and machine washable. Additionally, make sure the clothing is safe and free from any potential choking hazards.
