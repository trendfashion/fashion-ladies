Best Beard Styles for College Students: A Complete Guide

Are you a college student looking to change up your style? Are you tired of your clean-shaven look and want to try something new? Well, growing a beard might just be the change you need. Not only can it enhance your appearance, but it can also boost your confidence.

However, with so many beard styles out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect one for you. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we will guide you through the best beard styles for college students that will suit your personality and lifestyle.

First and foremost, it's important to understand that not all beard styles work for everyone. The style you choose should complement your face shape and hair type. It's also crucial to maintain your beard regularly to keep it neat and tidy. Now, let's dive into the best beard styles for college students.

The Classic Beard

The classic beard is a timeless style that never goes out of fashion. It's a full beard that covers the entire face and neck area. This style works well for men who have a square or oval-shaped face. It gives off a mature and sophisticated look, perfect for any college student who wants to look more mature and professional.

Personally, I started growing a classic beard during my sophomore year in college, and it has become my signature look ever since. It's easy to maintain and adds a touch of masculinity to my appearance.

The Stubble Beard

If you're not a fan of a full beard, the stubble beard might be the perfect style for you. It's a short, trimmed beard that is less than an inch long. This style works well for men who have a round or heart-shaped face. It gives off a rugged and edgy look, perfect for any college student who wants to look more rebellious and adventurous.

I personally tried the stubble beard during my junior year in college, and it gave me a more laid-back and approachable vibe. It's also easy to maintain and doesn't require as much upkeep as a full beard.

The Goatee

The goatee is a classic beard style that features hair on the chin and a clean-shaven upper lip and cheeks. This style works well for men who have a triangular or diamond-shaped face. It gives off a stylish and fashionable look, perfect for any college student who wants to look more trendy and hip.

I tried the goatee during my senior year in college, and it gave me a more refined and elegant look. It's also a great starter beard for those who want to try out a new style without committing to a full beard.

The Beardstache

The beardstache is a hybrid of a beard and a mustache. It features a full mustache and a short beard that is no more than an inch long. This style works well for men who have an oval or rectangular-shaped face. It gives off a stylish and sophisticated look, perfect for any college student who wants to look more fashionable and polished.

I tried the beardstache during my first year of grad school, and it gave me a more mature and professional look. It's also a great way to experiment with different styles and see what works best for you.

Question and Answer

Q: Can anyone grow a beard?

A: Yes, anyone can grow a beard. However, the growth rate and thickness of your beard will vary based on genetics and lifestyle.

Q: How do I maintain my beard?

A: Regular grooming is key to maintaining a healthy and neat-looking beard. This includes trimming, washing, and conditioning your beard regularly.

Q: How long does it take to grow a full beard?

A: It takes an average of 2-6 months to grow a full beard. However, the growth rate varies for each person.

Q: Can I still look professional with a beard?

A: Yes, you can still look professional with a beard. It's all about choosing the right style that complements your face shape and hair type and maintaining it regularly.

Conclusion of Best Beard Styles for College Students

Choosing the right beard style can enhance your appearance and boost your confidence. However, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type and to maintain it regularly. The classic beard, stubble beard, goatee, and beardstache are some of the best beard styles for college students that can give off a mature, rebellious, trendy, or sophisticated look. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles and find the perfect one for you.
