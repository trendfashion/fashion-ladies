Are you a boxing enthusiast looking for the best fights this year? Look no further! The world of boxing has seen some thrilling matches in 2021, leaving fans in awe of the fighters' dedication, endurance, and skill. Whether you are watching from the comfort of your home or at a live event, these matches are sure to give you an adrenaline rush.

Boxing is more than just two fighters in a ring. It's about the strategy, power, and technique that go into each punch. Watching a boxing match can be an emotional rollercoaster, as you witness the highs and lows of each round. But with so many fights happening, it can be overwhelming to decide which ones to watch.

So, what are the best boxing fights this year? Let's dive in and explore some of the most exciting matches that have taken place in 2021.

The Trilogy Fight: Tyson Fury vs Deontay Wilder III

One of the most anticipated fights of the year was the third match between Tyson Fury and Deontay Wilder. After their first two bouts ended in a draw and a Fury victory, respectively, fans were eager to see who would come out on top in the trilogy fight. And boy, did they deliver.

The fight was held on October 9th, 2021, at the T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas. Both fighters came in with a lot to prove, and they did not disappoint. The match was a back-and-forth battle, with each fighter landing powerful blows. But in the end, Fury came out on top, winning by technical knockout in the eleventh round.

The Unification Fight: Canelo Alvarez vs Caleb Plant

Another highly anticipated match was the unification fight between Canelo Alvarez and Caleb Plant. The two fighters had a heated exchange at a press conference, and fans were eager to see if that energy would translate into the ring.

The fight took place on November 6th, 2021, at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas. From the start, it was clear that both fighters were in it to win it. But Canelo's experience and power proved to be too much for Plant, and he won by technical knockout in the eleventh round.

The Upset: Josh Taylor vs Jose Ramirez

One of the biggest upsets of the year was the match between Josh Taylor and Jose Ramirez. Taylor came in as the underdog, but he proved to be a force to be reckoned with.

The fight took place on May 22nd, 2021, at the Virgin Hotels Las Vegas. Taylor dominated from the start, landing powerful punches and controlling the pace of the fight. In the end, he won by unanimous decision, becoming the undisputed super lightweight champion.

The Exhibition Fight: Floyd Mayweather vs Logan Paul

While not a traditional boxing match, the exhibition fight between Floyd Mayweather and Logan Paul was one of the most talked-about events of the year. Mayweather, one of the greatest boxers of all time, took on Paul, a YouTuber with no professional boxing experience.

The fight took place on June 6th, 2021, at the Hard Rock Stadium in Miami. While many fans were skeptical of the match, it turned out to be more competitive than expected. Mayweather landed more punches, but Paul held his own and managed to go the distance, lasting all eight rounds.

Conclusion of Best Boxing Fights This Year
