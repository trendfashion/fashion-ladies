Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for in a bridal lehenga designer?

A: When choosing a bridal lehenga designer, it is essential to look for someone who can understand your vision and bring it to life. Look for designers who have experience creating lehengas that suit your style and who use quality fabrics and intricate embroidery. It is also important to find a designer who can guide you in choosing the right colors, embroideries, and accessories to create a cohesive look.

Q: How much should I budget for a bridal lehenga?

A: Bridal lehengas can range from INR 50,000 to INR 5 lakhs or more, depending on the designer and the quality of the fabrics and embroidery. It is essential to set a budget before you start shopping and to stick to it to avoid overspending.

Q: How long does it take to get a bridal lehenga made?

A: The time it takes to get a bridal lehenga made can vary depending on the designer and the complexity of the design. It is best to start shopping for your lehenga at least six months before your wedding to allow enough time for fittings and alterations.

Q: How do I take care of my bridal lehenga?

A: Bridal lehengas are often made from delicate fabrics and intricate embroidery, so it is essential to handle them with care. It is best to store your lehenga in a cool, dry place and to avoid exposing it to direct sunlight or moisture. It is also important to get your lehenga dry cleaned by a professional after your wedding to ensure that it stays in good condition.
