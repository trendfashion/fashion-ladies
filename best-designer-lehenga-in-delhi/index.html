Find The Best Designer Lehenga In Delhi For Your Big Day

Written by Bardi Sep 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you getting married soon and looking for the perfect designer lehenga in Delhi? Or perhaps you’re attending a wedding as a guest and want to stand out with a stunning outfit. Whatever the occasion may be, finding the best designer lehenga in Delhi can be a daunting task. With so many options and styles available, it’s hard to know where to start.

Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through everything you need to know to find the best designer lehenga in Delhi, from where to shop to what to look for.

When it comes to finding the best designer lehenga in Delhi, it’s important to have a clear idea of what you’re looking for. Are you going for a traditional or modern look? Do you want intricate embroidery or simple elegance? Once you have a rough idea of what you want, it’s time to start browsing.

Where to Shop for the Best Designer Lehenga in Delhi

One of the best places to start your search for the best designer lehenga in Delhi is at a multi-designer store. These stores offer a wide range of designer lehengas from different designers, which means you’ll have plenty of options to choose from. Some of the best multi-designer stores in Delhi include Ogaan, Ensemble, and Aza.

Another option is to visit the designer’s flagship store. If you have a particular designer in mind, it’s worth visiting their store to see their latest collection and get a feel for their style. Some of the best designer stores in Delhi include Sabyasachi, Manish Malhotra, and Anita Dongre.

What to Look for in the Best Designer Lehenga in Delhi

When it comes to finding the best designer lehenga in Delhi, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, make sure the lehenga fits you well and is comfortable to wear. You don’t want to spend your big day adjusting your outfit or feeling uncomfortable.

Secondly, pay attention to the fabric and embellishments. The fabric should be of good quality and the embellishments should be well-crafted and not prone to falling off. Finally, make sure the lehenga suits your body type and skin tone.

How to Style the Best Designer Lehenga in Delhi

Once you’ve found the best designer lehenga in Delhi, it’s time to style it. A traditional lehenga can be paired with a matching dupatta and statement jewelry, while a more modern lehenga can be paired with a crop top and minimal jewelry. Don’t forget to also consider your hair and makeup, which should complement your outfit.

Question and Answer Section

Q. How much should I expect to spend on a designer lehenga in Delhi?

A. The price of a designer lehenga in Delhi can vary greatly depending on the designer, fabric, and embellishments. You can expect to spend anywhere from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 5 lakhs or more.

Q. How long does it take to get a designer lehenga made?

A. The time it takes to get a designer lehenga made can vary depending on the designer and the complexity of the design. It’s best to check with the designer or store for a specific timeline.

Q. Can I rent a designer lehenga in Delhi?

A. Yes, there are several stores in Delhi that offer designer lehenga rentals. This can be a more affordable option if you don’t want to invest in a lehenga that you may only wear once.

Q. What should I wear with a designer lehenga in Delhi?

A. You can pair a traditional lehenga with a matching dupatta and statement jewelry, while a more modern lehenga can be paired with a crop top and minimal jewelry. Don’t forget to also consider your hair and makeup, which should complement your outfit.

Conclusion of Best Designer Lehenga in Delhi

When it comes to finding the best designer lehenga in Delhi, it’s important to have a clear idea of what you want and where to look. Whether you choose to shop at a multi-designer store or visit the designer’s flagship store, pay attention to the fit, fabric, and embellishments. And don’t forget to style your lehenga with the right jewelry, hair, and makeup to complete your look.
