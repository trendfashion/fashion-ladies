Key Features of Best ERP Software for Garment Manufacturing Company in India

The best ERP software for garment manufacturing company in India should have the following key features:

Production Planning

Inventory Management

The software should have an inventory management module that helps you to track your inventory levels, monitor stock movements, and avoid stock-outs. It should provide real-time visibility into your inventory, enabling you to make informed decisions.
