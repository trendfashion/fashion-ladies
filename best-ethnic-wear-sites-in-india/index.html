Best Ethnic Wear Sites in India: Where to Shop for Traditional Indian Clothing Online

Are you looking for the best ethnic wear sites in India? Do you want to buy traditional Indian clothing online but don't know where to start? Shopping for ethnic wear can be overwhelming, especially if you're not familiar with the different styles and fabrics. Plus, not all online stores offer high-quality products or reliable shipping. In this post, we'll guide you through the best ethnic wear sites in India and help you make an informed decision.

If you're an Indian living abroad, you may miss wearing traditional clothes for festivals, weddings, or other special occasions. Or, you may want to gift your loved ones back home with authentic Indian attire. On the other hand, if you're a foreigner visiting India, you may want to experience the vibrant colors and designs of Indian clothing. Whatever your reason is, buying ethnic wear online is convenient, cost-effective, and offers a wide range of options.

So, what are the best ethnic wear sites in India? There are many online stores that sell Indian clothing, but not all of them are trustworthy or offer a good shopping experience. To narrow down your search, we've curated a list of the top ethnic wear sites in India based on factors like quality, variety, customer service, and pricing. Here are our top picks:

Biba

Biba is one of the most popular ethnic wear brands in India, known for its fusion of traditional and modern styles. The online store offers a vast collection of sarees, salwar suits, kurtas, lehengas, and accessories for women, as well as a men's section. Biba's products are made from high-quality fabrics and come in various sizes, colors, and designs. The website is user-friendly and has a customer-friendly return policy, making it a reliable choice for online shopping.

Jabong

Jabong is a leading fashion and lifestyle e-commerce platform in India that offers a wide range of ethnic wear for men, women, and kids. The website features over 1000 brands and 90,000 products, including sarees, salwar suits, kurtas, lehengas, and fusion wear. Jabong offers free shipping, easy returns, and secure payments, making it a go-to destination for fashion enthusiasts. The website also has a fashion blog and a mobile app for convenient shopping on-the-go.

Myntra

Myntra is another popular online fashion store in India that offers a vast collection of ethnic wear for men, women, and kids. The website features over 2000 brands and 3 lakh products, including sarees, salwar suits, kurtas, lehengas, and fusion wear. Myntra offers free shipping, easy returns, and cash on delivery, making it a hassle-free shopping experience. The website also has a style quiz and a virtual makeover tool to help you choose the perfect outfit.

Craftsvilla

Craftsvilla is a unique online store that specializes in handmade and ethnic products from India. The website features over 50,000 artisans and designers from all over India, offering a diverse range of ethnic wear, jewelry, home decor, and more. Craftsvilla's products are authentic, affordable, and support the local community. The website also offers international shipping and a 7-day return policy, making it a great choice for unique and sustainable shopping.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best ethnic wear for men in India?

A: Ethnic wear for men includes kurtas, sherwanis, dhotis, and bandhgalas. Some of the best ethnic wear brands for men in India are Manyavar, FabIndia, Raymond, and Peter England.

Q: What is the best fabric for ethnic wear in India?

A: The best fabric for ethnic wear in India depends on the occasion, climate, and personal preference. Some popular fabrics include cotton, silk, chiffon, georgette, and crepe.

Q: How to choose the right size for ethnic wear online?

A: To choose the right size for ethnic wear online, you should refer to the size chart provided by the website and measure yourself accurately. You can also read customer reviews and check if the website offers free returns or exchanges.

Q: What are the latest trends in ethnic wear in India?

A: The latest trends in ethnic wear in India include fusion wear, pastel colors, floral prints, cape sleeves, and statement jewelry. However, traditional styles like sarees, salwar suits, and lehengas are always in fashion.

Conclusion of Best Ethnic Wear Sites in India

Shopping for ethnic wear online can be a fun and easy way to add some Indian flair to your wardrobe or gift collection. With so many options available, it's important to choose a reliable and high-quality online store. Our list of the best ethnic wear sites in India includes Biba, Jabong, Myntra, and Craftsvilla, which offer a wide range of products, affordable prices, and excellent customer service. Whether you're looking for a bridal lehenga or a casual kurta, these online stores have got you covered. Happy shopping!
