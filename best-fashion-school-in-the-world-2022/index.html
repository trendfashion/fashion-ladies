The Importance of Location

When choosing the best fashion school in the world 2022, location is an important consideration. Attending a fashion school in a major fashion capital also provides students with access to fashion events, shows, and exhibitions that may not be available in other locations. This exposure to the industry can be invaluable in building a successful career in fashion.
