Top Genuine Leather Belt Brands In India

Written by Aprilia Sep 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly buying new belts that only last a few months? Do you want a high-quality leather belt that will stand the test of time? Look no further than the top genuine leather belt brands in India.

When it comes to purchasing a leather belt, the quality of the material is key. Many belts on the market claim to be made of leather but are actually made of synthetic materials that won't last. Investing in a genuine leather belt is worth it in the long run, but with so many options out there, it can be difficult to know where to start.

In this article, we'll explore the best genuine leather belt brands in India, what sets them apart, and how to choose the perfect belt for you.

The Top Genuine Leather Belt Brands in India

I have personally tried and tested various genuine leather belt brands in India and have found that these three stand out from the rest:

1. Hidesign: Hidesign is an Indian brand that has been producing high-quality leather goods since the 1970s. Their belts are made of full-grain leather and come in a variety of styles, from classic to trendy. They also offer a lifetime warranty on their products.

2. Woodland: Woodland is a well-known brand in India that has been around since 1992. Their leather belts are made of high-quality leather and come in a range of colors and designs. They also offer a one-year warranty on their products.

3. Da Milano: Da Milano is a luxury brand that offers a wide range of leather goods, including belts. Their belts are made of premium leather and feature unique designs that stand out from the crowd.

What to Look for in a Genuine Leather Belt

When shopping for a genuine leather belt, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Quality of Leather: Look for belts made of full-grain leather, which is the highest quality leather available. Avoid belts made of top-grain or corrected-grain leather, as they are lower quality and won't last as long.

2. Buckle: The buckle should be made of high-quality metal and should match the style of the belt.

3. Stitching: Look for belts with tight stitching that runs the entire length of the belt. Loose stitching is a sign of poor quality.

4. Size: Make sure to measure your waist before purchasing a belt to ensure a proper fit. A belt that is too small or too large will not only look awkward but will also wear out faster.

How to Care for Your Genuine Leather Belt

To ensure your genuine leather belt lasts as long as possible, be sure to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

1. Clean it regularly: Use a damp cloth to wipe down your belt regularly to remove dirt and sweat. Avoid using harsh chemicals or soap.

2. Condition it: Use a leather conditioner to keep the leather soft and supple. Apply it every few months or as needed.

3. Store it properly: Store your belt in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid hanging it, as this can stretch out the leather.

FAQs About Genuine Leather Belt Brands in India

Q: What is the difference between full-grain and top-grain leather?

A: Full-grain leather is the highest quality leather available, as it is made from the top layer of the hide and retains the natural grain of the leather. Top-grain leather is lower quality, as it is made from the second layer of the hide and has been sanded and buffed to remove imperfections.

Q: How long should a genuine leather belt last?

A: With proper care, a genuine leather belt can last for several years.

Q: Can I wear a leather belt with any outfit?

A: Yes, a leather belt is a versatile accessory that can be worn with a variety of outfits, from casual to formal.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a genuine leather belt?

A: The price of a genuine leather belt can vary depending on the brand and style, but expect to pay at least Rs. 1000 for a high-quality belt.

Conclusion

Investing in a high-quality genuine leather belt is a smart choice that will pay off in the long run. By choosing a reputable brand and taking good care of your belt, you can enjoy it for years to come. Consider one of the top genuine leather belt brands in India, such as Hidesign, Woodland, or Da Milano, for a belt that is both stylish and durable.
