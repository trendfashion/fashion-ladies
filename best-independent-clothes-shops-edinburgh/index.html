Incredible Best Independent Clothes Shops Edinburgh Trend In 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for unique and stylish clothes in Edinburgh, independent boutiques are the way to go. Not only will you find one-of-a-kind pieces, but you'll also be supporting local businesses. In this blog post, we'll be sharing the best independent clothes shops Edinburgh has to offer.

It can be overwhelming when searching for the perfect outfit, especially with so many options available. It's also frustrating when you can't find what you're looking for in mainstream stores. Independent clothes shops offer a refreshing change from the usual high street shops, providing a more personal and unique shopping experience.

So, what are the best independent clothes shops in Edinburgh? We've done the research so you don't have to. Here are our top picks:

1. Godiva Boutique

Located on West Port, Godiva Boutique offers a range of vintage-inspired clothing, accessories, and jewellery. The shop has a rockabilly vibe and caters to those who love the 50s and 60s fashion. You'll find everything from swing dresses to leather jackets.

2. Herman Brown

If you're looking for luxury vintage clothing, Herman Brown is the place to go. The shop has a wide range of designer clothing and accessories from the 1920s to the 1980s. The pieces are carefully curated and are of the highest quality.

3. Those Were The Days

Those Were The Days is a vintage clothing shop located in the Grassmarket. The shop has a vast selection of vintage clothing from the 1920s to the 1980s. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly and will be happy to help you find the perfect outfit.

4. Armstrong's Vintage Emporium

Armstrong's Vintage Emporium is another vintage clothing shop located in the Grassmarket. The shop has a vast selection of vintage clothing and accessories from the 1800s to the 1990s. You'll find everything from Victorian dresses to 90s grunge.

Personal Experience

My personal favourite is Godiva Boutique. I love the vintage-inspired clothing and the rockabilly vibe. The staff is always friendly and helpful, and I always leave with something unique.

Why Choose Independent Clothes Shops?

Independent clothes shops offer a more personal and unique shopping experience. The pieces are often one of a kind, so you won't have to worry about someone else wearing the same outfit as you. You'll also be supporting local businesses, which is always a good thing.

How to Shop at Independent Clothes Shops

When shopping at independent clothes shops, it's essential to keep an open mind. You never know what you might find. It's also a good idea to try things on, as the sizing can be different from what you're used to. Lastly, don't be afraid to ask the staff for help. They're knowledgeable and will be happy to assist you in finding the perfect outfit.

Benefits of Shopping at Independent Clothes Shops

Shopping at independent clothes shops has many benefits. You'll find unique pieces that you won't find in mainstream stores. You'll also be supporting local businesses, which is good for the local economy. Lastly, you'll be helping to reduce the environmental impact of fast fashion.

Conclusion of Best Independent Clothes Shops Edinburgh

Edinburgh has many independent clothes shops to choose from, each with its unique style and vibe. Whether you're looking for vintage clothing or something more modern, you're sure to find something that will suit your taste. Remember to keep an open mind and try things on, and don't be afraid to ask for help. Happy shopping!

Question and Answer

Q: Are independent clothes shops more expensive than high street shops?

A: It depends on the shop and the items you're looking for. Some independent clothes shops may be more expensive, but you'll find that the quality is often higher, and the pieces are unique.

Q: Do independent clothes shops only sell vintage clothing?

A: No, independent clothes shops sell a range of clothing, from vintage to modern. You'll find something for every style and taste.

Q: Are there any eco-friendly independent clothes shops in Edinburgh?

A: Yes, there are several eco-friendly independent clothes shops in Edinburgh. One example is Godiva Boutique, which offers a range of eco-friendly clothing and accessories.

Q: Can I find designer clothing at independent clothes shops?

A: Yes, you can find designer clothing at independent clothes shops. Herman Brown is an excellent example of a shop that offers high-end designer clothing and accessories.
