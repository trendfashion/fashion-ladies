Question and Answer

Q: How much does a saree cost in Dubai?

A: The price of a saree in Dubai can vary greatly, depending on the quality of the fabric and the design. However, you can expect to pay anywhere from AED 100 to AED 10,000 for a saree in Dubai.

Q: Can I bargain for the price of a saree in Dubai?

A: Yes, you can often negotiate the price of a saree in Dubai, especially in smaller shops. However, it's essential to be respectful and polite when bargaining.

Q: How do I know if a saree is of good quality?

A: When shopping for a saree, it's essential to look for high-quality fabric that is soft to the touch and drapes well. Additionally, look for neat embroidery or embellishments and make sure the saree is well-made.
