Best Leather Motorcycle Jackets 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Motorcycle jackets are an essential part of any biker's wardrobe. Not only do they provide protection from the elements, but they also offer a layer of protection in case of an accident. Choosing the right leather motorcycle jacket can be overwhelming, especially with the variety of styles and brands available in the market. In this article, we will explore the best leather motorcycle jackets 2020 has to offer and guide you through the process of finding the perfect one for you.

When it comes to purchasing a leather motorcycle jacket, there are a few pain points that riders commonly face. Firstly, the cost can be a deterrent. High-quality leather jackets can be expensive, and not everyone is willing to invest in one. Secondly, the fit can be an issue. Leather jackets tend to be stiff at first and may take some time to break in. Lastly, there is the question of style. With so many options available, it can be challenging to find a jacket that is not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best leather motorcycle jackets 2020 has to offer. We will highlight the features that make each jacket stand out, the pros and cons of each, and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed choice.

In summary, this article will cover the best leather motorcycle jackets 2020 has to offer, including their features, pros, and cons, to help you find the perfect one for you.

Top Picks for Best Leather Motorcycle Jackets 2020

One of the best leather motorcycle jackets 2020 has to offer is the Alpinestars GP Plus R v3 Leather Jacket. This jacket is made from premium leather and is ergonomically designed to provide maximum comfort and protection. It features a removable thermal liner, multiple pockets, and CE-certified armor in the elbows and shoulders.

Another great option is the Dainese Super Rider D-Dry Jacket. This jacket is made from cowhide leather and features waterproof and breathable D-Dry technology. It also includes removable composite elbow and shoulder protectors and a back protector pocket.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Leather Motorcycle Jacket

When choosing a leather motorcycle jacket, there are various factors to consider. Firstly, you need to consider the type of riding you will be doing. If you will be doing long-distance touring, you may want a jacket with more pockets for storage. If you will be riding in hot weather, you may want a jacket with ventilation. Secondly, you need to consider the level of protection you want. Look for jackets with CE-certified armor in the elbows and shoulders, as well as a back protector. Finally, you need to consider the style of the jacket. Choose a style that not only looks good but also fits in with your overall aesthetic.

How to Choose the Right Size

Choosing the right size for your leather motorcycle jacket is crucial. A jacket that is too small may restrict your movement, while a jacket that is too big may not provide adequate protection. To find the right size, measure your chest, waist, and hips, and compare them to the manufacturer's sizing chart. It's also a good idea to try on the jacket before purchasing it to ensure a proper fit.

How to Care for Your Leather Motorcycle Jacket

To ensure that your leather motorcycle jacket lasts as long as possible, it's essential to take care of it properly. Avoid exposing it to extreme temperatures or moisture, and store it in a cool, dry place when not in use. Use a leather cleaner and conditioner regularly to keep the leather supple and prevent cracking. It's also a good idea to have the jacket professionally cleaned and reconditioned every few years.

Conclusion of Best Leather Motorcycle Jackets 2020

When it comes to choosing the best leather motorcycle jacket for you, there are various factors to consider. From the type of riding you will be doing to the level of protection you want, there is a perfect jacket out there for everyone. By following the tips outlined in this article, you can find a jacket that not only looks good but also provides the protection you need on the road.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between cowhide and buffalo leather?

A: Cowhide leather is more durable and less expensive than buffalo leather. However, buffalo leather is more abrasion-resistant and tends to be more water-resistant than cowhide.

Q: Are leather motorcycle jackets waterproof?

A: Not all leather motorcycle jackets are waterproof. However, many jackets feature waterproof and breathable technology to keep you dry in wet weather.

Q: How long do leather motorcycle jackets last?

A: With proper care, a high-quality leather motorcycle jacket can last for many years. However, the lifespan of a jacket depends on various factors, including the quality of the leather, how often it is worn, and how well it is cared for.

Q: Can leather motorcycle jackets be repaired?

A: Yes, leather motorcycle jackets can be repaired. Many manufacturers offer repair services, and there are also third-party companies that specialize in leather repair.
