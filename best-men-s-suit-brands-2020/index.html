Top 5 Best Men's Suit Brands 2020: A Comprehensive Guide

Are you in search of the perfect suit? One that will make you look and feel like a million dollars? Look no further! In this article, we will be exploring the top 5 best men's suit brands of 2020. Whether you need a suit for a wedding, job interview, or special event, we've got you covered.

When it comes to finding the perfect suit, it can be overwhelming to navigate through the multitude of options available. From fit to fabric, color to design, there are many factors to consider. Not to mention, the price tag can often be a deterrent. But fear not, we have done the research for you and have compiled a list of the best brands to invest in for a high-quality suit that will last you for years to come.

So, without further ado, let's dive into the top 5 best men's suit brands of 2020:

1. Hugo Boss

My personal favorite on this list is Hugo Boss. Known for their sleek and modern designs, Hugo Boss offers a range of suits that will fit any occasion. Whether you're looking for a classic black suit or a bold patterned one, they have it all. Their suits are made with high-quality materials and are designed to fit like a glove. While Hugo Boss suits may be on the pricier side, they are definitely worth the investment.

2. Brooks Brothers

Brooks Brothers is a classic American brand that has been around since 1818. They are known for their timeless designs and impeccable quality. Their suits are perfect for those who want a traditional look with a modern twist. Brooks Brothers offers a variety of styles, fabrics, and colors to choose from. One thing to note is that their suits tend to run on the slimmer side, so make sure to try them on before purchasing.

3. J.Crew

If you're on a tighter budget, J.Crew is a great option. Their suits are affordable without sacrificing quality. They offer a range of styles and colors, from classic navy to bold plaid. J.Crew suits are perfect for those who want a more casual look, while still looking put-together.

4. Armani

Armani is a luxury Italian brand that is known for its high-end suits. Their designs are sleek and sophisticated, perfect for those who want to make a statement. Armani suits are made with the finest materials and are tailored to perfection. While they may be on the pricier side, they are definitely worth the investment for those who want a suit that will last them a lifetime.

5. SuitSupply

SuitSupply is a Dutch brand that offers high-quality suits at an affordable price. They offer a range of styles and colors, from classic to bold. One thing to note is that SuitSupply suits tend to fit slim, so make sure to try them on before purchasing. Overall, SuitSupply is a great option for those who want a high-quality suit without breaking the bank.

What to Look for in a Men's Suit

When shopping for a men's suit, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First and foremost, fit is everything. A well-fitted suit will make you look and feel confident. Make sure to try on different sizes and styles to find the perfect fit. Secondly, pay attention to the fabric. Look for suits that are made with high-quality materials, such as wool or cashmere. Lastly, consider the occasion. Different occasions call for different styles and colors. For example, a black suit is perfect for a formal occasion, while a navy suit is great for a more casual event.

How to Take Care of Your Suit

Investing in a high-quality suit is only half the battle. It's important to take care of your suit to ensure that it lasts you for years to come. Here are a few tips for suit care:

  • Always hang your suit on a wooden hanger to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid dry-cleaning your suit too often, as it can damage the fabric.
  • Instead, spot-clean your suit with a damp cloth when necessary.
  • Store your suit in a garment bag to protect it from dust and dirt.

FAQs

Q: What is the best brand for a classic black suit?

A: Hugo Boss and Brooks Brothers both offer great options for a classic black suit.

Q: What is the best brand for a more casual suit?

A: J.Crew and SuitSupply both offer great options for a more casual suit.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a high-quality suit?

A: Depending on the brand and quality, you can expect to spend anywhere from $500 to $2000 on a high-quality suit.

Q: How often should I dry clean my suit?

A: It's recommended to dry clean your suit once a season, or every three to six months.

Conclusion

Investing in a high-quality suit is a must for any man. Whether you're attending a wedding, job interview, or special event, a well-fitted suit will make you look and feel confident. The top 5 best men's suit brands of 2020 are Hugo Boss, Brooks Brothers, J.Crew, Armani, and SuitSupply. When shopping for a suit, make sure to keep fit, fabric, and occasion in mind. And once you've found the perfect suit, make sure to take care of it properly to ensure that it lasts you for years to come.
