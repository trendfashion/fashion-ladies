Question and Answer

Q: How do I know which walking sneaker is best for me?

A: It is essential to consider your foot type, walking style, and any foot issues you may have, such as plantar fasciitis. Additionally, try on different walking sneakers and walk around in them to ensure a comfortable fit and adequate support.

Q: How often should I replace my walking sneakers?

A: It is recommended to replace your walking sneakers every 300-500 miles or every six months, whichever comes first. This ensures that your sneakers provide adequate support and cushioning for your feet.

Q: Can I use my running sneakers for walking?

A: While running sneakers may provide adequate support and cushioning for walking, it is recommended to use walking sneakers for walking. Walking sneakers provide more support in the midsole and heel, which is essential for walking.

Q: Do I need to break in my walking sneakers?

A: It is recommended to break in your walking sneakers before using them for long periods. This allows your feet to adjust to the new shoes and ensures a comfortable fit.
