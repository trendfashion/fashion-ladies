Where To Buy The Best Formal Shirts A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have an upcoming event that requires you to wear a formal shirt? Are you tired of the limited options and high prices at your local department stores? Finding the best place to buy formal shirts can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we will explore the top options for purchasing high-quality formal shirts at affordable prices.

If you're in the market for a formal shirt, you're likely looking for something that fits well, looks great, and won't break the bank. Unfortunately, many brick-and-mortar stores charge a premium for formal wear, leaving shoppers feeling frustrated and discouraged.

The best place to buy formal shirts is online. Not only do online retailers offer a wider selection of styles and sizes, but they also tend to have lower prices than traditional stores. Plus, you can shop from the comfort of your own home and have your purchase delivered right to your doorstep.

Online Retailers

One of the best places to buy formal shirts online is through retailers like Amazon, Nordstrom, and Macy's. These websites offer a wide variety of styles and sizes at competitive prices. Additionally, they often have user reviews and ratings to help you make an informed decision.

Personally, I have had great experiences shopping for formal shirts on Amazon. The website has a vast selection of brands and styles, and the prices are often unbeatable. Plus, Amazon Prime members can enjoy free two-day shipping on eligible items.

Bespoke Tailors

If you're looking for a truly personalized experience, consider visiting a bespoke tailor. These professionals will take your measurements and create a custom shirt that fits you perfectly. While bespoke shirts can be more expensive than off-the-rack options, the quality and fit are unparalleled.

A few noteworthy bespoke tailors include J.Hilburn, Proper Cloth, and Ratio Clothing. These companies offer online measurement tools and an array of fabrics and styles to choose from.

Outlet Stores

If you're on a tight budget, outlet stores can be a great option for finding affordable formal wear. These stores sell overstocked and slightly imperfect items from designer brands at a discount. While the selection may be limited, you can often find high-quality shirts at a fraction of the original price.

Secondhand Shops

Finally, don't discount the value of secondhand shops when it comes to finding formal wear. Thrift stores and consignment shops often have gently used items from well-known brands at a fraction of the original cost. Plus, buying secondhand is an eco-friendly option that helps reduce waste in the fashion industry.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying a formal shirt?

A: When shopping for a formal shirt, look for a classic style that fits well and complements your body type. Pay attention to the fabric quality and make sure it is comfortable to wear.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a formal shirt?

A: The price of a formal shirt can vary widely depending on the brand and quality. You can find affordable options for under $50, while high-end bespoke shirts can cost $200 or more.

Q: Can I wear a formal shirt with jeans?

A: It depends on the occasion and dress code. While a formal shirt can be dressed down with jeans, it may not be appropriate for all events.

Q: How do I care for my formal shirt?

A: Always follow the care instructions on the label. In general, it's best to wash formal shirts in cold water and hang them to dry. Avoid using a dryer, as this can cause shrinkage and damage to the fabric.

Conclusion of Best Place to Buy Formal Shirts

When it comes to buying formal shirts, there are plenty of options to choose from. The key is to find a retailer that offers high-quality items at a price that fits your budget. Whether you opt for an online retailer, bespoke tailor, outlet store, or secondhand shop, you can find the perfect formal shirt for your next event.
