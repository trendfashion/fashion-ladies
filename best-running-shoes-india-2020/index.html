Best Running Shoes India 2020: My Personal Experience

My experience with finding the best running shoes in India was initially overwhelming. With so many brands and models to choose from, I felt lost and confused. Types of Running Shoes Available in India

Before purchasing running shoes, it's essential to understand the different types of shoes available in the Indian market. This knowledge will help you determine which type of shoe is best suited to your needs and preferences.

The three main types of running shoes available in India are:

  1. Cushioned Running Shoes
  2. Stability Running Shoes
  3. Minimalist Running Shoes

Cushioned Running Shoes

Cushioned running shoes are designed to provide maximum shock absorption and cushioning to your feet. These shoes are ideal for runners who have high arches or need extra cushioning to prevent injuries. Cushioned shoes are generally heavier and provide a lot of support, making them the perfect choice for long-distance runners.

Top Cushioned Running Shoes in India 2020

Some of the best-cushioned running shoes available in India in 2020 are:

  • Adidas Ultraboost 20
  • Nike Air Zoom Pegasus 37
  • ASICS Gel-Nimbus 22

Stability Running Shoes

Stability running shoes are specially designed to provide support and stability to runners who overpronate. Overpronation occurs when your foot rolls inward excessively while running, leading to injuries and discomfort. Stability shoes have a medial post that provides extra support to the arch of your foot, preventing overpronation.

Top Stability Running Shoes in India 2020

Some of the best stability running shoes available in India in 2020 are:

  • ASICS Gel-Kayano 26
  • Brooks Adrenaline GTS 20
  • New Balance Fresh Foam 860v10

Minimalist Running Shoes

Minimalist running shoes are designed to mimic the feeling of running barefoot. These shoes are lightweight and have minimal cushioning, allowing you to feel the ground beneath your feet. Minimalist shoes are ideal for runners who want to improve their running form and foot strength.

Top Minimalist Running Shoes in India 2020

Some of the best minimalist running shoes available in India in 2020 are:

  • Merrell Vapor Glove 4
  • Vibram FiveFingers V-Trail 2.0
  • Altra Escalante 2

Factors to Consider While Purchasing Running Shoes

When purchasing running shoes, there are several factors to consider to ensure that you select the best pair for your needs:

  • Foot Type: Determine your foot type, whether it's flat, neutral or high-arched, to find the right shoes that cater to your needs.
  • Running Style: Consider your running style, whether it's heel striking or forefoot striking, to find shoes that provide adequate support and cushioning.
  • Shoe Size: Ensure that you have the correct shoe size and that the shoes fit snugly without being too tight or too loose.
  • Brand: Choose reputable brands that have a history of producing high-quality running shoes.

Question and Answer Section

Q: What are the best running shoes for beginners?

A: The best running shoes for beginners are typically those that offer a lot of support and cushioning. Some of the best options include the Nike Air Zoom Pegasus 37, ASICS Gel-Nimbus 22, and Brooks Ghost 12.

Q: Can I wear running shoes for other activities?

A: Running shoes are designed specifically for running and may not be suitable for other activities. However, some running shoes, such as the Nike Free RN, can be used for cross-training and other low-impact activities.

Q: How often should I replace my running shoes?

A: It's generally recommended that you replace your running shoes every 300-500 miles or every six months, whichever comes first.

Q: What should I look for when trying on running shoes?

A: When trying on running shoes, ensure that there is enough space in the toe box and that the shoes fit snugly around your heel. Walk around in the shoes to ensure that they are comfortable and provide adequate support.

Conclusion of Best Running Shoes India 2020

Choosing the best running shoes in India can be a challenging task, but by understanding your needs and preferences, you can find the perfect pair for you. Remember to consider factors such as foot type, running style, shoe size, and brand when making your purchase. Additionally, make sure to invest in a good pair of shoes that are comfortable, supportive, and durable, to ensure that you can continue your fitness journey without any setbacks.
