Best Sustainable Athletic Wear The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jun 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of searching for athletic wear that is both stylish and sustainable? Do you want to make a positive impact on the environment while working out? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best sustainable athletic wear options available in the market.

As consumers, we are becoming more aware of the environmental impact of fast fashion and the textile industry as a whole. This awareness has led to a growing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly athletic wear. However, finding the right options can be overwhelming and confusing.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best sustainable athletic wear options available, including their features, benefits, and drawbacks. We will also share our personal experiences and recommendations.

In summary, this article will be your ultimate guide to sustainable athletic wear, providing you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Organic Cotton Athletic Wear

One of the most popular sustainable athletic wear options is organic cotton. It is grown without the use of harmful chemicals, pesticides, and fertilizers, making it a more sustainable and eco-friendly option than traditional cotton.

Personally, I recommend the brand Patagonia for their organic cotton athletic wear. Their clothes are not only stylish and comfortable but also durable and long-lasting. Plus, they have a lifetime warranty, which means that if their products ever fail, they will repair or replace them for free.

Recycled Polyester Athletic Wear

Another popular sustainable athletic wear option is recycled polyester. This material is made from post-consumer plastic bottles and other recycled materials, reducing waste and minimizing the use of virgin materials.

A brand that I recommend for recycled polyester athletic wear is Girlfriend Collective. Their clothes are not only made from recycled materials but also ethically made in a fair-wage factory in Vietnam. Plus, they offer a range of sizes, from XXS to 6XL, making their products inclusive for all body types.

Bamboo Athletic Wear

Bamboo is another sustainable athletic wear option that is eco-friendly and comfortable. It is soft, moisture-wicking, and naturally antimicrobial, making it a great choice for activewear.

A brand that I recommend for bamboo athletic wear is Boody. Their clothes are made from organic bamboo, making them not only sustainable but also hypoallergenic and breathable. Plus, their clothes are affordable and come in a range of colors and styles.

Hemp Athletic Wear

Hemp is a sustainable and durable material that is perfect for activewear. It is grown without the use of harmful pesticides and fertilizers, and it requires less water than traditional cotton.

A brand that I recommend for hemp athletic wear is WAMA. Their clothes are made from a blend of hemp and organic cotton, making them not only sustainable but also comfortable and breathable. Plus, their products are affordable and come in a range of sizes and styles.

Question and Answer

Q: Is sustainable athletic wear more expensive than traditional athletic wear?

A: It depends on the brand and the materials used. Some sustainable athletic wear brands may be more expensive than traditional brands, but others offer affordable options. It is important to consider the long-term benefits and environmental impact when making a purchase.

Q: Is sustainable athletic wear as durable as traditional athletic wear?

A: Yes, sustainable athletic wear can be just as durable as traditional athletic wear, if not more. Brands that use eco-friendly materials and sustainable production methods often prioritize quality and longevity in their products.

Q: How can I dispose of my old athletic wear sustainably?

A: Instead of throwing away your old athletic wear, consider donating it to a local charity or clothing recycling program. Some brands, such as Patagonia, also offer recycling programs for their products.

Q: Are there any sustainable athletic wear brands that offer plus sizes?

A: Yes, several sustainable athletic wear brands offer plus sizes, including Girlfriend Collective, Patagonia, and WAMA.

Conclusion of Best Sustainable Athletic Wear

There are many sustainable athletic wear options available in the market, from organic cotton to recycled polyester to bamboo and hemp. When choosing sustainable athletic wear, it is important to consider the materials used, the production methods, and the long-term environmental impact. By making a conscious choice, you can make a positive impact on the environment while staying stylish and comfortable during your workouts.
