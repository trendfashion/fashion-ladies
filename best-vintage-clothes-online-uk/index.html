Best Vintage Clothes Online Uk Where To Find Them

Written by Bardi Sep 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old clothes that you see in every high street store? Do you long for unique and timeless pieces that can't be found anywhere else? Look no further than the world of vintage clothing.

For many people, the idea of vintage clothing can be daunting. The thought of sifting through piles of old clothes in a musty thrift store might not sound like a fun shopping experience. However, with the rise of online vintage shops, finding the perfect vintage piece has never been easier.

If you're looking for the best vintage clothes online UK, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, determine what type of vintage clothing you're interested in. Are you looking for 1920s flapper dresses, 1950s circle skirts, or 1970s bohemian pieces? Once you've narrowed down your search, you can start exploring the many online vintage shops that cater to your style.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at the best vintage clothes online UK and where to find them. We'll cover everything from vintage marketplaces to specialist vintage boutiques, so you can find the perfect vintage piece for your wardrobe.

Asos Marketplace: A One-Stop Shop for Vintage

Asos Marketplace is a great place to start your vintage clothing journey. With over 800 boutiques selling vintage clothing, you're sure to find something that suits your style. Asos Marketplace carries everything from vintage designer pieces to affordable vintage finds. Plus, with the ability to search by era or style, finding your perfect vintage piece has never been easier.

My personal experience with Asos Marketplace has been great. I was looking for a vintage 1960s dress for a wedding and found the perfect one on the site. The dress arrived quickly and was exactly as described. I received many compliments on my unique dress, and I felt like a true vintage queen.

Beyond Retro: A Sustainable Vintage Option

Beyond Retro is a vintage clothing staple in the UK. With multiple brick and mortar stores and an extensive online presence, Beyond Retro is a great option for sustainable vintage clothing. They offer a wide range of vintage pieces, from designer labels to affordable finds. Plus, their commitment to sustainability means that you can feel good about your purchase.

When I was looking for a vintage denim jacket, Beyond Retro was my go-to. I found the perfect oversized Levi's jacket on their website and was thrilled with my purchase. The jacket was in great condition and had the perfect worn-in feel.

Vintage Marketplaces: Where to Find One-Of-A-Kind Pieces

If you're looking for truly unique vintage pieces, vintage marketplaces like Etsy and Depop are great options. These online marketplaces allow vintage sellers from all over the world to sell their wares. This means that you'll find pieces that you won't find anywhere else.

My personal experience with Etsy has been amazing. I was looking for a vintage beaded jacket for a special occasion and found the perfect one from a seller in France. The jacket was in great condition and arrived quickly. I received many compliments on my unique jacket and felt like a vintage goddess.

Tips for Buying Vintage Clothes Online

When buying vintage clothes online, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, always check the measurements of the garment. Vintage sizing can vary greatly from modern sizing, so it's important to know your measurements and compare them to the garment's measurements.

Second, read the item description carefully. Vintage clothing will often have some wear and tear, but it's important to know the extent of the damage before purchasing. If the item has any major flaws, make sure they're reflected in the price.

Third, don't be afraid to ask questions. If you're unsure about something, reach out to the seller and ask for more information or photos. Most vintage sellers are happy to help and want you to be happy with your purchase.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Clothes Online UK

When it comes to finding the best vintage clothes online UK, there are plenty of options available. Whether you're looking for designer vintage or affordable finds, there's a vintage shop out there for you. By keeping these tips in mind and exploring the many online vintage marketplaces, you're sure to find the perfect vintage piece for your wardrobe.

A: There are many great vintage clothing marketplaces, but Asos Marketplace, Beyond Retro, Etsy, and Depop are some of the most popular.

A: Always check the measurements of the garment and compare them to your own measurements. Vintage sizing can vary greatly from modern sizing, so it's important to know your measurements.

A: Yes, vintage clothing is a sustainable option. By buying vintage, you're giving new life to old clothes and reducing the demand for new clothing production.
