Discover The Best Vintage Clothing Shops In London

Written by Petter Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

London is a fashion capital, and for fashion enthusiasts, vintage clothing is a great way to express their unique style. However, finding the best vintage clothing shops in London can be a daunting task. You want to find stores that offer quality vintage clothing without breaking the bank.

If you're looking for the best vintage clothing shops in London, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll share some of the top stores that offer high-quality vintage clothing.

Our guide will help you navigate the city's vintage fashion scene and discover the best stores for unique finds that fit your budget. Whether you're looking for a vintage dress for a special occasion or a statement piece to add to your wardrobe, we've got you covered.

Brick Lane Market

Brick Lane Market is a must-visit for vintage clothing enthusiasts. The market is open every Sunday and is home to some of the best vintage clothing shops in London. Here you'll find a wide variety of vintage clothing, accessories, and jewelry. Brick Lane Market is the perfect place to explore vintage fashion and find unique pieces at affordable prices.

One of my personal experiences at Brick Lane Market was finding an amazing vintage leather jacket for just £25. It was a great deal, and I've received numerous compliments on the jacket.

Rokit

Rokit is a popular vintage clothing store with several locations throughout London. The store has a wide selection of vintage clothing, including dresses, coats, and accessories. Rokit is known for its quality vintage clothing, and the prices are reasonable. It's a great place to find unique pieces that you won't find anywhere else.

I recently visited Rokit's Covent Garden store and found a stunning vintage cocktail dress from the 1950s. The dress was in excellent condition, and I couldn't believe the price was only £50.

Beyond Retro

Beyond Retro is a vintage clothing store with locations in Shoreditch and Soho. The store has a large selection of vintage clothing for men and women, including dresses, jackets, and accessories. Beyond Retro is known for its high-quality vintage clothing, and the prices are reasonable.

If you're looking for a unique vintage piece, Beyond Retro is the place to go. I recently visited the Shoreditch store and found a beautiful vintage beaded clutch for just £20.

How to Shop for Vintage Clothing

When shopping for vintage clothing, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, vintage clothing is often sized differently than modern clothing, so be sure to try everything on. Second, check the condition of the clothing to make sure it's in good condition. Lastly, have an idea of what you're looking for, but be open to trying on new styles and pieces.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Clothing Shops in London

London is home to some of the best vintage clothing shops in the world. Whether you're a vintage enthusiast or new to the scene, there's something for everyone. Our guide to the best vintage clothing shops in London will help you discover unique pieces at affordable prices. Happy shopping!

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best time to go to Brick Lane Market?

A: Brick Lane Market is open every Sunday, and it's best to go early in the morning to avoid the crowds.

Q: Can you negotiate prices at vintage clothing shops in London?

A: Some vintage clothing shops in London may be willing to negotiate prices, but it's not common practice. It's best to ask politely and be prepared to pay the listed price.

Q: Can you find designer vintage clothing in London?

A: Yes, there are several vintage clothing shops in London that specialize in designer vintage clothing. However, be prepared to pay higher prices for these pieces.

Q: Are there any vintage clothing shops in London that offer online shopping?

A: Yes, several vintage clothing shops in London offer online shopping, including Beyond Retro and Rokit.
