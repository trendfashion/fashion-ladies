Best Way To Make Money Selling Old Clothes A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have a closet full of clothes you never wear? Are you looking for a way to make some extra cash? Selling old clothes can be a great way to make money while decluttering your space. However, it can be overwhelming to figure out the best way to do it. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the best way to make money selling old clothes.

If you're looking to sell old clothes, you're likely feeling the pain of cluttered closets and overstuffed drawers. It can be frustrating to have clothes taking up space that you don't wear, especially when you could be making money off of them. Additionally, the process of selling these clothes can be intimidating if you're not sure where to start.

The best way to make money selling old clothes is to sell online through platforms like Poshmark, Depop, or eBay. These websites allow you to create an online store where you can easily list and sell your clothes. They also have a built-in audience of buyers who are looking for secondhand clothing.

In summary, the best way to make money selling old clothes is to sell online through platforms like Poshmark, Depop, or eBay. These websites provide an easy way to list and sell your clothes to a large audience of buyers who are interested in secondhand clothing.

Selling on Poshmark

Poshmark is a popular platform for selling secondhand clothing. To get started, create an account and start listing your items. Take clear, well-lit photos of your clothes and write detailed descriptions. Poshmark takes a 20% commission on all sales, so keep that in mind when pricing your items.

Personally, I've had great success selling on Poshmark. I've found that taking the time to create detailed listings with clear photos and descriptions helps my items sell quickly. Additionally, Poshmark has a built-in shipping system that makes it easy to ship out items once they've sold.

Selling on Depop

Depop is another popular platform for selling secondhand clothing. This app has a younger audience than Poshmark, so it may be a better option if you're selling trendier or more unique items. To get started on Depop, create an account and start listing your items. Depop takes a 10% commission on all sales.

I've also had success selling on Depop, particularly with items that have a more unique or trendy style. I've found that buyers on Depop are often looking for statement pieces that they can't find elsewhere.

Tips for Selling on Poshmark and Depop

Whether you're selling on Poshmark, Depop, or another platform, there are a few tips that can help you maximize your sales. First, take clear, well-lit photos of your items. This will help potential buyers see the details of the clothing and get a better sense of what they're purchasing.

Second, write detailed descriptions of your items. Include information about the brand, size, condition, and any notable features. This will help buyers make an informed decision about whether or not to purchase your item.

Shipping and Pricing

When it comes to shipping, make sure to factor in the cost of shipping when pricing your items. Platforms like Poshmark and Depop have built-in shipping systems, but you'll need to pay for the cost of shipping out of your earnings. Additionally, make sure to price your items competitively. Look at similar items on the platform and price your items accordingly.

Conclusion of Best Way to Make Money Selling Old Clothes

Selling old clothes can be a great way to make some extra cash and declutter your space. By using online platforms like Poshmark and Depop, you can easily list and sell your items to a large audience of buyers. Remember to take clear photos, write detailed descriptions, and price your items competitively to maximize your sales.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know what price to list my items for?

A: Look at similar items on the platform and price your items accordingly. Additionally, consider the condition and original price of the item when determining a price.

Q: What should I do if an item doesn't sell?

A: Consider lowering the price or relisting the item with new photos or a more detailed description.

Q: Can I sell items that are damaged or stained?

A: It's generally not recommended to sell items that are damaged or stained, as buyers may be less likely to purchase them.

Q: How can I increase my sales on these platforms?

A: Share your listings on social media and interact with other users on the platform to increase your visibility. Additionally, consider offering bundle discounts or promotions to entice buyers.
