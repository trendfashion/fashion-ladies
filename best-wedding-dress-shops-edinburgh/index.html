1. Ivory Whites

Located in the heart of Edinburgh, Ivory Whites is a beautiful boutique that offers a wide range of wedding dresses. Their dresses are made from high-quality materials and are designed to flatter a variety of body types. Their prices range from £1000 to £3000, making them a great option for brides on a budget. One of the best things about Ivory Whites is their exceptional customer service. They go above and beyond to make sure that their brides feel comfortable and confident in their dresses.

My personal experience with Ivory Whites was amazing. The staff were incredibly friendly and helpful, and they made me feel like a princess. They listened to my wants and needs and helped me find the perfect dress for my big day.

2. La Novia

La Novia is another great wedding dress shop in Edinburgh. They offer a wide range of dresses that are perfect for both traditional and modern brides. Their prices range from £1000 to £5000, so they cater to brides with varying budgets. One thing that sets La Novia apart from other shops is their bespoke dress service. They can work with you to create a unique dress that is tailored to your specific wants and needs.

My friend had her heart set on a specific style of dress, but couldn't find it anywhere. La Novia worked with her to create a bespoke dress that was exactly what she wanted. The end result was stunning.

The Edinburgh Wedding Collective

The Edinburgh Wedding Collective is a group of independent wedding suppliers, including wedding dress shops. They have a variety of shops that offer a range of styles and price points. Some of the shops that are part of the collective include Rachel Scott Couture, Freja Designer Dressmaking, and Kudos Bridal. The collective is a great option for brides who want to explore a variety of shops in one location.

How to Prepare for Wedding Dress Shopping

Before you start wedding dress shopping, it's important to do some preparation. Here are some tips to help you get ready:

  • Set a budget: Determine how much you are willing to spend on your dress.
  • Research styles: Look online and in magazines to get an idea of what styles you like.
  • Make appointments: Call ahead to book appointments at the shops you want to visit.
  • Bring support: Bring along a trusted friend or family member to help you make your decision.
  • Be open-minded: Try on dresses that you may not have considered before.

3. Emma Roy

Emma Roy is a well-known wedding dress shop in Edinburgh. They have been in business for over 30 years and have a wide selection of dresses to choose from. Their prices range from £800 to £3000, so they cater to a variety of budgets. Emma Roy is known for their excellent customer service and knowledgeable staff.

When I went to Emma Roy, I was blown away by their selection of dresses. They had so many options to choose from and the staff were so helpful in guiding me through the process. I ultimately found my dream dress at Emma Roy.

Question and Answer

Q: How many wedding dress shops are there in Edinburgh?

A: There are several wedding dress shops in Edinburgh, including Ivory Whites, La Novia, and Emma Roy.

Q: Do I need to make an appointment to try on dresses?

A: Yes, it's always best to call ahead and make an appointment to ensure that the shop has availability and can give you their undivided attention.

Q: How much should I budget for my wedding dress?

A: Wedding dresses can range in price from a few hundred pounds to tens of thousands of pounds. It's important to set a budget that you are comfortable with and stick to it.

Q: What should I bring with me to my appointment?

A: It's a good idea to bring along a strapless bra and shoes that are similar to what you plan on wearing on your wedding day.

Conclusion of Best Wedding Dress Shops in Edinburgh

When it comes to finding the perfect wedding dress, it's important to do your research and visit several shops before making a decision. Edinburgh has a variety of great wedding dress shops to choose from, including Ivory Whites, La Novia, and Emma Roy. By following our tips and advice, we're confident that you'll find the dress of your dreams.
