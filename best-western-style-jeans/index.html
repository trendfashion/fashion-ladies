How to Style Best Western Style Jeans

When it comes to western style jeans, it is essential to choose the right fit. Look for a pair of jeans that fit snugly around the waist and hips and taper down towards the ankles. The length of the jeans should sit just above the ankle bone.

Caring for Your Best Western Style Jeans

To keep your western style jeans looking their best, it is essential to care for them properly. Wash them in cold water and hang them to dry. Avoid using bleach or fabric softener, as these can damage the denim material. If you need to iron your jeans, use a low heat setting and iron them inside out to avoid damaging any embellishments or designs.
