Best Women s Gym Wear Brands Elevate Your Workout Style

Written by Aprilia Nov 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing uncomfortable gym clothes that don't fit right or don't look good? Do you struggle to find workout clothes that are both functional and fashionable? If so, you're not alone. Many women face the same struggle when it comes to finding the best gym wear brands.

But don't worry - we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore the top women's gym wear brands that offer both style and performance. Say goodbye to ill-fitting leggings and hello to comfortable, stylish workout gear!

When it comes to finding the best women's gym wear brands, there are several factors to consider. First and foremost, you want to look for brands that offer high-quality, durable materials that can withstand the wear and tear of frequent workouts. You also want to look for brands that offer a wide range of sizes and styles to accommodate different body types and preferences.

In addition to quality and variety, you may also want to consider factors like price, sustainability, and brand ethos when selecting your gym wear. Some women may prioritize eco-friendly materials or ethical manufacturing practices, while others may be more focused on affordability or trendy designs.

1. Lululemon

For many women, Lululemon is the gold standard when it comes to women's gym wear brands. Known for their high-quality fabrics, flattering fits, and trendy designs, Lululemon offers a wide range of workout clothes for women. From their iconic Wunder Under leggings to their stylish sports bras and jackets, Lululemon has everything you need to look and feel your best during your workouts.

Personally, I love Lululemon's Align leggings - they're incredibly soft and comfortable, and they come in a variety of colors and patterns. I also appreciate that Lululemon offers free hemming services for their pants, so you can get the perfect fit for your body.

2. Athleta

Athleta is another popular women's gym wear brand that offers both style and performance. Their clothes are made with high-quality, sustainable materials and are designed to fit a variety of body types. Athleta offers a wide range of workout clothes, including leggings, sports bras, tops, and jackets.

One of my favorite Athleta products is their Salutation leggings - they're incredibly comfortable and come in a variety of lengths and styles. I also appreciate that Athleta offers extended sizing options for women who may need larger sizes.

3. Outdoor Voices

If you're looking for a more affordable option that still offers great style and quality, consider checking out Outdoor Voices. This up-and-coming women's gym wear brand offers a range of workout clothes made with high-quality, sustainable materials. Their clothes are designed to be both functional and fashionable, with unique colors and patterns that stand out from other brands.

Personally, I love Outdoor Voices' TechSweat leggings - they're incredibly comfortable and moisture-wicking, making them perfect for sweaty workouts. I also appreciate that Outdoor Voices offers a range of sizes, including extended sizing options.

4. Girlfriend Collective

If sustainability is a top priority for you, consider checking out Girlfriend Collective. This women's gym wear brand is committed to using eco-friendly materials and ethical manufacturing practices to create their clothes. Their leggings, sports bras, and tops are all made with recycled materials and are designed to be both comfortable and stylish.

One of my favorite products from Girlfriend Collective is their High-Rise Pocket leggings - they're incredibly comfortable and come with pockets, which is always a plus in my book. I also appreciate that Girlfriend Collective offers a range of sizes and styles to accommodate different body types and preferences.

Question and Answer:

Q: What are some other factors to consider when selecting women's gym wear brands?

A: In addition to factors like quality, variety, and price, you may also want to consider things like sustainability, brand ethos, and customer reviews when selecting women's gym wear brands. It's important to find a brand that aligns with your values and offers products that meet your specific needs and preferences.

Q: Are expensive gym wear brands always better than cheaper ones?

A: Not necessarily. While some expensive gym wear brands may offer higher quality materials or more trendy designs, there are also plenty of affordable gym wear options that offer great style and performance. It's all about finding a brand that works for you and your budget.

Q: How can I find the right size for my gym wear?

A: Most women's gym wear brands offer size charts on their websites that can help you determine the best size for your body. It's also a good idea to read customer reviews to see if the clothes tend to run large or small. And if you're still unsure, don't be afraid to reach out to the brand's customer service team for assistance.

Q: Can I wear gym wear for activities other than working out?

A: Absolutely! Many women's gym wear brands offer stylish, comfortable clothes that can be worn for a variety of activities, from running errands to brunch with friends. Don't be afraid to mix and match your gym wear with other pieces in your wardrobe to create versatile outfits.

Conclusion of Best Women's Gym Wear Brands

When it comes to finding the best women's gym wear brands, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you prioritize high-quality materials, trendy designs, or eco-friendly manufacturing practices, there's a brand out there that will meet your needs. By considering factors like quality, variety, and price, you can find the perfect gym wear to elevate your workout style and help you feel confident and comfortable during your workouts.
