Emaar Properties is one of the most prominent real estate developers in the UAE and is known for developing iconic projects such as the Burj Khalifa and the Dubai Mall. The company was founded in 1997 and has since expanded its operations to several countries worldwide.

Jumeirah Group is a Dubai-based luxury hospitality company that operates several hotels and resorts across the UAE and beyond. The company was founded in 1997 and has since become one of the most recognizable brands in the luxury hospitality sector.

Lulu Group International is a Dubai-based retail conglomerate that operates several hypermarkets and supermarkets across the UAE and other countries. The company was founded in 2000 and has since become one of the fastest-growing retail chains in the region.

The Lulu hypermarket chain is a one-stop-shop for all your shopping needs, offering a wide range of products at competitive prices. I have been a regular customer of Lulu hypermarkets for several years now, and I have always been impressed with their product range, quality, and customer service.
