Discover The Best Black Owned Cheap Clothing Brands

Written by Petter Oct 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you want to support black-owned businesses and shop for affordable clothes at the same time? Finding black-owned cheap clothing brands can be a challenge, but it's worth the effort. Not only will you get stylish clothes at a fraction of the cost, but you'll also contribute to the growth of black entrepreneurship.

If you're tired of overpriced clothes and want to explore new brands, keep reading. We'll introduce you to some of the best black-owned cheap clothing brands and tell you why they're worth checking out.

Are you wondering why black-owned cheap clothing brands are important? There are several reasons. First, black-owned businesses often struggle to get funding and support in a predominantly white industry. By choosing to shop from black-owned brands, you're helping them succeed and grow. Second, cheap clothing brands can be a lifesaver for people on a tight budget. You don't have to spend a fortune to look good and feel confident.

In this article, we'll showcase some of the best black-owned cheap clothing brands and tell you what makes them unique. We'll cover everything from streetwear to sustainable fashion, so there's something for everyone.

1. Melanin Apparel

When it comes to streetwear, Melanin Apparel is a top pick. This brand specializes in graphic tees, hoodies, and sweatshirts with empowering messages for black women and men. They also sell accessories like hats and bags.

I stumbled upon Melanin Apparel on Instagram and fell in love with their designs. I ordered a hoodie and a beanie, and I was impressed with the quality and the price. The hoodie was only $35, and the beanie was $18. Plus, they offer free shipping on orders over $75.

2. Nubian Skin

If you're looking for nude lingerie and hosiery that matches your skin tone, Nubian Skin is the brand for you. They offer a variety of shades to suit different complexions, so you can find the perfect match.

I had a hard time finding nude tights that didn't make me look ashy or pale, but Nubian Skin solved that problem. Their tights are comfortable, durable, and affordable. You can get a pair for as low as $12.

3. Fanm Djanm

For bold and colorful headwraps, Fanm Djanm is a must-visit. This brand celebrates African culture and heritage through its headwraps, clothing, and accessories.

I discovered Fanm Djanm on Pinterest and was blown away by the creativity and beauty of their headwraps. I ordered a few for myself and my friends, and we got so many compliments. The headwraps are versatile and can be styled in different ways, so you can create a unique look every time.

4. Grass-Fields

Grass-Fields is a UK-based brand that offers vibrant African print clothing for women. Their designs are inspired by African culture and nature, and they use sustainable materials to reduce their environmental impact.

I stumbled upon Grass-Fields on Instagram and was impressed by the range of colors and patterns they offer. I ordered a dress and a skirt, and I was happy with the fit and the quality. The prices are reasonable, considering the uniqueness and the craftsmanship of the designs.

How to Shop for Black Owned Cheap Clothing Brands

If you want to support black-owned businesses and save money on clothes, here are some tips:

  • Research online: Use social media, blogs, and search engines to find black-owned cheap clothing brands that align with your style and values.
  • Check reviews: Read customer reviews and testimonials to get an idea of the quality, the fit, and the customer service of the brand.
  • Compare prices: Don't settle for the first brand you find. Compare prices and styles across different brands to find the best deal.
  • Sign up for newsletters: Many black-owned cheap clothing brands offer discounts and promotions for their subscribers. Sign up for their newsletters to stay updated.
  • Spread the word: Share your favorite black-owned cheap clothing brands with your friends and family. Word of mouth is a powerful tool for supporting small businesses.

Question and Answer

Q: Are black-owned cheap clothing brands only for black people?

A: No, black-owned cheap clothing brands are for everyone who wants to support black entrepreneurship and shop for affordable clothes. These brands offer a diverse range of styles and sizes, so anyone can find something they like.

Q: Are black-owned cheap clothing brands low quality?

A: No, black-owned cheap clothing brands can offer high-quality clothes at a lower price because they don't have to pay for expensive marketing or retail space. Many of these brands use sustainable materials and ethical practices to create their products.

Q: Can I return items from black-owned cheap clothing brands?

A: It depends on the brand's policy. Make sure to read their return and exchange policy before making a purchase. Some brands offer free returns or exchanges within a certain period, while others don't accept returns at all.

Q: How can I find black-owned cheap clothing brands in my area?

A: You can use online directories like Black-Owned Brooklyn or Official Black Wall Street to find black-owned businesses in your area. You can also check social media for local black-owned clothing brands.

Conclusion of black owned cheap clothing brands

Shopping for black-owned cheap clothing brands is a win-win situation. You get to support black entrepreneurship and get stylish clothes at an affordable price. Whether you're looking for streetwear, lingerie, headwraps, or African print clothing, there's a black-owned brand out there for you. Remember to do your research, compare prices, and spread the word about your favorite brands. Together, we can make a difference in the fashion industry and beyond.
