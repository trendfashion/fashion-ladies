One of the best black-owned expensive clothing brands is Pyer Moss. Founded by designer Kerby Jean-Raymond, Pyer Moss is known for its high-quality pieces and innovative designs. Another black-owned expensive clothing brand is Telfar. Founded by Telfar Clemens, the brand is known for its iconic Telfar Shopping Bag, which has become a staple in the fashion industry.
