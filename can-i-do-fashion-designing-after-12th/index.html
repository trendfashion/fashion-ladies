Can I Do Fashion Designing After 12Th

Written by Petter Sep 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a creative person who loves fashion and designing? Are you wondering if you can pursue fashion designing after completing your 12th grade? If yes, then this article is for you!

Many students are interested in fashion designing, but they are not sure if it is the right career path for them. Some may worry about the future job prospects, while others may be concerned about the cost of education. However, if you have a passion for fashion and the determination to succeed, then there are plenty of opportunities available for you to pursue a career in fashion designing.

The answer is yes! You can definitely do fashion designing after completing your 12th grade. Fashion designing is a popular career choice for many students after their 12th standard. In fact, many fashion design institutes offer undergraduate courses in fashion designing that you can enroll in after completing your 12th grade.

If you want to pursue fashion designing after your 12th grade, then you need to start by choosing the right course and institute. There are several fashion design courses available, such as Bachelor of Design (B.Des), Bachelor of Fashion Design (BFD), and Bachelor of Fashion Technology (BFTech). You can choose the course that best suits your interests and career goals.

To get admission in a fashion design institute, you need to appear for an entrance exam, which tests your creativity, drawing skills, and general knowledge about the fashion industry. Once you clear the exam, you can enroll in the course of your choice and start your journey towards becoming a fashion designer.

My Experience with Fashion Designing After 12th

When I completed my 12th grade, I was confused about what career path to choose. I was interested in fashion designing, but I wasn't sure if it was the right choice for me. However, I decided to take the entrance exam for a fashion design institute and see how it goes.

To my surprise, I cleared the exam and got admission in one of the top fashion design institutes in the country. The course was challenging, but it was also exciting and fulfilling. I learned about different aspects of fashion designing, such as sketching, pattern-making, textile design, and garment construction.

After completing my course, I got a job in a leading fashion brand and started my career as a fashion designer. It has been a few years since then, and I have never regretted my decision to pursue fashion designing after my 12th grade. It is a dynamic and rewarding career that allows me to express my creativity and make a difference in the fashion industry.

Job Opportunities in Fashion Designing After 12th

There are plenty of job opportunities available for fashion designers after completing their 12th grade. Some of the popular career options include:

Fashion Designer

As a fashion designer, you will be responsible for creating designs for clothing, accessories, and footwear. You will need to have a good understanding of fashion trends, fabrics, and colors to create designs that appeal to the target audience.

Fashion Stylist

As a fashion stylist, you will be responsible for creating looks for clients, such as celebrities, models, or individuals. You will need to have a good understanding of fashion trends, body types, and personal styles to create looks that suit the client's needs and preferences.

Fashion Merchandiser

As a fashion merchandiser, you will be responsible for managing the buying and selling of fashion products. You will need to have a good understanding of market trends, consumer behavior, and product development to make informed decisions about what products to buy and sell.

Fashion Illustrator

As a fashion illustrator, you will be responsible for creating sketches and illustrations of fashion designs. You will need to have a good understanding of fashion trends, drawing skills, and artistic techniques to create visually appealing illustrations.

Conclusion of Can I do Fashion Designing After 12th

In conclusion, if you have a passion for fashion and the determination to succeed, then you can definitely pursue fashion designing after completing your 12th grade. With the right course and institute, you can learn the skills and knowledge required to become a successful fashion designer. So, go ahead and follow your dreams!

Question and Answer

Q. What is the eligibility criteria for pursuing fashion designing after 12th?

A. To pursue fashion designing after 12th, you need to have completed your 12th grade from a recognized board. You also need to appear for an entrance exam conducted by fashion design institutes.

Q. What are the skills required to become a successful fashion designer?

A. To become a successful fashion designer, you need to have a good understanding of fashion trends, fabrics, colors, and patterns. You also need to have good communication skills, creativity, and drawing skills.

Q. What is the salary of a fashion designer?

A. The salary of a fashion designer varies depending on their experience, skills, and job profile. However, on average, a fashion designer can earn between Rs. 3 lakhs to Rs. 10 lakhs per annum.

Q. What are the job prospects for fashion designers?

A. Fashion designing is a dynamic and growing industry, with plenty of job opportunities available for talented and skilled professionals. Some of the popular career options include fashion designer, fashion stylist, fashion merchandiser, and fashion illustrator.
