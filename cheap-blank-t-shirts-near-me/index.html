Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if the t-shirt is good quality?

A: Look for t-shirts made from 100% cotton or a cotton blend with a high thread count (at least 4.5 oz). Check the stitching, seams, and hems for durability and neatness. Feel the fabric for softness and thickness.

Q: Can I customize my blank t-shirt?

A: Yes, you can customize your blank t-shirt by printing or embroidering your design, logo, or message on it. Many local print shops or online printing services offer custom printing for blank t-shirts at affordable prices.

Q: How many t-shirts should I buy?

A: It depends on your needs and budget. If you need t-shirts for personal use, you can buy as many as you want. If you need t-shirts for business or events, consider the number of people you need to outfit and the frequency of use.

Q: How can I make my blank t-shirt last longer?

A: To make your blank t-shirt last longer, wash it in cold water, use a gentle detergent, and hang it to dry. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the fabric. Store your t-shirt in a dry and cool place.
