Skateboarding has been around since the 1950s, and it has come a long way since then. Over the years, many classic skateboard brands have emerged, shaping the sport as we know it today. Powell Peralta is one of the most iconic brands in skateboarding history. Founded in 1978 by George Powell and Stacy Peralta, the brand quickly became known for its innovative designs and high-quality products. Powell Peralta is best known for its Bones Brigade skate team, which included legendary skaters like Tony Hawk and Steve Caballero. Today, Powell Peralta continues to produce some of the best skateboards, wheels, and accessories on the market. Santa Cruz Skateboards is another classic brand that has been around since the 1970s. Founded by Richard Novak, Doug Haut, and Jay Shuirman, Santa Cruz quickly became known for its iconic Screaming Hand logo and punk-inspired designs. Today, Santa Cruz continues to produce high-quality skateboards, apparel, and accessories that are beloved by skaters around the world. Vision Skateboards is a brand that was popular in the 1980s and has recently made a comeback. Founded in 1976, Vision was known for its colorful graphics and innovative designs.
