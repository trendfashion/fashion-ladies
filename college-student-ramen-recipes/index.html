College Student Ramen Recipes A Guide To Quick And Easy Meals

Written by Luffy Oct 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a college student on a tight budget, struggling to find cheap and easy meals to make? Ramen noodles may be your go-to, but eating them every day can get boring quickly. Fear not, as we have compiled a list of delicious college student ramen recipes that will spice up your meals without breaking the bank.

As a college student, you may find yourself with limited time, money, and cooking skills. That's why we have created this guide to help you make tasty and cheap meals that are simple to prepare. With these college student ramen recipes, you will be able to impress your friends and family with your culinary skills.

In this article, we will cover a range of college student ramen recipes that are easy to make, require minimal cooking skills, and are budget-friendly. We will also provide tips on how to elevate your ramen noodles to the next level.

Spicy Peanut Butter Ramen

One of our favorite college student ramen recipes is the Spicy Peanut Butter Ramen. This recipe is perfect for those who love a bit of heat and are looking for a more substantial meal.

To make this recipe, start by cooking your ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a separate pan, sauté some garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add in some chopped veggies of your choice and cook until tender. In a small bowl, mix together some peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, and sriracha. Add the sauce to the veggies and mix well. Finally, add in the cooked ramen noodles and stir until everything is well combined. Garnish with some chopped peanuts and green onions.

Chicken and Veggie Ramen Stir Fry

If you're looking for a more substantial meal, try out our Chicken and Veggie Ramen Stir Fry. This recipe is perfect for those who want to add some protein and veggies to their ramen noodles.

To make this recipe, start by cooking your ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a separate pan, cook some diced chicken until browned. Add in some chopped veggies of your choice and cook until tender. In a small bowl, mix together some soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger. Add the sauce to the chicken and veggies and mix well. Finally, add in the cooked ramen noodles and stir until everything is well combined. Garnish with some chopped green onions.

How to Elevate Your Ramen Noodles

If you're getting tired of plain ramen noodles, here are some tips to elevate your meals:

  • Add some protein: Try adding some cooked chicken, beef, or tofu to your ramen noodles to make them more filling.
  • Add some veggies: Chop up some veggies of your choice and add them to your ramen noodles for a healthier meal.
  • Spice it up: Add some sriracha, hot sauce, or chili flakes to your ramen noodles for a bit of heat.
  • Get creative: Experiment with different sauces and toppings to find your perfect ramen noodle recipe.

How to Cook Ramen Noodles

Cooking ramen noodles is easy. Simply bring a pot of water to boil, add the noodles, and cook for 3-4 minutes or until tender. Drain the noodles and add them to your recipe.

Curry Ramen Noodles

Another one of our favorite college student ramen recipes is the Curry Ramen Noodles. This recipe is perfect for those who love a bit of spice and want a more flavorful meal.

To make this recipe, start by cooking your ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a separate pan, sauté some garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add in some chopped veggies of your choice and cook until tender. In a small bowl, mix together some curry powder, coconut milk, soy sauce, and honey. Add the sauce to the veggies and mix well. Finally, add in the cooked ramen noodles and stir until everything is well combined. Garnish with some chopped cilantro.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use any type of ramen noodles for these recipes?
A: Yes, you can use any type of ramen noodles for these recipes. Just be sure to cook them according to the package instructions.

Q: Can I make these recipes ahead of time?
A: Yes, you can make these recipes ahead of time and store them in the fridge for up to 3 days. Just be sure to reheat them before serving.

Q: Can I add different veggies to these recipes?
A: Yes, you can add any veggies you like to these recipes. Just be sure to chop them up small enough so they cook evenly.

Q: Are these recipes spicy?
A: Some of these recipes are spicy, but you can adjust the heat level to your liking.

Conclusion of College Student Ramen Recipes

With these college student ramen recipes, you can enjoy delicious and budget-friendly meals without sacrificing flavor. Whether you're a beginner cook or an experienced chef, these recipes are easy to make and require minimal cooking skills. So, next time you're craving a quick and easy meal, try one of these tasty college student ramen recipes.
