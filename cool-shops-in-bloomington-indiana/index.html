Cool Shops In Bloomington Indiana A Guide To The Best Retail Therapy Spots

Written by William Oct 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you in need of some retail therapy, but tired of the same old stores? Look no further than Bloomington, Indiana! This college town has a variety of unique and trendy shops to satisfy any shopping craving.

Whether you're searching for the perfect vintage find or wanting to support local businesses, Bloomington has something for everyone. With so many options, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

Here's a guide to some of the best cool shops in Bloomington Indiana:

The Back Door

The Back Door is a vintage store located in the heart of downtown Bloomington. With a curated selection of clothing, accessories, and home goods from the 60s to the 90s, this store is a treasure trove for vintage lovers. The prices are affordable and the staff is friendly, making it a must-visit for anyone looking for a unique piece to add to their wardrobe.

Gather Handmade Shoppe and Co.

If you're looking for one-of-a-kind, handmade items, look no further than Gather Handmade Shoppe and Co. This store features local artists and makers, offering everything from jewelry to ceramics to candles. Not only will you leave with a unique purchase, but you'll also be supporting the local community.

The Vintage Phoenix

The Vintage Phoenix is a consignment shop that offers both vintage and contemporary clothing for men and women. With a constantly changing inventory, you never know what you'll find. Plus, the prices are unbeatable, making it a great spot for budget-conscious shoppers.

The Green Nursery

The Green Nursery is a family-owned business that specializes in eco-friendly baby products. From cloth diapers to organic clothing, this store has everything you need to raise your little one in an environmentally conscious way. The staff is knowledgeable and helpful, making it a great resource for new parents.

The Question and Answer Section

Q: What is the best time to visit these cool shops in Bloomington Indiana?

A: The best time to visit these cool shops in Bloomington Indiana is during the weekend, when most of the stores are open and bustling with activity.

Q: Are these shops suitable for kids?

A: Some of the shops, such as The Green Nursery, are geared towards parents and children. However, others may not have much to offer for young children.

Q: Are these shops accessible for people with disabilities?

A: Most of these shops are located on the ground floor and are wheelchair accessible. However, it's always a good idea to call ahead to confirm.

Q: Do these shops offer online shopping?

A: Some of the shops, such as Gather Handmade Shoppe and Co., offer online shopping. However, it's always best to check the individual store's website or social media pages for more information.

Conclusion of Cool Shops in Bloomington Indiana

Bloomington, Indiana may be a small town, but it's big on unique and trendy shopping options. From vintage finds to handmade goods, there's something for everyone. So next time you're in need of some retail therapy, head to Bloomington and explore these cool shops!
