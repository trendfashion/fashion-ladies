Columbus, Ohio is a hidden gem for cool shops that offer unique items not found in big box stores. From vintage clothing to handcrafted jewelry, there's something for everyone in this vibrant city. If you're looking for a shopping experience that's out of the ordinary, you've come to the right place.

Shopping can be a stressful experience, especially if you're not sure where to go. It can be overwhelming to navigate a new city and find shops that fit your style and budget. That's where this guide comes in - we'll give you the inside scoop on the coolest shops in Columbus, Ohio.

Are you looking for a unique shopping experience that's off the beaten path? Do you want to support local businesses and artists? If so, this guide is for you. We'll give you a rundown of the best shops in Columbus, Ohio that offer one-of-a-kind items you won't find anywhere else.

If you're looking for a shopping experience that's out of the ordinary, Columbus, Ohio has plenty of options. From vintage clothing shops to record stores, there's something for everyone. The city has a thriving arts scene, which means you'll find plenty of handmade and unique items. Some of the best shops in the city include:

Clothing Stores

One of the best things about Columbus is its thriving vintage clothing scene. Some of the best shops in the city for vintage clothing include:

The Attic - This shop has a wide variety of vintage clothing and accessories for both men and women. You'll find everything from vintage band t-shirts to 90s grunge-inspired dresses.

Flower Child - This shop specializes in vintage clothing from the 60s and 70s. If you're looking for a retro outfit that will make you stand out, this is the place to go.

Record Stores

If you're a music lover, you'll want to check out some of the record stores in Columbus. Some of the best include:

Lost Weekend Records - This shop has a great selection of new and used vinyl, CDs, and cassettes. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly, and they love to help customers find new music.

Used Kids Records - This shop has been a Columbus institution since the 80s. They have a wide variety of new and used records, and they're always buying collections.

Artisanal Goods

If you're looking for handmade and unique items, Columbus has plenty of options. Some of the best shops for artisanal goods include:

Wholly Craft - This shop specializes in handmade items from local artists. You'll find everything from handcrafted jewelry to screen-printed t-shirts.

Stump - This shop has a wide variety of handmade home goods, including pottery, candles, and textiles. They also offer workshops where you can learn to make your own crafts.

How to Navigate the Shops

When you're exploring the shops in Columbus, it's important to have a plan. Start by doing some research online to find shops that fit your style and budget. Make a list of the shops you want to visit, and plan your route ahead of time. Be sure to wear comfortable shoes, as you'll be doing a lot of walking. And don't be afraid to ask the staff for recommendations - they're experts in their fields and love to help customers find new treasures.

Conclusion

Shopping in Columbus, Ohio is a unique experience that you won't find anywhere else. Whether you're looking for vintage clothing, handmade jewelry, or new and used records, there's something for everyone in this vibrant city. Plan your route ahead of time, wear comfortable shoes, and be prepared to discover some hidden gems.

A: Columbus is a great city to visit any time of year, but if you're looking for a unique shopping experience, be sure to check out the Columbus Flea, which takes place several times a year and features local vendors and artisans.
