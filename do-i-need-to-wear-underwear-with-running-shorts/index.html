Do I Need To Wear Underwear With Running Shorts

Written by Aprilia Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Running is a great way to stay in shape and improve your overall health. However, for many people, the question of whether or not to wear underwear with running shorts can be a source of confusion and even discomfort. If you're unsure about whether or not to wear underwear with your running shorts, this post is for you. We'll discuss the pros and cons of wearing underwear, as well as some tips for staying comfortable while you run.

Many people find that wearing underwear with running shorts can cause chafing and discomfort, especially during longer runs. Additionally, some types of underwear can ride up and cause irritation. However, going without underwear can also be uncomfortable, as it can lead to excessive sweating and rubbing against the skin. Ultimately, the decision of whether or not to wear underwear with running shorts is a personal one that depends on your individual comfort level.

If you're considering going without underwear, it's important to choose shorts that are specially designed for running. Look for shorts that are made from lightweight, breathable materials like mesh or moisture-wicking fabric. You may also want to consider purchasing shorts with built-in liners, which can provide additional support and comfort.

My Personal Experience

Personally, I prefer to wear running shorts without underwear. I find that the built-in liners provide enough support and prevent any chafing or irritation. However, I know that this isn't the case for everyone, and some people may prefer to wear underwear for added comfort and support.

Pros and Cons of Wearing Underwear with Running Shorts

As mentioned earlier, there are both pros and cons to wearing underwear with running shorts. Some of the main advantages of wearing underwear include added support and protection against chafing and irritation. However, underwear can also be uncomfortable and restrictive, especially during longer runs when sweat and moisture can build up.

On the other hand, going without underwear can provide a more comfortable and free feeling, but it can also lead to irritation and chafing. It's important to weigh the pros and cons and decide what works best for you.

Tips for Staying Comfortable While Running

Regardless of whether or not you choose to wear underwear with your running shorts, there are some tips you can follow to stay comfortable and prevent chafing and irritation. These include:

  • Choosing shorts with built-in liners or made from breathable materials
  • Applying anti-chafing cream or petroleum jelly to areas prone to chafing
  • Wearing moisture-wicking socks to prevent blisters
  • Drying off thoroughly after a run to prevent moisture buildup

How to Choose the Right Running Shorts

When choosing running shorts, it's important to consider factors like fit, breathability, and support. Look for shorts that are snug but not too tight, and made from lightweight, moisture-wicking materials. Consider shorts with built-in liners for added support, and avoid shorts with rough seams or tags that can cause irritation.

My Final Thoughts

Ultimately, the decision of whether or not to wear underwear with running shorts is a personal one that depends on your individual comfort level. There are pros and cons to both options, so it's important to weigh the factors and decide what works best for you. Just remember to choose shorts that are designed for running, and take steps to prevent chafing and irritation.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear regular underwear with my running shorts?

A: It's generally not recommended to wear regular underwear with running shorts, as it can cause chafing and irritation. Instead, look for shorts with built-in liners or made from breathable materials.

Q: What kind of shorts are best for running?

A: Look for shorts that are made from lightweight, moisture-wicking materials and have built-in liners for added support. Avoid shorts with rough seams or tags that can cause irritation.

Q: How can I prevent chafing while running?

A: Apply anti-chafing cream or petroleum jelly to areas prone to chafing, and choose shorts with built-in liners or made from breathable materials.

Q: Should I wear underwear with compression shorts?

A: Compression shorts are designed to provide support and reduce muscle fatigue, so it's generally not necessary to wear underwear with them. However, it's important to choose high-quality compression shorts that fit properly and are made from breathable materials.

Conclusion of Do I Need to Wear Underwear with Running Shorts

Ultimately, the decision of whether or not to wear underwear with running shorts is a personal one that depends on your individual comfort level. There are pros and cons to both options, so it's important to weigh the factors and decide what works best for you. Just remember to choose shorts that are designed for running, and take steps to prevent chafing and irritation.
