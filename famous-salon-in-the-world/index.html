John Barrett Salon, New York

Another famous salon in the world is the John Barrett Salon in New York. This chic, modern salon is located in the heart of Manhattan and is known for its personalized approach to beauty. The salon's founder, John Barrett, is a celebrity stylist who has worked with everyone from Sarah Jessica Parker to Kate Middleton.
