Kim Jones

Kim Jones is a British fashion designer who has been the artistic director of Dior Men since 2018. He was born in London in 1979 and studied at Central Saint Martins before launching his own menswear brand in 2003. Jones is known for his fusion of streetwear and luxury fashion, as well as his collaborations with artists and designers.

Jones has worked with several South Korean brands, including Fila and Gentle Monster. He has also collaborated with K-pop group BTS, designing the costumes for their Love Yourself World Tour. Jones' work with BTS helped bring attention to South Korean fashion and its unique style.

Juun.J

Juun.J is a South Korean fashion designer who launched his eponymous brand in 2007. He is known for his oversized silhouettes and deconstructed designs, which often feature military-inspired elements. Juun.J has shown his collections at Paris Fashion Week since 2009 and has been praised for his innovative designs.

His designs have been worn by celebrities such as G-Dragon and Rihanna, and his brand has collaborated with several international brands, including Converse and Samsung. Juun.J's signature style has helped define the Korean fashion scene and has influenced designers around the world.

Chung Chung Lee

Chung Chung Lee is a South Korean fashion designer who launched his brand, The Sirius, in 2004. He is known for his minimalist designs, which often feature monochromatic colors and clean lines. Lee has shown his collections at Seoul Fashion Week and has been recognized for his innovative use of fabrics and textures.

Lee's designs have been worn by celebrities such as CL and Sandara Park, and his brand has collaborated with several international brands, including Reebok and Superga. Lee's minimalist style has helped set him apart in the Korean fashion scene and has earned him a loyal following.
