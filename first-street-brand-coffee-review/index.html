How to Brew First Street Brand Coffee

The perfect cup of coffee depends on the brewing method. For First Street Brand Coffee, the recommended brewing method is the drip method. Here's how to brew the coffee:

  1. Measure out the appropriate amount of coffee for your desired strength.
  2. Grind the coffee beans to your desired consistency.
  3. Place the coffee grounds in a coffee filter.
  4. Boil water and pour it over the coffee grounds.
  5. Allow the coffee to brew for 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Remove the filter and serve the coffee.
