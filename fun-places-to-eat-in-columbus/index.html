Fun Places to Eat in Columbus #1: North Market

North Market is a must-visit spot for any food lover in Columbus. This indoor market features over 30 local vendors, each serving up their own unique cuisine. From sushi to barbecue to crepes, there is something for everyone at North Market. Plus, the atmosphere is lively and fun, making it the perfect spot for a casual lunch or dinner.

Fun Places to Eat in Columbus #2: Dirty Frank's Hot Dog Palace

If you are looking for a unique dining experience, head to Dirty Frank's Hot Dog Palace. This casual spot serves up some of the best hot dogs in town, with options ranging from classic to creative. Plus, the retro decor and laid-back vibe make it a fun spot to hang out with friends.

The Best Neighborhoods for Foodies in Columbus

If you are looking for a neighborhood with great food options, there are a few areas in Columbus that stand out. The Short North Arts District is known for its trendy restaurants and bars, while German Village is home to some of the city's best fine dining spots. If you are on a budget, head to the University District for cheap eats and student favorites.
