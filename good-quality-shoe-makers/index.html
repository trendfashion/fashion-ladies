How to Care for Your Good Quality Shoes

Once you've invested in a good quality pair of shoes, it's important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your good quality shoes:

- Use a shoe horn when putting on your shoes to avoid damaging the heel counter.

- Use a shoe tree to help your shoes keep their shape and prevent creasing.

- Clean your shoes regularly with a soft cloth and leather cleaner.

- Apply a leather conditioner to keep the leather soft and supple.
