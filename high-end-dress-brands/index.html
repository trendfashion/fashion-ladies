High End Dress Brands A Guide To The Best In The Market

Written by Petter Oct 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a fashion lover, finding the perfect dress for a special occasion can be a challenge. High end dress brands promise quality and style, but often come at a steep price. In this guide, we'll explore the best high end dress brands in the market and help you find the perfect dress to make a statement.

When it comes to high end dress brands, the target audience is women who value quality, design, and exclusivity. These brands are known for their attention to detail, luxurious fabrics, and impeccable craftsmanship. While the price point can be a deterrent, those who invest in high end dresses often see it as a long-term investment in their wardrobe.

In this guide, we'll take a closer look at some of the top high end dress brands in the market, including their signature styles, pricing, and where to shop for them. We'll also provide tips on how to choose the perfect dress for your body type and occasion.

1. Oscar de la Renta

My personal experience with Oscar de la Renta was unforgettable. The dress I wore to my sister's wedding was elegant and timeless. The brand is known for its feminine silhouettes, intricate embroidery, and luxurious fabrics. Their dresses are perfect for formal events, weddings, and galas. Prices range from $2,000 to $10,000, and can be found at high end department stores like Neiman Marcus and Saks Fifth Avenue.

2. Marchesa

If you're looking for a dress that's more dramatic and whimsical, Marchesa is the brand for you. Their signature style includes layers of tulle, intricate beading, and floral appliques. Their dresses are perfect for red carpet events and black tie affairs. Prices range from $2,000 to $20,000 and can be found at high end department stores like Bergdorf Goodman and Net-a-Porter.

The Importance of Quality

One of the reasons high end dress brands are so popular is because of their commitment to quality. When you invest in a high end dress, you're not just paying for the design, you're paying for the quality of the fabric, the attention to detail, and the craftsmanship. This means that your dress will not only look better, but it will also last longer. High end dresses are often made from luxurious fabrics like silk, lace, and chiffon, which are not only beautiful but also durable.

How to Choose the Perfect High End Dress

Choosing the perfect high end dress can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. When selecting a dress, it's important to consider your body type, the occasion, and your personal style. If you're petite, look for dresses with a shorter hemline to elongate your figure. If you're curvy, look for dresses that cinch at the waist to accentuate your curves. When it comes to occasion, consider the dress code and the venue. For example, a black tie event calls for a formal gown, while a cocktail party may require a shorter, more playful dress.

3. Carolina Herrera

Carolina Herrera is known for her elegant and sophisticated designs. Her dresses are perfect for the modern woman who wants to make a statement without being too flashy. Her signature style includes clean lines, bold colors, and timeless silhouettes. Prices range from $1,500 to $10,000 and can be found at high end department stores like Neiman Marcus and Bergdorf Goodman.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high end dresses worth the investment?
A: Yes, investing in a high end dress is worth it if you're looking for quality, design, and exclusivity. High end dresses are often made from luxurious fabrics and impeccable craftsmanship, which means they will last longer and look better than cheaper alternatives.

Q: Where can I buy high end dresses?
A: You can buy high end dresses at high end department stores like Neiman Marcus, Saks Fifth Avenue, and Bergdorf Goodman. You can also shop online at luxury retailers like Net-a-Porter and Moda Operandi.

Q: How do I care for my high end dress?
A: High end dresses require special care to maintain their quality. Always follow the care instructions on the label, and consider taking your dress to a professional cleaner. Store your dress in a cool, dry place, and avoid hanging it for long periods of time to prevent stretching or misshaping.

Q: Can I rent high end dresses?
A: Yes, there are several online rental services that offer high end dresses for rent. This is a great option if you want to wear a designer dress for a special occasion without the high price tag.

Conclusion of High End Dress Brands

High end dress brands offer quality, design, and exclusivity for those who value fashion. Investing in a high end dress can be a long-term investment in your wardrobe, and can make you feel confident and empowered. With this guide, we hope you've gained a better understanding of the top high end dress brands in the market and how to choose the perfect dress for your occasion and body type.
