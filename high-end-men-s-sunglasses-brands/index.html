High End Men's Sunglasses Brands: The Ultimate Guide

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of sunglasses, high end men's sunglasses brands can provide both style and function. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right brand for your needs.

If you're in the market for high end men's sunglasses, chances are you're looking for a combination of quality, durability, and style. You may also be concerned with UV protection and lens quality, which are important factors to consider when shopping for sunglasses.

In this article, we'll explore some of the top high end men's sunglasses brands, their target audience, and what makes them stand out in the crowded sunglasses market.

Ray-Ban

Ray-Ban is one of the most well-known and respected high end men's sunglasses brands on the market today. Their target audience is anyone looking for classic, timeless style with modern function. Ray-Ban sunglasses are known for their iconic aviator and wayfarer styles, which have been worn by everyone from Tom Cruise in Top Gun to Audrey Hepburn in Breakfast at Tiffany's. In addition to their classic styles, Ray-Ban also offers a range of modern designs, such as the Justin and the Clubmaster.

Personally, I own a pair of Ray-Ban aviators and they are my go-to sunglasses for any occasion. They provide excellent UV protection and the polarized lenses reduce glare, making them perfect for driving or outdoor activities.

Maui Jim

Maui Jim is a high end men's sunglasses brand that focuses on high-quality lenses and advanced technology. Their target audience is anyone who wants the best possible lens quality and clarity. Maui Jim sunglasses are known for their polarized lenses, which provide superior glare reduction and color enhancement. They also offer a range of lens colors and coatings, including their patented MauiGradient and PolarizedPlus2 technologies.

As someone who spends a lot of time on the water, I can attest to the quality of Maui Jim sunglasses. The polarized lenses provide excellent glare reduction and the colors are incredibly vibrant and true to life.

Oakley

Oakley is a high end men's sunglasses brand that focuses on performance and innovation. Their target audience is anyone who wants the best possible technology and functionality in their sunglasses. Oakley sunglasses are known for their advanced lens coatings, such as Prizm and HDO, which provide excellent clarity and color enhancement. They also offer a range of frame styles, including sport-specific designs for activities such as cycling, running, and golf.

Personally, I own a pair of Oakley Flak 2.0 sunglasses and they are incredibly comfortable and functional. The Prizm lenses provide excellent color enhancement and the frame design is perfect for outdoor activities.

Tom Ford

Tom Ford is a high end men's sunglasses brand that focuses on luxury and fashion. Their target audience is anyone looking for high-end, designer sunglasses that make a bold statement. Tom Ford sunglasses are known for their unique frame designs and high-quality materials, such as Italian acetate and titanium. They also offer a range of lens colors and coatings, including polarized options.

While I don't own a pair of Tom Ford sunglasses myself, I have tried them on and they are undeniably stylish and well-made. If you're looking for a pair of sunglasses to make a fashion statement, Tom Ford is definitely a brand to consider.

Question & Answer

Q: Are high end men's sunglasses worth the investment?

A: Yes, high end men's sunglasses are worth the investment if you want quality, durability, and style. They also typically offer better lens quality and UV protection than cheaper sunglasses.

Q: What should I look for in high end men's sunglasses?

A: When shopping for high end men's sunglasses, look for quality materials, advanced lens technology, and a style that suits your personal taste and needs.

Q: How can I tell if a pair of sunglasses offers good UV protection?

A: Look for sunglasses that offer 100% UV protection or UV400 protection. You can also check the label or packaging for information on UV protection.

Q: Can I wear high end men's sunglasses for sports or outdoor activities?

A: Yes, many high end men's sunglasses brands offer sport-specific designs that are perfect for outdoor activities. Look for features such as polarized lenses, lightweight frames, and rubber nose pads for added comfort and functionality.

Conclusion of High End Men's Sunglasses Brands

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of sunglasses, high end men's sunglasses brands offer a combination of style, function, and quality. Whether you're looking for classic aviators or sport-specific designs, there's a brand out there that will meet your needs. By considering factors such as UV protection, lens quality, and frame design, you can find a pair of sunglasses that will not only look great, but also provide superior functionality and protection for your eyes.
