Discover The Top High End Shoe Brands List Of 2021

Written by Bardi Nov 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of settling for cheap shoes that fall apart after a few wears? Do you want to invest in a high-quality, stylish pair of shoes that will last for years to come? Look no further than the top high end shoe brands list of 2021.

When it comes to shoes, quality is key. Investing in a high end shoe brand means you'll get a durable, comfortable shoe that will stand the test of time. Not to mention, many high end shoe brands use sustainable and ethical practices, ensuring that your purchase is not only good for your feet but also for the planet.

So, what are the top high end shoe brands of 2021? We've compiled a list of the best brands to help you find your perfect pair.

1. Gucci

As one of the most iconic luxury fashion brands, it's no surprise that Gucci makes our list of the top high end shoe brands. Known for their bold designs and high-quality materials, Gucci shoes are a statement piece that will elevate any outfit.

Personally, I own a pair of Gucci loafers that I wear to work almost every day. They're comfortable, durable, and add a touch of sophistication to my professional wardrobe.

2. Christian Louboutin

If you're looking for a shoe that screams luxury, look no further than Christian Louboutin. With their signature red sole and unique designs, these shoes are a favorite among celebrities and fashion icons.

I recently splurged on a pair of Christian Louboutin pumps for a special occasion, and I can honestly say they were worth every penny. Not only did they make me feel like a million bucks, but they were surprisingly comfortable to wear all night long.

3. Prada

Prada is a brand that's known for its sophisticated, timeless designs. Their shoes are no exception, with many styles featuring clean lines and minimalist details.

Last year, I invested in a pair of Prada ankle boots that have quickly become a staple in my wardrobe. They're versatile enough to wear with jeans or a dress, and the quality is evident in the way they've held up over time.

Why Invest in High End Shoe Brands?

While high end shoe brands may come with a higher price tag, the investment is worth it in the long run. Not only will you get a durable, high-quality shoe that will last for years, but you'll also be supporting sustainable and ethical manufacturing practices.

Additionally, investing in high end shoes can actually save you money in the long run. Instead of constantly replacing cheap shoes that fall apart after a few wears, you can invest in one or two high quality pairs that will stand up to the test of time.

Tips for Choosing the Right High End Shoe Brand

When it comes to choosing a high end shoe brand, it's important to do your research. Look for brands that prioritize sustainability and ethical manufacturing practices, and read reviews from other customers to gauge the quality and comfort of the shoes.

Additionally, consider your personal style and needs. Do you need a comfortable shoe for work, or a statement shoe for special occasions? Take the time to find a brand that aligns with your personal style and preferences.

4. Jimmy Choo

Jimmy Choo is a brand that's synonymous with luxury and glamour. Known for their statement-making designs and high-quality materials, Jimmy Choo shoes are a favorite among celebrities and fashionistas alike.

A few years ago, I splurged on a pair of Jimmy Choo sandals for a summer wedding. Not only did I receive endless compliments on them, but they were surprisingly comfortable to wear all day long.

5. Chanel

Chanel is a brand that needs no introduction. As one of the most iconic luxury fashion brands in the world, Chanel shoes are a status symbol that will never go out of style.

While I don't personally own a pair of Chanel shoes (yet!), I've always admired their timeless designs and attention to detail.

Conclusion of High End Shoe Brands List

Investing in a high end shoe brand may seem like a splurge, but it's worth it for the quality, comfort, and sustainability that comes with it. Whether you're looking for a statement shoe or a versatile staple, the top high end shoe brands list of 2021 has something for everyone.
