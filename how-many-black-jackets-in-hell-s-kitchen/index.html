Are you a fan of the popular cooking show Hell's Kitchen? If so, you may be wondering how many black jackets are given out in the show each season. If you're not familiar with the show, a black jacket is a coveted prize awarded to the best chefs in the competition. Keep reading to find out more about how many black jackets are given out in Hell's Kitchen.

The competition in Hell's Kitchen is tough, and the chefs are put through a series of challenges to test their culinary skills. The ultimate goal is to win a black jacket, which signifies that the chef has made it to the top tier of the competition. However, not all chefs are awarded a black jacket, and the process of elimination can be grueling.

Each season of Hell's Kitchen typically has around 16 contestants, and only a handful of them will make it to the coveted black jacket round. The number of black jackets given out each season can vary, but it usually ranges from 5 to 8.
