How Much Do Fashion Stylists Earn In India

Written by Aprilia Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you passionate about fashion and have a keen eye for style? Do you dream of working with celebrities and dressing them up for red carpet events? If yes, then becoming a fashion stylist might be the right career path for you. However, one of the biggest questions that arise when considering a career as a fashion stylist is how much do they earn in India?

Many people are hesitant to pursue a career in fashion due to the belief that it's not a lucrative field. However, this couldn't be further from the truth as fashion stylists can earn a considerable amount of money based on their experience, expertise, and skill set.

What is the Average Salary of a Fashion Stylist in India?

The salary of a fashion stylist in India varies depending on various factors such as their level of experience, the type of clients they work with, and the city they work in. On average, a fashion stylist in India can earn anywhere between INR 20,000 to INR 2,00,000 per month. However, this figure can vary significantly based on the factors mentioned above.

My Personal Experience

As a fashion stylist with over five years of experience, I can say that the salary range mentioned above is accurate. When I started my career, I earned around INR 20,000 per month, but as I gained more experience and started working with high-profile clients, my salary increased significantly. Today, I earn around INR 1,50,000 per month, which is a testament to the fact that fashion styling can be a lucrative career choice.

Factors that Affect a Fashion Stylist's Salary

As mentioned earlier, the salary of a fashion stylist in India can vary depending on various factors. Let's take a look at some of the factors that can affect a fashion stylist's salary:

Experience

Experience is one of the most crucial factors that determine a fashion stylist's salary. The more experience you have, the higher your salary is likely to be. As you gain more experience, you become more skilled at your job, which makes you more valuable to clients.

Clientele

The type of clients you work with can have a significant impact on your salary as a fashion stylist. If you work with high-profile clients such as celebrities, politicians, or business tycoons, you can expect to earn a higher salary than if you work with regular individuals.

Is Fashion Styling a Lucrative Career Choice in India?

Yes, fashion styling can be a lucrative career choice in India. While it's true that the salary range of a fashion stylist can vary significantly, with the right skills, expertise, and experience, you can earn a considerable amount of money. Moreover, the fashion industry in India is rapidly growing, which means that there is a high demand for fashion stylists.

Question and Answer

Q: What qualifications do I need to become a fashion stylist in India?

A: While there are no specific qualifications required to become a fashion stylist in India, having a degree in fashion design or a related field can give you an edge over other candidates. Additionally, completing a course in fashion styling can also be beneficial.

Q: Can I work as a freelance fashion stylist in India?

A: Yes, you can work as a freelance fashion stylist in India. Many fashion stylists prefer to work as freelancers as it gives them more control over their work schedule and allows them to work with a variety of clients.

Q: How can I increase my salary as a fashion stylist in India?

A: To increase your salary as a fashion stylist in India, you need to focus on building your skills, expertise, and experience. Additionally, working with high-profile clients and building a strong network can also help you earn a higher salary.

Q: What is the job outlook for fashion stylists in India?

A: The job outlook for fashion stylists in India is positive as the fashion industry is rapidly growing. Moreover, with the rise of social media and the increasing importance of personal branding, the demand for fashion stylists is likely to increase in the coming years.

Conclusion of How Much Do Fashion Stylists Earn in India

In conclusion, fashion styling can be a lucrative career choice in India. While the salary range of a fashion stylist can vary depending on various factors, with the right skills, experience, and expertise, you can earn a considerable amount of money. If you're passionate about fashion and have a keen eye for style, then becoming a fashion stylist might be the perfect career path for you.
