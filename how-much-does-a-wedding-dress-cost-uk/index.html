Factors that Influence Wedding Dress Cost

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can influence the cost of a wedding dress. These include:

Designer

Dresses from high-end designers such as Vera Wang and Jenny Packham are typically more expensive than those from lesser-known labels.

Fabric

Luxurious fabrics such as silk and lace can drive up the price of a dress, while synthetic materials may be more affordable.

Embellishments

Beading, embroidery, and other embellishments can add to the cost of a dress, particularly if they are done by hand.
