How To Hide The Laces In Sneakers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of tying your sneakers every time you put them on? Do you find the laces to be more of a hindrance than a help? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with keeping their laces tied or find them to be an annoyance during physical activity. Fortunately, there is a solution: hiding the laces in your sneakers. In this tutorial, we'll walk you through the steps of how to hide the laces in sneakers.

If you've ever found yourself tripping over your untied laces or struggling to get them to stay tied, you know how frustrating it can be. Not to mention, laces can get in the way during exercise, making it difficult to move freely. By hiding the laces in your sneakers, you can eliminate these issues and enjoy a more comfortable and hassle-free experience.

First, remove the laces from your sneakers. You can do this by gently pulling them out from the eyelets on either side of the shoe. Once the laces are removed, you can begin the process of hiding them inside the shoe.

To hide the laces, start by folding them in half. Then, tuck the folded lace into the top of the shoe, underneath the tongue. Make sure the lace is tucked in securely and not visible from the outside of the shoe.

Next, take the remaining lace and wrap it around the back of the shoe, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Once you reach the top of the shoe, tuck the lace into the same spot where you tucked in the folded lace. Again, make sure the lace is secure and not visible from the outside of the shoe.

In summary, to hide the laces in your sneakers, you'll need to remove the laces, fold them in half, tuck them underneath the tongue of the shoe, wrap the remaining lace around the back of the shoe, and tuck it in next to the folded lace. This will keep the laces hidden and out of the way, allowing you to enjoy a more comfortable and hassle-free experience.

Tools You'll Need

To successfully hide the laces in your sneakers, you'll need a few tools. These include:

- Your sneakers

- A flat surface to work on

- A chair or stool to sit on (optional)

Now that you have everything you need, it's time to get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Remove the Laces

The first step in hiding the laces in your sneakers is to remove them. Gently pull the laces out from the eyelets on either side of the shoe. Set the laces aside for now.

Step 2: Fold the Laces in Half

Take one of the laces and fold it in half. The lace should now be half the length it was before. Repeat this step with the other lace.

Step 3: Tuck the Folded Laces Underneath the Tongue of the Shoe

Take one of the folded laces and tuck it underneath the tongue of the shoe. Make sure the lace is tucked in securely and not visible from the outside of the shoe. Repeat this step with the other lace.

Step 4: Wrap the Remaining Lace Around the Back of the Shoe

Take the remaining lace and wrap it around the back of the shoe, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Once you reach the top of the shoe, tuck the lace into the same spot where you tucked in the folded lace. Again, make sure the lace is secure and not visible from the outside of the shoe.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to keep in mind when hiding the laces in your sneakers:

- Make sure the laces are tucked in securely to prevent them from coming loose during physical activity.

- If you have trouble tucking the laces in, try using a small tool like a pen or pencil to help you tuck them in more easily.

- Experiment with different ways of wrapping the lace around the back of the shoe to find the method that works best for you.

Question and Answer

Q: Will hiding the laces in my sneakers damage the shoes?

A: No, hiding the laces in your sneakers should not cause any damage to the shoes.

Q: Can I still adjust the fit of my sneakers if the laces are hidden?

A: Yes, you can still adjust the fit of your sneakers even if the laces are hidden. Simply use your fingers to adjust the tongue of the shoe and make any necessary adjustments to the fit.

Q: Is hiding the laces in sneakers suitable for all types of sneakers?

A: Yes, hiding the laces is suitable for all types of sneakers that have laces.

Q: Will hiding the laces make my sneakers look different?

A: No, hiding the laces should not significantly alter the appearance of your sneakers.

Conclusion of How to Hide the Laces in Sneakers

By hiding the laces in your sneakers, you can enjoy a more comfortable and hassle-free experience. Follow these steps to hide the laces in your sneakers and enjoy a more streamlined look and feel. Don't let laces hold you back from your favorite activities any longer!
