How To Measure Neck Size On Dress Shirt A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of buying dress shirts that don't fit properly? One of the most important factors in finding the perfect dress shirt is knowing your neck size. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of how to measure neck size on dress shirt.

We understand that measuring your neck size can be a pain point for many people. It can be confusing and intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. However, knowing your neck size is crucial to finding a dress shirt that fits comfortably and looks great on you.

What is Neck Size and Why is it Important?

Neck size is the measurement of the circumference of your neck. It is an important factor when it comes to finding a dress shirt that fits properly because the collar of the shirt should fit comfortably around your neck without being too tight or too loose.

If the collar of your dress shirt is too tight, it can cause discomfort and restrict your movement. On the other hand, if the collar is too loose, it can look sloppy and unprofessional. Therefore, it is important to measure your neck size accurately to ensure that you find a dress shirt that fits perfectly.

How to Measure Neck Size on Dress Shirt

Here are the steps you should follow to measure your neck size on a dress shirt:

  1. Use a soft measuring tape. If you don't have one, you can use a piece of string and then measure the length of the string with a ruler.
  2. Stand in front of a mirror and make sure your posture is straight.
  3. Wrap the measuring tape around the base of your neck, where your neck meets your shoulders. Make sure the tape is snug but not too tight.
  4. Read the measurement on the tape or measure the length of the string with a ruler.
  5. Round the measurement to the nearest half inch.

Now that you have your neck size, you can use it to find the perfect dress shirt that fits comfortably and looks great on you.

Tips for Measuring Neck Size on Dress Shirt

Here are some tips to keep in mind when measuring your neck size on a dress shirt:

  • Make sure you're standing up straight and looking straight ahead when taking the measurement.
  • Don't pull the measuring tape too tight or too loose. It should be snug but not constricting.
  • If you're in between sizes, it's better to go up a size than down.
  • Measure your neck size every few months, as it can change due to weight gain or loss.

Common Questions About How to Measure Neck Size on Dress Shirt

Q: Can I measure my neck size by myself?

A: Yes, you can measure your neck size by yourself. However, it's easier if you have someone else do it for you.

Q: What if my neck size is between two sizes?

A: If your neck size is between two sizes, it's better to go up a size than down. You can always adjust the fit of the dress shirt using tailoring or alterations.

Q: How often should I measure my neck size?

A: It's a good idea to measure your neck size every few months, especially if you've gained or lost weight.

Q: Can I use a regular measuring tape to measure my neck size?

A: Yes, you can use a regular measuring tape to measure your neck size. Just make sure it's soft and flexible.

Conclusion of How to Measure Neck Size on Dress Shirt

Knowing your neck size is an important factor in finding a dress shirt that fits comfortably and looks great on you. By following the steps we've outlined in this guide, you can easily measure your neck size on a dress shirt. Remember to measure your neck size every few months, as it can change due to weight gain or loss. With this knowledge, you can confidently shop for dress shirts that fit you perfectly.
