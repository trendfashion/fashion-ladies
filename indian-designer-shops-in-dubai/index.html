Explore The Best Indian Designer Shops In Dubai

Written by Luffy Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in Dubai and looking for the best Indian designer shops? Look no further. Dubai is home to some of the best Indian designer shops that cater to your fashion needs. From traditional Indian wear to contemporary designs, these shops offer a wide range of options to choose from.

Shopping for Indian designer wear can be a daunting task, especially if you are new to the city or not familiar with the local market. It can be overwhelming to navigate through the numerous shops and find the one that meets your needs.

But worry not; we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the best Indian designer shops in Dubai and help you find the perfect outfit for any occasion.

Find Traditional Indian Wear at Ritu Kumar

Ritu Kumar is a well-known name in the Indian fashion industry, and her Dubai store offers a beautiful collection of traditional Indian wear. From sarees to lehengas, salwar kameez to anarkalis, you can find everything here. The store is located in the Dubai Mall and is easily accessible.

I recently visited Ritu Kumar and found the staff to be very helpful. They assisted me in finding the perfect saree for a wedding, and the quality of the fabric was excellent. The prices are on the higher side, but the quality justifies it.

Contemporary Designs at Manish Malhotra

Manish Malhotra is a name synonymous with Bollywood fashion, and his Dubai store offers a stunning collection of contemporary Indian wear. From elegant gowns to trendy lehengas, you can find everything here. The store is located in the Dubai Mall and is easily accessible.

I visited Manish Malhotra for a friend's engagement and found the perfect outfit there. The staff was friendly and helped me choose the right outfit. The prices are on the higher side, but the quality of the fabric and design make it worth it.

Explore Multi-Designer Collections at Aara Fashion

Aara Fashion is a multi-designer store that offers a wide range of Indian designer wear. From bridal wear to casual wear, you can find everything here. The store is located in the Wafi Mall and is easily accessible.

I recently visited Aara Fashion and was impressed with the variety of collections they had. The staff was friendly and helped me find the perfect outfit for a family function. The prices are on the higher side, but the quality of the outfits justifies it.

Shop for Budget-Friendly Outfits at Kala Niketan

Kala Niketan is a popular Indian designer store that offers budget-friendly outfits without compromising on quality. From sarees to salwar kameez, you can find a range of designs here. The store is located in the Meena Bazaar area and is easily accessible.

I visited Kala Niketan for a friend's wedding and found the perfect outfit within my budget. The staff was helpful, and the quality of the fabric was good. The prices are reasonable, and you can find some good deals during the sale season.

Question and Answer

Q1. What is the best time to visit Indian designer shops in Dubai?

A. The best time to visit Indian designer shops in Dubai is during the shopping festival season, which usually starts in December and lasts until February. You can find some great deals and discounts during this season.

Q2. Are there any online Indian designer shops in Dubai?

A. Yes, there are several online Indian designer shops in Dubai that offer a range of designs. Some popular ones are Jiva Couture, Utsav Fashion, and Ethnic Wear.

Q3. Can I find custom-made Indian designer outfits in Dubai?

A. Yes, several Indian designer shops in Dubai offer custom-made outfits. You can discuss your requirements with the designer and get an outfit tailored to your specifications.

Q4. Are Indian designer outfits expensive in Dubai?

A. Indian designer outfits can be expensive in Dubai, but you can find budget-friendly options as well. It all depends on the designer and the quality of the outfit.

Conclusion of Indian Designer Shops in Dubai

Dubai is home to some of the best Indian designer shops that cater to your fashion needs. From traditional Indian wear to contemporary designs, you can find everything here. It can be overwhelming to navigate through the numerous shops in Dubai, but with the help of this guide, you can find the perfect outfit for any occasion.
