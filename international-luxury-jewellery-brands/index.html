Brands to Know: Tiffany & Co.

When it comes to luxury jewellery brands, Tiffany & Co. is a household name. Founded in 1837, the brand is known for its iconic blue box and timeless pieces. Personally, I fell in love with the brand when my partner proposed to me with a Tiffany & Co. engagement ring. The quality and craftsmanship of the ring were evident, making it a piece I will treasure forever.

Tiffany & Co. is known for its high-quality diamonds, which are ethically sourced and conflict-free. Additionally, the brand is committed to sustainability and uses recycled materials in its jewellery. If you are looking for a timeless piece that will never go out of style, Tiffany & Co. is an excellent place to start.

Investing in Luxury Jewellery

Investing in luxury jewellery brands can be a smart move for those looking to add value to their collection. How to Care for Your Luxury Jewellery

When investing in luxury jewellery, it is essential to take proper care of the pieces to ensure they last a lifetime. It is recommended to store jewellery in a cool, dry place and avoid exposing it to harsh chemicals or extreme temperatures. Additionally, it is essential to clean jewellery regularly to maintain its shine and lustre.
