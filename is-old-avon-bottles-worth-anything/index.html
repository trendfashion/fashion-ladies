Are Old Avon Bottles Worth Anything

Written by Eveline Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have a collection of old Avon bottles gathering dust in your attic or basement? Perhaps you inherited them from a family member and you're wondering if they're worth anything. Many people have similar questions about their old Avon bottles, and the answer isn't always clear-cut.

Avon has been producing collectible bottles since the 1960s, and they've released thousands of different designs over the years. While some of these bottles can be quite valuable, others are not worth much at all. The value of an old Avon bottle depends on a variety of factors, including its age, rarity, condition, and design.

The short answer to the question of whether old Avon bottles are worth anything is: it depends. Some bottles can fetch hundreds or even thousands of dollars at auction, while others are worth only a few dollars. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the factors that determine the value of old Avon bottles and give you some tips on how to determine whether your collection is worth anything.

Factors That Determine the Value of Old Avon Bottles

One of the most important factors that determines the value of an old Avon bottle is its age. Generally speaking, the older the bottle, the more valuable it is likely to be. Bottles from the 1960s and 1970s are often quite collectible, as are bottles from special limited edition releases.

Rarity is another key factor that can impact the value of old Avon bottles. If a particular bottle was only produced in small quantities or for a limited time, it may be more valuable than other bottles from the same era. Additionally, bottles with unique or unusual designs are often more collectible than those with more common designs.

The condition of an old Avon bottle is also important when it comes to determining its value. Bottles that are in excellent condition, with no chips, cracks, or other damage, will generally be worth more than those that show signs of wear and tear. Finally, the design of the bottle itself can have a significant impact on its value. Some of the most collectible old Avon bottles are those with intricate or unusual designs, such as those shaped like cars, animals, or other objects.

Personal Experience with Old Avon Bottles

My grandmother was an Avon lady for many years, and she had a large collection of old Avon bottles. When she passed away, my family was left with the task of sorting through her belongings, including her collection of bottles. We quickly realized that some of the bottles were quite valuable, while others were not worth much at all.

One of the most valuable bottles in my grandmother's collection was a limited edition bottle that had been released in the 1970s. It featured a unique design and was in excellent condition, and we were able to sell it for several hundred dollars. On the other hand, some of the more common bottles in her collection were only worth a few dollars each.

Tips for Determining the Value of Your Old Avon Bottles

If you have a collection of old Avon bottles and you're wondering whether they're worth anything, there are a few things you can do to determine their value. First, you can do some research online to see if you can find similar bottles for sale, and how much they're selling for. You can also consult with a professional appraiser who specializes in collectibles, who can give you a more accurate idea of the value of your bottles.

Conclusion of Old Avon Bottles Worth Anything

While not all old Avon bottles are worth a lot of money, some can be quite valuable. The value of an old Avon bottle depends on a variety of factors, including its age, rarity, condition, and design. If you have a collection of old Avon bottles, it's worth taking the time to determine their value, as you may be sitting on a valuable collection.
