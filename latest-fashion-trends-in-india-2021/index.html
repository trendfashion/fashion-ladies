Latest Fashion Trends In India 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

India is a country that is known for its diverse culture and traditions. Fashion in India has always been an integral part of this rich culture. With the changing times, the fashion industry in India has undergone a significant transformation. The year 2021 has brought forth a plethora of new and exciting fashion trends that are worth exploring.

For many, keeping up with the latest fashion trends in India can be a challenge. From traditional attire to western wear, there are a lot of options to choose from. Additionally, there is always the pressure to stay updated with the latest trends and styles.

If you are someone who struggles with keeping up with the latest fashion trends in India, then this guide is for you. In this article, we will explore the latest fashion trends in India for 2021 and how you can incorporate them into your wardrobe.

So, without further ado, let's dive into the world of fashion and explore the latest trends in India for 2021.

The Rise of Sustainable Fashion

Sustainability has become a buzzword in the fashion industry, and for good reason. The fashion industry is one of the biggest polluters globally, and the rise of sustainable fashion is a step towards reducing its impact on the environment. In 2021, sustainable fashion is becoming increasingly popular in India. From eco-friendly fabrics to upcycled clothing, sustainable fashion is here to stay.

Recently, I had the opportunity to visit a sustainable fashion exhibition in Mumbai. It was fascinating to see the innovative ways in which designers were using eco-friendly materials to create beautiful fashion pieces. From dresses made of bamboo to jackets made of recycled plastic, the possibilities are endless when it comes to sustainable fashion.

Bright Colors and Bold Prints

2021 is all about bold colors and prints. Bright colors such as hot pink, vivid red, and electric blue are making a comeback. Whether you're wearing a saree, a kurta, or a dress, incorporating bright colors into your wardrobe is an easy way to stay on-trend.

Prints are also a big trend this year. From floral to geometric, there is a print for everyone. Bold prints are perfect for making a statement, and they can be incorporated into any outfit. For example, you can pair a printed blouse with a solid-colored saree or wear a printed dress with neutral accessories.

The Return of Retro Fashion

Retro fashion is making a comeback in 2021. From bell-bottoms to oversized sunglasses, the 70s and 80s are back in style. Denim jackets, wide-leg trousers, and tie-dye prints are all popular trends this year. There is a certain nostalgia associated with retro fashion, and it's a great way to add some fun to your wardrobe.

How to Incorporate Latest Fashion Trends in India 2021 into Your Wardrobe

Now that we've explored the latest fashion trends in India for 2021, you may be wondering how to incorporate them into your wardrobe. Here are some tips:

  • Invest in sustainable fashion pieces that you can wear for years to come.
  • Add some bright colors and prints to your wardrobe to stay on-trend.
  • Experiment with retro fashion by incorporating vintage pieces into your outfit.

The Comfortable Fashion Trend

2021 has been a year of comfort, and fashion is no exception. Athleisure wear is becoming increasingly popular in India, and it's not hard to see why. Comfortable clothing such as leggings, joggers, and oversized t-shirts are perfect for lounging around the house or running errands. The best part? You can still look stylish while being comfortable.

Accessorize, Accessorize, Accessorize

Accessories are a great way to elevate any outfit. In 2021, statement jewelry and hair accessories are on-trend. From chunky necklaces to oversized earrings, there are a lot of options to choose from. Hair accessories such as headbands and scrunchies are also making a comeback.

Question and Answer Section

Q: What are some sustainable fashion brands in India?

A: Some sustainable fashion brands in India include B Label, Doodlage, and No Nasties.

Q: How can I incorporate bright colors into my wardrobe?

A: You can start by adding a pop of color to your outfit with accessories such as a bright-colored scarf or bag. You can also experiment with bright colored clothing such as a hot pink kurta or an electric blue dress.

Q: How can I wear retro fashion without looking outdated?

A: You can incorporate retro pieces into your outfit by pairing them with modern clothing. For example, you can wear a denim jacket with a pair of skinny jeans and ankle boots.

Q: Can I wear athleisure wear outside of the house?

A: Absolutely! Athleisure wear is perfect for running errands, going to the gym, or even meeting friends for a casual lunch.

Conclusion of Latest Fashion Trends in India 2021

The fashion industry in India is constantly evolving, and keeping up with the latest trends can be a challenge. However, by incorporating some of the latest fashion trends in India for 2021 into your wardrobe, you can stay on-trend while still being true to your personal style. From sustainable fashion to retro pieces, there are a lot of options to choose from. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles and trends to find what works best for you!
