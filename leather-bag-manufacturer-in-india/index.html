What to Look for in a Leather Bag Manufacturer in India?

When choosing a leather bag manufacturer in India, there are several factors to consider:

How to Choose the Right Leather:

When choosing a leather bag, it is important to consider the type of leather that is used. The two most common types of leather are full-grain and top-grain. Full-grain leather is the highest quality and is made from the top layer of the hide. It is durable, ages well, and has a natural texture. Top-grain leather is made from the second layer of the hide and is slightly less durable than full-grain leather. However, it is still a good quality option.
