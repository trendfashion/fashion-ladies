Men s Casual Shoe Trends 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

From classic sneakers to comfortable loafers, men's casual shoe trends 2021 are all about style and comfort. Whether you're looking to upgrade your footwear collection or need a new pair of shoes for a specific occasion, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to stay on-trend.

As men, we often struggle to find the perfect balance between style and comfort when it comes to footwear. With so many options available in the market, it can be challenging to keep up with the latest trends and find a pair that meets our needs. In addition, there are different types of shoes for different occasions, making it difficult to know what to wear and when to wear it.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to men's casual shoe trends 2021. We will cover the latest styles, materials, and colors, as well as provide tips on how to wear them and what occasions they are best suited for.

In summary, this article will cover everything you need to know about men's casual shoe trends 2021, including the latest styles, materials, and colors, as well as tips on how to wear them and what occasions they are best suited for.

Classic Sneakers

Classic sneakers are a staple in any man's shoe collection. They are versatile and can be dressed up or down, making them perfect for a variety of occasions. Whether you're running errands or going out for drinks with friends, classic sneakers are a great choice.

In 2021, classic sneakers are all about minimalism. White sneakers with simple designs are the most popular style, with brands like Adidas, Nike, and Common Projects leading the way. However, if you're looking for something more colorful, you can opt for sneakers with bright accents or bold patterns.

Personally, I love wearing classic sneakers with a pair of dark jeans and a simple t-shirt. It's a casual and comfortable look that always looks great.

Loafers

Loafers are a comfortable and stylish option for men's casual shoes in 2021. They are perfect for wearing to the office or out for a casual dinner. Loafers come in a variety of materials, including leather, suede, and canvas, making them a versatile choice.

In terms of style, loafers are trending towards a more minimalist look in 2021. Simple designs with clean lines are the most popular, with brands like Gucci and Tod's leading the way. However, if you're looking for something more unique, you can opt for loafers with bold colors or patterns.

I love wearing loafers with a pair of chinos and a button-down shirt. It's a classic look that never goes out of style.

Boat Shoes

Boat shoes are a classic men's casual shoe that never goes out of style. They are perfect for wearing to the beach, on a boat, or out for a casual lunch. Boat shoes are typically made from leather or canvas and have a non-slip sole, making them perfect for wet or slippery surfaces.

In 2021, boat shoes are trending towards a more minimalist look, with simple designs and neutral colors being the most popular. Brands like Sperry and Sebago are leading the way in boat shoe trends.

I love wearing boat shoes with a pair of shorts and a simple t-shirt. It's a comfortable and casual look that's perfect for summer.

Sandals

Sandals are a comfortable and breathable option for men's casual shoes in 2021. They are perfect for wearing to the beach, around the house, or out for a casual lunch. Sandals come in a variety of styles, including flip-flops, slides, and gladiator sandals.

In terms of style, sandals are trending towards a more minimalist look in 2021. Simple designs with clean lines are the most popular, with brands like Birkenstock and Teva leading the way.

I love wearing sandals with a pair of shorts and a simple t-shirt. It's a comfortable and casual look that's perfect for summer.

Question and Answer

Q: What are the most popular colors for men's casual shoes in 2021?

A: Neutral colors like white, black, and beige are the most popular for men's casual shoes in 2021.

Q: Can I wear loafers with shorts?

A: Yes, you can wear loafers with shorts. It's a comfortable and stylish look that's perfect for summer.

Q: Are boat shoes suitable for formal occasions?

A: Boat shoes are not suitable for formal occasions. They are best suited for casual occasions like the beach or a casual lunch.

Q: What types of socks should I wear with sandals?

A: It's best to wear no-show socks or go sockless when wearing sandals.

Conclusion of Men's Casual Shoe Trends 2021

In conclusion, men's casual shoe trends 2021 are all about style and comfort. Classic sneakers, loafers, boat shoes, and sandals are all popular styles, with minimalist designs and neutral colors being the most popular. When it comes to wearing these shoes, it's important to choose the right style for the occasion and pair them with the right clothing. By following these tips, you'll be able to stay on-trend and comfortable in 2021.
