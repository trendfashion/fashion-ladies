The Different Types of Men's Formal Shoes

As mentioned earlier, men's formal shoes come in a variety of styles. Here's a quick rundown of some of the most popular types:

Brogues: Brogues are a type of shoe with decorative perforations along the edges and toe. They come in a variety of styles and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.
