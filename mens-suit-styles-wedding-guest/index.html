Mens Suit Styles For Wedding Guests A Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Weddings are a special occasion and dressing up for them can be a challenge, especially for men. It can be difficult to know what to wear, what style to choose, and how to accessorize. In this article, we'll explore some popular mens suit styles for wedding guests, so you can look your best and feel confident.

If you're attending a wedding as a guest, you want to look your best, but you don't want to upstage the groom. You also want to dress appropriately for the occasion, whether it's a formal, semi-formal, or casual wedding. So, what should you wear?

There are a few things to keep in mind when choosing your outfit for a wedding. First, consider the dress code. If the invitation specifies black tie, you should wear a tuxedo. If it's semi-formal, you can wear a suit or a blazer and dress pants. If it's a casual wedding, you can go for a more relaxed look, such as a linen suit or a sports coat and khakis.

When it comes to mens suit styles for wedding guests, there are a few popular options to choose from. These include:

The Classic Suit

A classic suit is always a safe choice for a wedding. You can't go wrong with a navy or charcoal suit, which are versatile and can be dressed up or down. Pair your suit with a crisp white shirt, a solid tie, and dress shoes. You can also add a pocket square for a pop of color.

Personally, I attended a wedding last year wearing a classic navy suit. It was a formal wedding, so I paired my suit with a white shirt, a black tie, and black dress shoes. I also added a black pocket square to finish off the look.

The Three-Piece Suit

A three-piece suit is a more formal option that consists of a jacket, vest, and pants. This style is perfect for a black tie or semi-formal wedding. You can opt for a classic color such as black or grey, or go for something more bold like a burgundy or navy blue.

I recently attended a semi-formal wedding and wore a dark grey three-piece suit. I paired it with a white shirt, a black tie, and black dress shoes. The vest added an extra layer of sophistication to the outfit.

The Double-Breasted Suit

A double-breasted suit is a stylish option that has gained popularity in recent years. This style features a jacket with two columns of buttons, creating a more structured and formal look. You can go for a classic color like navy or grey, or choose a more daring color like green or burgundy.

Personally, I haven't worn a double-breasted suit to a wedding yet, but I've seen it done well. It's a great option for a black tie or formal wedding, and can be paired with a white shirt, a solid tie, and dress shoes.

The Seersucker Suit

A seersucker suit is a more casual option that's perfect for a summer wedding. This style features a lightweight fabric with a puckered texture, making it breathable and comfortable in warm weather. You can go for a classic blue and white stripe or choose a more colorful option.

I attended a beach wedding last year and wore a seersucker suit. I paired it with a white shirt, a navy tie, and brown dress shoes. The lightweight fabric kept me cool in the summer heat, and the blue and white stripe added a fun touch to the outfit.

Accessorizing Your Suit

Once you've chosen your suit, it's time to accessorize. Here are some tips:

- Tie: Choose a tie that complements the color of your suit and shirt. A solid tie is always a safe choice, but you can also opt for a patterned tie for a more daring look.

- Pocket Square: A pocket square adds a pop of color and personality to your outfit. You can go for a classic white square or choose a color that complements your tie.

- Shoes: Dress shoes are a must for a wedding. Choose a pair that's comfortable and complements the color of your suit. Black or brown are classic options.

- Belt: If you're wearing a belt, choose one that matches the color of your shoes.

Tips for Choosing the Right Suit

When choosing a suit for a wedding, keep these tips in mind:

- Consider the dress code: Make sure you're dressed appropriately for the occasion.

- Choose the right fit: Your suit should fit well and be comfortable to wear. Consider getting it tailored if necessary.

- Stick to classic colors: If you're not sure what color to choose, stick to classic colors like navy, charcoal, or black.

- Don't upstage the groom: Your suit should be stylish but not showy. Avoid anything that's too bold or attention-grabbing.

Conclusion of Mens Suit Styles for Wedding Guests

Choosing the right suit for a wedding can be a challenge, but with these tips, you'll be able to make a stylish and appropriate choice. Whether you opt for a classic suit, a three-piece suit, a double-breasted suit, or a seersucker suit, make sure it fits well and complements the dress code. Don't forget to accessorize with a tie, pocket square, shoes, and belt. With these mens suit styles for wedding guests, you'll be able to look your best and feel confident.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a black suit to a wedding?

A: Yes, a black suit is a classic and appropriate choice for a wedding, especially if it's a formal or black tie event.

Q: Can I wear a patterned suit to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a patterned suit, but make sure the pattern isn't too bold or attention-grabbing. Stick to classic patterns like pinstripes or herringbone.

Q: Can I wear a bow tie to a wedding?

A: Yes, a bow tie is a stylish and appropriate choice for a wedding, especially if it's a formal or black tie event. Make sure it complements the color of your suit and shirt.

Q: Can I wear a vest without a jacket to a wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code. If it's a black tie or semi-formal wedding, you should wear a jacket with your vest. If it's a casual wedding, you can wear a vest without a jacket.
