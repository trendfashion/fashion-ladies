Mens Sunglasses Brands Logos A Guide To The Top Brands

Written by Bardi Sep 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in the market for a new pair of sunglasses but don't know where to start? With so many brands and styles out there, it can be overwhelming trying to find the perfect pair. One way to narrow down your search is by looking at the logos of different mens sunglasses brands. The logo can give you a good idea of the brand's style and quality. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at some of the top mens sunglasses brands logos and what they represent.

When it comes to choosing a pair of sunglasses, there are a few things to keep in mind. You want to find a pair that not only looks good on you but also provides adequate protection from the sun's harmful rays. Additionally, you want to look for a brand that is reputable and has a history of producing high-quality products. By paying attention to the logos of different brands, you can get a sense of their style and quality before even trying on a pair of sunglasses.

Now, let's take a closer look at some of the top mens sunglasses brands logos and what they stand for.

Ray-Ban

Ray-Ban is one of the most well-known sunglasses brands in the world. Their logo is a simple white script that reads "Ray-Ban" with a red dot above the "i." The brand has been around since the 1930s and is known for its classic and timeless designs. Ray-Ban sunglasses are made with high-quality materials and provide excellent UV protection.

Personally, I love Ray-Ban sunglasses. I have a pair of Wayfarers that I wear almost every day. They're comfortable and look great with just about any outfit.

Oakley

Oakley is another popular sunglasses brand that is known for its sporty and innovative designs. The brand's logo features an "O" with the word "Oakley" written underneath. Oakley sunglasses are designed for athletes and outdoor enthusiasts and are made with cutting-edge materials and technology.

I've worn Oakley sunglasses while playing sports and they're incredibly comfortable and provide great eye protection. The lenses are also polarized, which helps reduce glare and improve visibility on sunny days.

Maui Jim

Maui Jim is a relatively new player in the sunglasses industry but has quickly gained a reputation for producing high-quality and stylish sunglasses. The brand's logo features a stylized "MJ" in blue and white. Maui Jim sunglasses are designed with the island lifestyle in mind and are made with polarized lenses that provide excellent color clarity and contrast.

How to Choose the Right Pair of Sunglasses

When it comes to choosing a pair of sunglasses, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure the sunglasses provide adequate UV protection. Look for sunglasses that block at least 99% of UVA and UVB rays.

Next, consider the shape of your face. Different styles of sunglasses look better on different face shapes. For example, square-shaped frames look best on round faces, while round frames look best on square faces.

Finally, consider your personal style. You want to choose a pair of sunglasses that not only look good on you but also reflect your personal style. Whether you prefer classic and timeless designs or sporty and innovative styles, there's a pair of sunglasses out there for you.

Conclusion of Mens Sunglasses Brands Logos

When it comes to choosing a pair of sunglasses, paying attention to the logo of different brands can give you a good idea of their style and quality. Some of the top mens sunglasses brands logos include Ray-Ban, Oakley, and Maui Jim. By choosing a reputable brand that provides adequate UV protection and reflects your personal style, you can find the perfect pair of sunglasses to suit your needs.
