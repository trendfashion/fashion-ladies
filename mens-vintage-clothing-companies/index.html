How to Care for Your Vintage Clothing

Caring for your vintage clothing is important if you want it to last for years to come. First, always read the care instructions on the label before washing your vintage clothing. Second, avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, as these can damage the fabric. Finally, store your vintage clothing in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
