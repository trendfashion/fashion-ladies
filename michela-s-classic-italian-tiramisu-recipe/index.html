Michela's Classic Italian Tiramisu Recipe

When I first tried Michela's Classic Italian Tiramisu Recipe, I was blown away by how delicious it was. The cream was perfectly smooth and had just the right amount of sweetness, while the coffee-soaked ladyfingers added a nice crunch to the dessert.

To make Michela's Classic Italian Tiramisu Recipe, you'll need the following ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup strong brewed coffee
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 24-30 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Start by whisking the egg yolks and sugar together in a heatproof bowl set over a pot of simmering water. Add the milk and continue whisking until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. Remove from heat and let cool.

In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form. In another bowl, beat the mascarpone cheese until smooth. Fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone cheese until well combined.

In a small bowl, mix the coffee and rum together. Dip each ladyfinger in the coffee mixture and arrange them in a single layer in a 9x13 inch dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.

Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight. To serve, dust with cocoa powder and enjoy!

Why is Michela's Classic Italian Tiramisu Recipe so special?

Michela's Classic Italian Tiramisu Recipe is special because it's a traditional recipe that has been passed down through generations of Italian families. Michela herself learned the recipe from her grandmother, who was known for her delicious tiramisu.

One thing that sets Michela's recipe apart is the use of rum, which adds a nice depth of flavor to the dessert. The recipe also calls for a generous amount of mascarpone cheese, which gives the cream a rich, velvety texture that's hard to resist.

Tips and Tricks for Making the Perfect Tiramisu

If you want to make the perfect tiramisu, here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind:

  • Use high-quality ingredients, such as fresh eggs, good-quality coffee, and real vanilla extract.
  • Make sure to whip the cream until stiff peaks form, as this will give the dessert a light, airy texture.
  • Don't over-soak the ladyfingers in the coffee mixture, as this can make them too soggy.
  • Refrigerate the tiramisu for at least 2 hours before serving, as this will allow the flavors to meld together and the cream to set.

How to Store Tiramisu

If you have leftover tiramisu, it's important to store it properly to keep it fresh. The best way to store tiramisu is to cover it with plastic wrap and refrigerate it for up to 3 days. You can also freeze tiramisu for up to 2 months, but be sure to thaw it in the refrigerator before serving.
