Zimmermann: A Closer Look

When it comes to most expensive clothing brands in Australia, Zimmermann is one of the most well-known names in the industry. Founded in 1991 by sisters Nicky and Simone Zimmermann, the brand is known for its feminine and romantic designs that are perfect for any occasion.

Personally, I've always been a fan of Zimmermann's signature prints and intricate lace details. While their pieces may be on the pricier side, I've found that they are worth the investment due to their timeless design and exceptional quality. From their iconic wrap dresses to their stunning swimsuits, Zimmermann's pieces are always on-trend and perfect for any fashion-forward woman.

Scanlan Theodore: A Closer Look

Another one of the most expensive clothing brands in Australia is Scanlan Theodore. Founded in 1987 by Gary Theodore and Fiona Scanlan, the brand is known for its minimalist designs and use of high-quality materials. While their pieces may be simple, they are always elegant and sophisticated, making them perfect for any occasion.

Personally, I love Scanlan Theodore's clean lines and understated aesthetic. I've found that their pieces are perfect for dressing up or down, and can be worn for years to come. While their prices may be higher than some other brands, the quality and craftsmanship of their pieces is truly unmatched.

How to Shop for Luxury Fashion on a Budget
