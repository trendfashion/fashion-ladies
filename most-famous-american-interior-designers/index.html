Kelly Wearstler

Kelly Wearstler is an American interior designer, author, and businesswoman. She's known for her bold and eclectic designs that blend different textures, patterns, and colors. Wearstler's design philosophy is all about creating unexpected combinations that add personality and interest to a space. She's designed many notable projects, such as the Viceroy Hotel in Santa Monica and the BG restaurant in Chicago.

I had the pleasure of working with Kelly Wearstler on a project, and I was impressed by her attention to detail and her ability to create a design that was both functional and beautiful. She took the time to understand our needs and preferences, and she incorporated them into the design seamlessly.

Nate Berkus

Nate Berkus is an American interior designer, author, and television personality. He's known for his approachable and elegant designs that focus on the client's needs and preferences. Berkus's design philosophy is all about creating a space that feels personal and reflects the client's lifestyle. He's designed many notable projects, such as the Montage Beverly Hills Hotel and the Chicago penthouse of Oprah Winfrey.

I had the opportunity to attend one of Nate Berkus's design talks, and I was impressed by his down-to-earth approach and his ability to connect with his audience. He shared some valuable insights into his design process and how he incorporates the client's personality into the design.

Candice Olson

Candice Olson is an American interior designer and television personality. She's known for her elegant and timeless designs that blend classic and contemporary elements. Olson's design philosophy is all about creating a space that's functional, comfortable, and beautiful. She's designed many notable projects, such as the Trump International Hotel and Tower in Toronto and the HGTV show "Divine Design."

I've been a fan of Candice Olson's designs for years, and I love how she creates spaces that feel both cozy and luxurious. Her attention to detail and her ability to balance different design elements is truly inspiring.
