Discover The Most Popular Car Colour In Canada 2021

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to buy a car in Canada but you're not sure what colour to choose? Or are you simply curious about the latest trends in car colours? If so, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll explore the most popular car colour in Canada for 2021 and why it's such a popular choice among car buyers.

Choosing the right colour for your car is an important decision. After all, you'll be driving it for years to come, and you want to make sure you choose a colour that you'll be happy with for the long haul. But with so many colours to choose from, it can be overwhelming to make a decision.

The most popular car colour in Canada for 2021 is white. According to a recent report by Axalta, a leading global coatings supplier, white is the most popular car colour in Canada for the 11th year in a row. In fact, white accounts for 29% of all new cars sold in Canada.

So why is white such a popular choice among car buyers? One reason is that it's a timeless colour that never goes out of style. White cars are also seen as modern and sleek, and they tend to hold their value better than cars in other colours.

My Experience with White Cars

I've owned several cars in my lifetime, and two of them happened to be white. The first was a Toyota Corolla, and the second was a Honda Civic. I chose white for both of these cars because I liked how it looked – it was clean, simple, and elegant.

But I also found that white was a practical choice. For one thing, it didn't show dirt and grime as easily as darker colours. And in the summertime, it didn't get as hot inside the car as a black car would.

Why Do People Choose White Cars?

Aside from the reasons already mentioned, there are several other factors that make white a popular car colour in Canada. For one thing, it's a safe choice – it's a neutral colour that won't clash with other colours on the road. It's also a versatile colour that can be paired with different interior colours and finishes.

Another reason people choose white cars is that they're easy to maintain. Scratches and dings are less noticeable on a white car than on a darker-coloured car, and touch-up paint is readily available at most auto parts stores.

How Does White Compare to Other Car Colours?

While white is the most popular car colour in Canada, it's not the most popular colour worldwide. According to the same report by Axalta, the most popular colour globally for 2021 is gray, followed by white and black.

However, different regions have different preferences when it comes to car colours. For example, in North America, white is the most popular colour, while in Europe, black is the most popular colour.

How to Choose the Right Colour for Your Car

When choosing a colour for your car, there are several factors to consider. First and foremost, you should choose a colour that you like and that reflects your personality. You should also consider the resale value of the car – choosing a popular colour like white or black can help your car hold its value over time.

Other factors to consider include the climate where you live (darker colours can get hotter in the sun), the type of driving you do (dirt roads and off-roading may require a darker colour), and the style of the car (sporty cars may look better in brighter colours).

Conclusion of Most Popular Car Colour in Canada 2021

So there you have it – white is the most popular car colour in Canada for 2021. Whether you're buying a new car or simply curious about the latest trends in car colours, white is a safe and practical choice that never goes out of style.
