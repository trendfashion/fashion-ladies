Most Popular High End Furniture Brands A Guide To Finding The Best

Written by William Aug 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you in the market for high end furniture brands but not sure where to start? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to navigate through the different brands and styles. But fear not – we’ve got you covered with a guide to the most popular high end furniture brands and what makes them stand out.

When it comes to high end furniture, quality, craftsmanship, and design are key factors to consider. You want furniture that not only looks good but also lasts for years to come. Additionally, high end furniture often carries a higher price tag, so it’s important to make sure you’re investing in something that’s worth the cost.

So, what are the most popular high end furniture brands? Below, we’ve rounded up some of the top names in the industry and what sets them apart.

1. Restoration Hardware

Restoration Hardware is known for its luxurious and timeless designs that blend classic and modern elements. The brand offers a wide range of furniture and home decor items, from sofas and chairs to lighting and accessories. Restoration Hardware prides itself on using high-quality materials and expert craftsmanship to create pieces that are both beautiful and functional.

Personally, I’ve had my eye on Restoration Hardware’s Cloud Sofa for years. With its plush cushions and deep seats, it’s the perfect place to cozy up and relax.

2. Ethan Allen

Ethan Allen is another well-known name in the world of high end furniture. The brand is known for its traditional designs that exude elegance and sophistication. From dining tables and chairs to bedroom sets and home office furniture, Ethan Allen has everything you need to create a timeless and stylish home.

I recently purchased a dining set from Ethan Allen and was blown away by the level of detail and craftsmanship. The table and chairs are not only beautiful but also sturdy and well-made.

The Benefits of Investing in High End Furniture

While high end furniture may come with a higher price tag, there are numerous benefits to investing in quality pieces. For one, high end furniture is often made with better materials and superior craftsmanship, which means it will last longer and require less maintenance over time.

Additionally, high end furniture is often more comfortable and functional than cheaper alternatives. You’ll also have access to a wider range of styles and designs, allowing you to create a truly unique and personalized home.

Tips for Choosing the Right High End Furniture for Your Home

When shopping for high end furniture, it’s important to keep a few key factors in mind. First, consider your personal style and the overall aesthetic of your home. Look for pieces that complement your existing decor and bring a sense of cohesiveness to your space.

You should also consider the size and layout of your home when choosing furniture. Make sure that the pieces you choose fit comfortably in your space and don’t overwhelm the room.

3. Bernhardt

Bernhardt is a family-owned furniture company that has been around for over 125 years. The brand is known for its high-quality materials, expert craftsmanship, and innovative designs. From sofas and chairs to bedroom sets and dining tables, Bernhardt has a wide range of furniture options to suit any style.

Personally, I love Bernhardt’s Criteria Collection, which features sleek and modern designs that are perfect for a contemporary home.

4. Baker Furniture

Baker Furniture is a luxury furniture brand that’s been around since 1890. The brand is known for its exceptional quality and attention to detail, as well as its commitment to sustainability and responsible manufacturing practices. From sofas and chairs to bedroom sets and home office furniture, Baker Furniture has everything you need to create a stylish and sophisticated home.

Question and Answer:

Q: Is high end furniture worth the investment?

A: Yes, high end furniture is worth the investment for several reasons. For one, it’s often made with better materials and superior craftsmanship, which means it will last longer and require less maintenance over time. Additionally, high end furniture is often more comfortable and functional than cheaper alternatives.

Q: How do I choose the right high end furniture for my home?

A: When choosing high end furniture, consider your personal style and the overall aesthetic of your home. Look for pieces that complement your existing decor and bring a sense of cohesiveness to your space. You should also consider the size and layout of your home when choosing furniture.

Q: What are some of the top high end furniture brands?

A: Some of the top high end furniture brands include Restoration Hardware, Ethan Allen, Bernhardt, and Baker Furniture.

Q: How can I tell if a piece of furniture is high quality?

A: Look for furniture that’s made with high-quality materials, such as solid wood, leather, or natural fibers. Additionally, pay attention to the level of detail and craftsmanship – high quality furniture will often have intricate details and flawless finishes.

Conclusion of Most Popular High End Furniture Brands:

When it comes to high end furniture, there are countless brands to choose from. However, by focusing on quality, craftsmanship, and design, you can narrow down your options and find the perfect pieces for your home. Whether you prefer traditional elegance or modern minimalism, there’s a high end furniture brand out there that’s perfect for you.
