How to Care for Your Levi's Jeans

To keep your Levi's jeans looking their best, it's important to care for them properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your jeans inside out in cold water to prevent fading and shrinkage.
  • Avoid using fabric softener, as it can damage the denim.
  • Hang your jeans to dry instead of using a dryer, which can cause shrinkage and damage the denim.
  • If you need to iron your jeans, do so on a low heat setting to prevent damage to the denim.
