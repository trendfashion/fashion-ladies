Renewable Energy Sources in Australia

Australia has abundant renewable energy sources, including wind, solar, hydro, and geothermal power. Wind power is the most widely used renewable energy source in the country, followed by solar power. Hydro power is also an important source of renewable energy in Australia, with large hydroelectric plants located in several states.
