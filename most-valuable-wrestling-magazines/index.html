Discover The Most Valuable Wrestling Magazines To Add To Your Collection

Written by Robby Oct 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a wrestling enthusiast looking for the most valuable wrestling magazines to add to your collection? As a wrestling fan, you know that magazines play a crucial role in your overall experience of the sport. They provide insider information, breaking news, and in-depth interviews with your favorite wrestlers.

But with so many wrestling magazines out there, it can be overwhelming to know which ones are worth your investment. That's why we've put together a list of the most valuable wrestling magazines that every wrestling fan should have.

So, whether you're a longtime wrestling fan or just getting started, this article is for you. Read on to discover the most valuable wrestling magazines to add to your collection.

Wrestling Observer Newsletter

One of the most respected wrestling magazines in the industry is the Wrestling Observer Newsletter. Founded in 1983 by Dave Meltzer, this magazine is known for its unbiased reporting, insider information, and in-depth analysis of the wrestling industry.

As a wrestling fan, I've been a subscriber to the Wrestling Observer Newsletter for years. I appreciate the level of detail and insight that goes into each issue. From match analysis to behind-the-scenes news, this magazine has it all.

Pro Wrestling Illustrated

Another must-have wrestling magazine for any collector is Pro Wrestling Illustrated. Founded in 1979, this magazine is known for its comprehensive coverage of the wrestling industry. It includes rankings, interviews, and features on top wrestlers from around the world.

I remember flipping through Pro Wrestling Illustrated as a kid and being in awe of the larger-than-life wrestlers featured in its pages. To this day, it remains a staple in my collection.

The Wrestling Magazines of the 90s

If you're looking for a blast from the past, you can't go wrong with wrestling magazines from the 90s. During this decade, wrestling was at its peak in popularity, and magazines like The Wrestler, Inside Wrestling, and WCW Magazine were flying off the shelves.

As someone who grew up in the 90s, I have a special place in my heart for these magazines. They're a time capsule of sorts, capturing the larger-than-life wrestlers, storylines, and events that defined the era.

How to Build Your Collection

Now that you know which wrestling magazines are the most valuable, you're probably wondering how to get your hands on them. Here are a few tips:

1. Check online marketplaces like eBay and Amazon for back issues.

2. Attend wrestling conventions and trade shows, where vendors often sell rare and hard-to-find magazines.

3. Network with other wrestling fans and collectors, who may be willing to sell or trade magazines from their own collections.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a wrestling magazine is valuable?

A: Look for rare or hard-to-find issues, issues featuring popular wrestlers or storylines, and issues in good condition.

Q: Can I sell my wrestling magazines?

A: Yes, you can sell your wrestling magazines online or at trade shows. Just make sure they're in good condition and that you're pricing them fairly.

Q: Are digital wrestling magazines worth collecting?

A: While digital magazines are convenient, they don't hold the same value as physical magazines. If you're looking to build a valuable collection, stick with physical copies.

Q: Can I subscribe to these wrestling magazines?

A: Yes, all of the magazines mentioned in this article offer subscription options.

Conclusion of Most Valuable Wrestling Magazines

Whether you're a longtime fan or just getting started, adding wrestling magazines to your collection is a great way to enhance your overall experience of the sport. By investing in the most valuable wrestling magazines, you'll gain access to insider information, breaking news, and in-depth analysis of the industry.

So, what are you waiting for? Start building your collection today!
