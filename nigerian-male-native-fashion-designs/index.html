Types of Nigerian Male Native Fashion Designs

Nigerian male native fashion designs come in many different styles and fabrics. Some of the most popular styles include:

Agbada: This is a flowing robe that is often worn with a matching hat and shoes. It is typically made from high-quality fabrics like silk or lace.

Babariga: This is a loose-fitting shirt that is typically worn with a matching pair of pants. It is usually made from cotton or other lightweight materials.

Kaftan: This is a long, flowing robe that is typically worn with a matching hat and shoes. It is often made from high-quality fabrics like silk or lace.

Sokoto: This is a loose-fitting pair of pants that are typically worn with a matching shirt. They are usually made from cotton or other lightweight materials.
