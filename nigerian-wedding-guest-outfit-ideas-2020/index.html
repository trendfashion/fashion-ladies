Nigerian Wedding Guest Outfit Ideas 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by William Oct 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you attending a Nigerian wedding soon and struggling to figure out what to wear? Do you want to look stylish and on-trend while still respecting the cultural traditions of the event? We've got you covered with the latest Nigerian wedding guest outfit ideas for 2020.

Weddings are joyous occasions, but they can also be stressful when it comes to figuring out what to wear. As a guest, you want to look your best while still being respectful of the culture and traditions of the event. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect outfit for a Nigerian wedding.

Our guide to Nigerian wedding guest outfit ideas 2020 will help you navigate this tricky fashion landscape and ensure that you look stylish and appropriate for the occasion.

Nigerian Wedding Guest Outfit Ideas 2020: Traditional vs. Modern

When it comes to Nigerian wedding guest outfits, there are two main options: traditional and modern. Traditional outfits are often made from beautiful, colorful fabrics like Ankara and lace and feature intricate embroidery and beading. Modern outfits, on the other hand, take inspiration from Western fashion trends and can be more streamlined and minimalist.

Personally, I prefer to wear traditional Nigerian outfits to weddings as they are a beautiful way to showcase the country's rich cultural heritage. However, there are plenty of stylish modern options available for those who prefer a more understated look.

Choosing the Right Fabric

One of the most important things to consider when choosing a Nigerian wedding guest outfit is the fabric. As mentioned, traditional Nigerian fabrics like Ankara and lace are popular choices, but there are also many other options available. When selecting a fabric, consider the weather, the time of day, and the formality of the event. For example, if the wedding is taking place outside in the middle of summer, you might want to opt for a lightweight cotton or linen fabric.

Accessorizing Your Outfit

No Nigerian wedding guest outfit is complete without the right accessories. Jewelry, headwraps, and shoes can all help to take your outfit to the next level. When it comes to jewelry, statement pieces like chunky beaded necklaces or oversized earrings can add a pop of color and personality to your look. Headwraps, or gele, are a traditional Nigerian accessory that can be worn in a variety of styles to complement your outfit. And when it comes to shoes, opt for something comfortable as you'll likely be on your feet for most of the day.

Styling Your Hair and Makeup

When it comes to hair and makeup, the key is to keep it simple and elegant. A chic updo or sleek ponytail can help to keep you cool in the heat while still looking stylish. For makeup, opt for a natural, dewy look with a pop of color on the lips or eyes.

FAQs About Nigerian Wedding Guest Outfit Ideas 2020

Q: Can I wear white to a Nigerian wedding?

A: No, white is typically reserved for the bride and should be avoided by wedding guests.

Q: Can men wear traditional Nigerian outfits to weddings?

A: Yes, men can wear traditional Nigerian outfits like agbada or babariga to weddings.

Q: Can I wear a jumpsuit to a Nigerian wedding?

A: Yes, a jumpsuit can be a stylish and modern option for a Nigerian wedding guest outfit. Just make sure that the fabric and style are appropriate for the occasion.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a Nigerian wedding?

A: No, jeans are too casual for a Nigerian wedding and should be avoided.

Conclusion of Nigerian Wedding Guest Outfit Ideas 2020

Attending a Nigerian wedding can be an amazing experience, but it can also be stressful when it comes to choosing the right outfit. By following our guide to Nigerian wedding guest outfit ideas 2020, you'll be able to find the perfect outfit that is stylish, appropriate, and respectful of the culture and traditions of the event.
