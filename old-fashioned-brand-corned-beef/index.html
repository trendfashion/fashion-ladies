The Cooking Process of Old Fashioned Brand Corned Beef

The cooking process of old fashioned brand corned beef is what sets it apart from other types of corned beef. The meat is cured in a special brine solution, which helps to tenderize it and infuse it with flavor. It is then slow-cooked for several hours, resulting in a tender and juicy dish that is packed with flavor.

How to Cook Old Fashioned Brand Corned Beef

Cooking old fashioned brand corned beef is incredibly easy. All you need is a slow cooker and a few ingredients. Firstly, place the corned beef in the slow cooker and add enough water to cover it. Then, add your desired seasonings and vegetables, such as onions, garlic, and carrots. Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, or until the meat is tender and falls apart easily.
