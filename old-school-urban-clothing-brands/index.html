Old School Urban Clothing Brands A Guide To The Classics

Written by Bardi Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring clothes? Do you want to stand out from the crowd with your unique style? Look no further than old school urban clothing brands. These classic brands have been around for decades, and for good reason. They offer a timeless and authentic look that never goes out of style.

Old school urban clothing brands cater to individuals who want to express themselves through their fashion choices. These brands offer an alternative to mainstream fashion, which can often feel generic and uninspired. With old school urban clothing brands, you can make a statement and show off your individuality.

The target of old school urban clothing brands is anyone who appreciates classic streetwear. Whether you're a fan of hip hop, skateboarding, or just love the retro look, these brands are for you. They offer a range of styles, from baggy jeans and graphic tees to bold prints and statement accessories.

In this article, we'll dive into some of the most iconic old school urban clothing brands and what makes them so special. We'll also explore the history behind these brands and their impact on streetwear culture.

FUBU

One of the most recognizable old school urban clothing brands is FUBU. Founded in 1992 by Daymond John, FUBU (which stands for "For Us, By Us") was created as a way to celebrate and represent black culture. The brand's signature look includes oversized jerseys, baggy jeans, and bold prints.

Personally, I remember wearing FUBU in the late 90s and early 2000s. I loved the way the clothes made me feel - confident, stylish, and like I was part of something bigger than myself. FUBU represented a movement, a way for black people to express themselves and show off their culture.

Karl Kani

Karl Kani is another iconic old school urban clothing brand. Founded in 1989 by Karl Kani himself, the brand was one of the first to blend hip hop and fashion. Kani's designs often featured bold prints, baggy jeans, and oversized graphic tees.

When I think of Karl Kani, I think of the classic 90s hip hop look. I remember seeing rappers like Tupac and Biggie wearing Karl Kani clothes in their music videos, and thinking how cool they looked. Karl Kani was a pioneer in streetwear fashion, and his influence can still be seen today.

Air Jordan

While not exclusively a streetwear brand, Air Jordan is still one of the most iconic old school urban clothing brands. The brand was created in 1984 by Nike, in collaboration with basketball legend Michael Jordan. Air Jordans are known for their unique design and high level of comfort, making them a favorite among sneakerheads and basketball fans alike.

As someone who loves sneakers, I can attest to the appeal of Air Jordans. There's something special about owning a pair of Jordans - they make you feel like you're part of a community. And with new releases coming out every year, there's always something to look forward to.

How to Shop for Old School Urban Clothing Brands

If you're interested in trying out old school urban clothing brands for yourself, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, do your research. Look up the history and background of the brands you're interested in, so you can fully appreciate their significance. Second, be prepared to spend a little extra money. Old school urban clothing brands are often more expensive than mainstream fashion, but the quality and style are worth it. Finally, don't be afraid to mix and match. Old school urban clothing brands are all about self-expression, so have fun with it!

Question and Answer

Q: What are some other old school urban clothing brands?

A: Other notable old school urban clothing brands include Cross Colours, Sean John, and Phat Farm.

Q: Can old school urban clothing brands be worn by anyone?

A: Absolutely! Old school urban clothing brands are for anyone who appreciates the style and history behind them.

Q: Where can I find old school urban clothing brands?

A: You can find old school urban clothing brands at specialty stores, online retailers, and even thrift shops.

Q: What should I pair with old school urban clothing brands?

A: Old school urban clothing brands can be paired with a variety of different accessories and styles. Experiment with different combinations to find what works best for you.

Conclusion of Old School Urban Clothing Brands

Old school urban clothing brands offer a unique and authentic style that can't be found anywhere else. From FUBU to Karl Kani to Air Jordan, these brands represent a movement and a culture that has had a lasting impact on streetwear fashion. If you're looking to stand out from the crowd and express yourself through your fashion choices, old school urban clothing brands are the way to go.
