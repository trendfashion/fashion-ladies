Top Popular Purse Brands For College Students

Written by Bardi May 19, 2023 · 4 min read

College is a time for growth and exploration. As a college student, you need to be ready for anything, from a day of classes to a night out with friends. One essential item that every college student needs is a purse. A good purse is not only functional, but it also adds style to your outfit. However, choosing the right purse can be a challenge, especially with so many options on the market.

Are you tired of carrying a heavy backpack around campus? Do you want a purse that can hold all your essentials while still looking fashionable? If so, then you're in the right place. In this blog post, we will discuss the top popular purse brands for college students.

When it comes to choosing a purse, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you want a purse that can hold all your necessary items, such as your phone, wallet, keys, and books. Second, you want a purse that is comfortable to carry around all day. Third, you want a purse that matches your personal style. With these factors in mind, let's take a closer look at the top popular purse brands for college students.

Kate Spade

When it comes to popular purse brands for college students, Kate Spade is a top choice. Kate Spade purses are known for their classic and sophisticated designs. They are made with high-quality materials and are built to last. Kate Spade purses are also versatile and can be worn with anything from a casual outfit to a formal dress.

I personally own a Kate Spade purse and have found it to be the perfect size for carrying all my essentials. The adjustable strap also makes it easy to wear as a shoulder bag or crossbody. Kate Spade purses range in price from around $100 to $500, depending on the style and materials used.

Vera Bradley

Another popular purse brand for college students is Vera Bradley. Vera Bradley purses are known for their colorful and playful designs. They are made with high-quality materials and are designed to be both functional and stylish. Vera Bradley purses come in a variety of sizes and styles, so you can find one that fits your needs.

Personally, I love my Vera Bradley crossbody purse. It's the perfect size for carrying my essentials, and the adjustable strap makes it easy to wear. Vera Bradley purses range in price from around $30 to $200, depending on the style and materials used.

Longchamp

If you're looking for a purse that is both stylish and practical, then Longchamp is a great option. Longchamp purses are known for their iconic foldable design and high-quality materials. They are perfect for carrying your essentials to class or on the go. Longchamp purses come in a variety of sizes and styles, so you can find one that fits your needs.

Personally, I love my Longchamp tote bag. It's the perfect size for carrying my books and laptop, and the foldable design makes it easy to store when not in use. Longchamp purses range in price from around $100 to $700, depending on the style and materials used.

Michael Kors

If you're looking for a purse that screams luxury, then Michael Kors is the brand for you. Michael Kors purses are known for their high-quality leather and classic designs. They are perfect for adding a touch of sophistication to any outfit. Michael Kors purses come in a variety of sizes and styles, so you can find one that fits your needs.

Personally, I love my Michael Kors crossbody purse. It's the perfect size for carrying my essentials, and the leather is so soft and durable. Michael Kors purses range in price from around $100 to $1000, depending on the style and materials used.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when choosing a purse for college?

A: When choosing a purse for college, you should look for one that can hold all your necessary items, is comfortable to carry around all day, and matches your personal style.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a college purse?

A: The price of a college purse can vary depending on the brand and materials used. You can expect to spend anywhere from $30 to $1000.

Q: What are some other popular purse brands for college students?

A: Other popular purse brands for college students include Coach, Fossil, and Marc Jacobs.

Q: What style of purse is best for college?

A: The best style of purse for college is one that is functional and comfortable to carry around all day. Crossbody and tote bags are popular choices.

Conclusion of Popular Purse Brands for College Students

Choosing the right purse is essential for every college student. The top popular purse brands for college students include Kate Spade, Vera Bradley, Longchamp, and Michael Kors. When choosing a purse, consider factors such as size, comfort, and style. No matter what brand you choose, a good purse can add style and functionality to your college experience.
