Best Popular Sunglasses Brands In India For New Ideas

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In sunny India, sunglasses are not only a fashion statement but also a necessity. With the summer season approaching, it's time to upgrade your collection of sunglasses. In this blog post, we will be discussing popular sunglasses brands in India that can elevate your style while protecting your eyes from harmful UV rays.

Whether you're looking for sunglasses for driving, sports, or just to make a fashion statement, there are certain pain points that come with choosing the perfect pair. These include finding sunglasses that fit your face shape, provide adequate protection, and are comfortable to wear for long hours.

If you're looking for a wide variety of sunglasses brands to choose from, India has got you covered. From luxury brands to affordable options, the market is flooded with options.

In summary, popular sunglasses brands in India offer a wide range of options to choose from, ensuring that you find the perfect pair of sunglasses to suit your needs and budget.

Ray-Ban

Ray-Ban is undoubtedly one of the most popular sunglasses brands in India. The brand is known for its classic and timeless designs, making it a favorite among many. The brand offers a wide range of frames, including aviators, wayfarers, and clubmasters. I personally own a pair of Ray-Ban aviators and can vouch for their durability and timeless style.

Fasttrack

Fasttrack is a popular Indian brand that offers a wide range of affordable sunglasses for both men and women. The brand is known for its trendy designs, making it a favorite among the younger generation. I have owned a pair of Fasttrack sunglasses for years now, and they have held up surprisingly well despite their affordable price point.

Maui Jim

Maui Jim is a luxury sunglasses brand that offers a wide range of stylish and high-quality sunglasses. The brand is known for its polarized lenses, which provide excellent protection against harmful UV rays. While the brand may be on the pricier side, the quality and durability of their sunglasses make it a worthwhile investment.

How to choose the perfect pair of sunglasses?

When choosing the perfect pair of sunglasses, it's important to consider your face shape, the level of protection you require, and your personal style. If you have a round face, go for square or rectangular frames to provide contrast. If you have a square face, go for round or oval frames to soften your features. Additionally, look for sunglasses with polarized lenses to provide adequate protection against harmful UV rays.

Oakley

Oakley is a popular sports sunglasses brand that offers a wide range of durable and high-quality sunglasses. The brand is known for its innovative designs and technology, making it a favorite among athletes and sports enthusiasts. I have used Oakley sunglasses while playing outdoor sports, and they have provided excellent protection against the sun while staying comfortable and secure during physical activity.

Question and Answer

Q: Are polarized lenses necessary in sunglasses?

A: Polarized lenses are not necessary but are highly recommended as they provide excellent protection against harmful UV rays and reduce glare, making them ideal for outdoor activities.

Q: Can I wear sunglasses indoors?

A: While it is not necessary to wear sunglasses indoors, certain medical conditions such as light sensitivity may require you to wear them indoors. Additionally, some people wear sunglasses indoors as a fashion statement.

Q: How often should I replace my sunglasses?

A: It is recommended to replace your sunglasses every two years, or sooner if they become damaged or scratched.

Q: Are expensive sunglasses worth the investment?

A: While expensive sunglasses may seem like a splurge, they often offer better quality and durability, making them a worthwhile investment in the long run.

Conclusion of popular sunglasses brands in India

In conclusion, popular sunglasses brands in India offer a wide range of options to choose from, ensuring that you find the perfect pair of sunglasses to suit your needs and budget. Whether you're looking for a classic pair of Ray-Bans or a trendy pair of Fasttracks, there is a brand out there for everyone. Remember to choose sunglasses that provide adequate protection and fit your personal style to elevate your summer fashion game.
