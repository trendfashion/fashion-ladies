Discover The Best Retro Clothing Stores In The Uk

Written by Aprilia Nov 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old fashion trends? Do you want to stand out and express your unique personal style? Look no further than retro clothing stores in the UK! These stores offer a wide selection of vintage and retro-inspired clothing that will make you feel like a fashion icon.

If you're someone who loves the nostalgia of the past, then retro clothing stores in the UK will be your new favorite shopping destination. These stores cater to those who want to look different and stand out from the crowd. Retro clothing is all about expressing yourself and showcasing your personality through fashion.

The target of retro clothing stores in the UK is people who appreciate classic style and want to incorporate it into their wardrobe. Whether you're looking for a bold statement piece or a subtle vintage touch, retro clothing stores have something for everyone.

In this article, we'll explore the best retro clothing stores in the UK and provide you with everything you need to know about shopping for retro fashion. From vintage-inspired dresses to retro accessories, we'll cover it all!

Top Retro Clothing Stores in the UK

One of the best things about retro clothing stores in the UK is that each one has its own unique style and selection. Here are a few of our favorites:

1. Beyond Retro

With locations in London, Brighton, and Bristol, Beyond Retro is a must-visit for anyone looking for vintage and retro clothing. The store offers a wide selection of clothing from the 1920s to the 1990s, as well as upcycled pieces made from recycled materials.

Personally, I love the Beyond Retro in Brighton. The store is huge and has an incredible selection of vintage denim, leather jackets, and band t-shirts. I always leave with a unique and stylish piece that I can't find anywhere else.

2. Rokit

Rokit has been a staple in the London vintage scene since it opened its doors in 1986. The store offers a wide selection of vintage and retro clothing, as well as its own line of upcycled clothing. Rokit also has a great selection of vintage accessories, including sunglasses, bags, and jewelry.

I visited the Rokit store in Camden and was blown away by the selection of vintage sportswear. I found a vintage Adidas tracksuit that I wear all the time now.

What to Look for in a Retro Clothing Store

When shopping for retro clothing, it's important to look for stores that offer high-quality pieces that are still in good condition. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Quality of Clothing

Make sure to inspect the clothing closely for any tears, stains, or other damage. It's also a good idea to check the clothing tag for care instructions. Vintage clothing often requires special care.

2. Selection of Clothing

Look for stores that offer a wide selection of clothing from different eras. This will give you more options to choose from and allow you to create a unique look.

3. The Vintage Emporium

The Vintage Emporium is located in London's Shoreditch neighborhood and offers a wide selection of vintage and retro clothing, as well as antique furniture and home decor. The store has a unique and eclectic vibe that makes it a fun shopping experience.

When I visited The Vintage Emporium, I was impressed by the selection of vintage dresses and jumpsuits. The store also has a great selection of vintage hats and accessories.

4. Lucy in Disguise

Lucy in Disguise is a vintage and costume rental shop located in London's Covent Garden. The store has an incredible selection of vintage and retro clothing that you can rent for a special occasion or event.

When I rented a dress from Lucy in Disguise for a 1920s-themed party, I was impressed by the quality of the dress and the level of service. The staff was incredibly helpful and made sure that the dress fit me perfectly.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between vintage and retro clothing?

A: Vintage clothing refers to clothing that is at least 20 years old and is considered a classic or iconic piece from a specific era. Retro clothing, on the other hand, is clothing that is inspired by a specific era but is not necessarily from that era.

Q: Can you find retro clothing for men?

A: Yes! Many retro clothing stores in the UK offer a wide selection of men's clothing, including vintage denim, leather jackets, and band t-shirts.

Q: Is retro clothing expensive?

A: It depends on the store and the piece of clothing. While some retro clothing can be expensive, there are also many affordable options available.

Q: Can you find retro clothing online?

A: Yes! Many retro clothing stores in the UK have online shops, making it easy to shop for retro fashion from the comfort of your own home.

Conclusion of Retro Clothing Stores in the UK

Whether you're a fashionista or just looking to add some unique pieces to your wardrobe, retro clothing stores in the UK offer something for everyone. From vintage-inspired dresses to retro accessories, these stores are the perfect place to find your next statement piece.
