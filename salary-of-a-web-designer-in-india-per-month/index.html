How Much Salary Does A Web Designer In India Make Per Month

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a web designer in India wondering how much salary you should expect to make per month? Or are you an employer looking to hire a web designer and want to know what is the average salary range? Salary is one of the most important factors that both employees and employers consider in the hiring process. In this article, we will discuss the salary of a web designer in India per month and related keywords to help you understand the market better.

The salary of a web designer in India per month can vary depending on various factors such as experience, skills, location, industry, and company size. Knowing the average salary range can help you negotiate your salary or determine the salary range for a position you are hiring for.

The average salary of a web designer in India per month is around INR 25,000 to INR 50,000. However, this can vary depending on the factors mentioned above. For example, a web designer with 1-4 years of experience can expect to make around INR 20,000 to INR 40,000 per month, while a web designer with 5-9 years of experience can make around INR 40,000 to INR 80,000 per month.

In summary, the salary of a web designer in India per month can range from INR 25,000 to INR 80,000 depending on factors such as experience, skills, location, industry, and company size.

Factors Affecting the Salary of a Web Designer in India

Experience is one of the most important factors that affect the salary of a web designer in India. The more experience you have, the higher your salary will be. Skills such as proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other programming languages also play a role in determining the salary. Location is another factor that affects the salary of a web designer in India. Web designers in metropolitan cities such as Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore tend to make more than web designers in smaller cities. Industry and company size also play a role in determining the salary of a web designer in India.

Personal Experience

As a web designer with 5 years of experience in Mumbai, I make around INR 60,000 per month. My salary has increased over the years as I gained more experience and skills. I have worked for both small and large companies, and I noticed that the salary offered by large companies tend to be higher than small companies.

Salary Comparison in Different Industries

The salary of a web designer in India per month can also vary depending on the industry. For example, a web designer working in the IT industry can make more than a web designer working in the media industry. The salary can also vary depending on the company size. A web designer working for a large multinational company can make more than a web designer working for a small local company.

Salary Comparison in Different Cities

The salary of a web designer in India per month can also vary depending on the location. For example, a web designer working in Mumbai can make more than a web designer working in Pune. The cost of living in metropolitan cities is higher, and therefore, the salary tends to be higher as well.

How to Negotiate Your Salary as a Web Designer in India

When negotiating your salary as a web designer in India, it is important to do your research and know your worth. Research the average salary range for your level of experience and skills. Be confident and make a strong case for why you deserve the salary you are asking for. Be open to negotiation and try to find a middle ground that works for both you and your employer.

Conclusion of Salary of a Web Designer in India per Month

In conclusion, the salary of a web designer in India per month can vary depending on factors such as experience, skills, location, industry, and company size. It is important to do your research and negotiate your salary confidently. Keep in mind that salary is not the only factor to consider when accepting a job offer. Other factors such as work-life balance, job security, and benefits should also be taken into account.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary of a web designer with 2 years of experience in India?

A: A web designer with 2 years of experience in India can expect to make around INR 20,000 to INR 30,000 per month.

Q: Does location affect the salary of a web designer in India?

A: Yes, location is one of the factors that affect the salary of a web designer in India. Web designers in metropolitan cities such as Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore tend to make more than web designers in smaller cities.

Q: What skills are important for a web designer in India?

A: Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other programming languages are important skills for a web designer in India. Soft skills such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills are also important.

Q: Can a web designer in India make more than INR 80,000 per month?

A: Yes, a web designer with extensive experience and skills can make more than INR 80,000 per month. The salary of a web designer in India per month can vary depending on various factors such as experience, skills, location, industry, and company size.
