Discovering The Best Small Clothing Brands For Men In The Uk

Written by Frank Aug 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old high street fashion? Do you want to support small businesses while looking stylish? If you're a man in the UK, you're in luck! There are plenty of small clothing brands that offer unique and high-quality pieces that will make you stand out from the crowd.

One of the biggest pain points for men when it comes to clothing is finding something that's both stylish and affordable. Many high street stores offer fast fashion that falls apart after a few wears, and designer brands can be too expensive for the average person. Small clothing brands offer a solution to this problem, as they often use high-quality materials and ethical production methods, all while keeping their prices reasonable.

So, what should you look for when searching for small clothing brands in the UK? First and foremost, consider your personal style and what you're looking for in your wardrobe. Are you in need of basics like t-shirts and jeans, or are you looking for something more unique like a statement jacket or printed shirt? Once you have a clear idea of what you want, you can begin your search.

To make things easier for you, we've rounded up some of the best small clothing brands for men in the UK. Keep reading to discover our top picks and find your new favourite brand.

1. Oliver Spencer

Oliver Spencer is a UK-based brand that offers modern, stylish clothing with a focus on quality and sustainability. The brand uses natural materials like cotton, wool, and silk, and works with factories and mills that have a commitment to ethical production. From tailored suits to casual t-shirts, Oliver Spencer has something for every occasion.

Personally, I love Oliver Spencer's attention to detail and the way their pieces fit perfectly. The brand has a unique aesthetic that sets it apart from other small clothing brands, and I always feel confident and stylish when wearing their clothes.

2. Universal Works

Universal Works is another UK brand that offers a range of clothing for men. The brand takes inspiration from workwear and traditional clothing, but puts a modern twist on each piece. Universal Works uses sustainable materials and ethical production methods, and offers a range of sizes to ensure that every man can find something that fits well.

I've been a fan of Universal Works for years, and their pieces always stand the test of time. The brand's jackets and trousers are particularly impressive, and I always receive compliments when I wear them.

3. Percival

Percival is a London-based brand that offers unique and quirky clothing for men. The brand's pieces often feature bold prints and colours, and are designed to make a statement. Percival uses sustainable materials and ethical production methods, and offers a range of sizes to ensure that everyone can find something they love.

I was initially drawn to Percival because of their fun and playful designs, but I was impressed by the quality of their clothing as well. The brand's t-shirts and sweatshirts are particularly comfortable and well-made.

4. Alk Phenix

Alk Phenix is a newer brand that offers performance-focused clothing for men. The brand's pieces are designed to be both stylish and functional, and are made with high-quality materials like merino wool and Gore-Tex. Alk Phenix offers a range of sizes and styles, from technical jackets to everyday basics.

As someone who loves spending time outdoors, Alk Phenix's clothing is a game-changer. The brand's jackets and trousers are perfect for hiking and camping, but they still look great in everyday settings.

Question and Answer

Q: Are small clothing brands more expensive than high street stores?

A: Not necessarily. While some small clothing brands may have higher price points, many offer affordable options that are comparable to high street stores. Plus, you can feel good about supporting a small business and investing in high-quality clothing that will last longer than fast fashion.

Q: How can I ensure that a small clothing brand is ethical?

A: Look for brands that are transparent about their production methods and materials. Many small clothing brands will have information on their website about where their clothing is made and what materials they use. You can also look for certifications like Fairtrade or GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) that indicate ethical and sustainable practices.

Q: Do small clothing brands offer a good range of sizes?

A: It depends on the brand, but many small clothing brands offer a wider range of sizes than high street stores. Some brands even offer made-to-measure options to ensure that their clothing fits perfectly.

Q: Can I find unique clothing from small brands?

A: Absolutely! Small clothing brands often offer pieces that you won't find anywhere else. Whether you're looking for a statement jacket or a printed shirt, you're sure to find something unique from a small brand.

Conclusion of Small Clothing Brands for Men in the UK

Small clothing brands offer a unique and stylish alternative to high street fashion. Whether you're in need of basics or statement pieces, there are plenty of brands in the UK that offer high-quality, ethical clothing for men. By supporting small businesses, you can invest in clothing that will last longer and feel good about your impact on the environment and society.
