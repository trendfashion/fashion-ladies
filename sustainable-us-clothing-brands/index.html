10 Sustainable Us Clothing Brands You Need To Know About

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of shopping from fast fashion brands that harm the environment and exploit workers? Do you want to switch to sustainable clothing but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this post, we will introduce you to 10 sustainable US clothing brands that are ethical, eco-friendly, and stylish.

Sustainable US clothing brands are those who prioritize the health of the planet and its people over profit. They use organic or recycled materials, practice fair labor, and minimize waste in the production process. Their mission is to create fashion that doesn't harm the environment or exploit workers, and they are committed to transparency and accountability.

In this post, we will introduce you to 10 sustainable US clothing brands that are making a difference in the fashion industry. We will discuss their mission, values, and practices, and provide you with some examples of their products. By the end of this post, you will have a better understanding of sustainable fashion and some great options for ethical and eco-friendly clothing.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Patagonia

Patagonia is a well-known brand that has been leading the way in sustainable fashion for decades. Their mission is to "build the best product, cause no unnecessary harm, use business to inspire and implement solutions to the environmental crisis." They use organic cotton, recycled materials, and innovative technologies to create high-quality outdoor clothing that is both functional and stylish.

Personally, I have been a fan of Patagonia for years. I own a few of their jackets, and they are durable, comfortable, and warm. I also appreciate their commitment to transparency and activism. They not only create sustainable products but also advocate for environmental protection and social justice.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Everlane

Everlane is a brand that is known for its transparency and minimalism. They offer high-quality basics such as t-shirts, jeans, and sweaters that are made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton and recycled polyester. They also provide information about the factories where their products are made and the cost breakdown of each item.

I recently purchased a t-shirt from Everlane, and I was impressed by the quality and fit. It's soft, comfortable, and versatile, and I feel good knowing that it was made ethically and sustainably.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Reformation

Reformation is a brand that combines sustainability with fashion-forward designs. They use eco-friendly fabrics such as Tencel and deadstock materials to create trendy dresses, skirts, and tops. They also have a program called "Carbon is Canceled" where they offset their carbon emissions by investing in renewable energy projects.

I have always admired Reformation's aesthetic and their commitment to sustainability. Their clothes are feminine, playful, and flattering, and they make me feel good about my choices as a consumer.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Girlfriend Collective

Girlfriend Collective is a brand that specializes in activewear made from recycled plastic bottles. They use a closed-loop production process where they transform waste into high-quality leggings, sports bras, and shorts. They also prioritize inclusivity and offer a wide range of sizes and colors.

I recently purchased a pair of leggings from Girlfriend Collective, and I was amazed by the quality and fit. They are soft, stretchy, and durable, and I love that they are made from recycled materials. I also appreciate their commitment to body positivity and diversity.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Eileen Fisher

Eileen Fisher is a brand that is known for its timeless and elegant designs. They use sustainable materials such as organic cotton, linen, and Tencel, and they prioritize fair labor and supply chain transparency. They also have a program called "Renew" where they take back and resell used Eileen Fisher clothing.

I have always admired Eileen Fisher's aesthetic and their commitment to sustainability. Their clothes are versatile, comfortable, and sophisticated, and they make me feel good about my choices as a consumer.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Pact

Pact is a brand that specializes in basics such as underwear, t-shirts, and leggings made from organic cotton. They prioritize fair labor and supply chain transparency and use eco-friendly dyes and processes. They also have a program called "Give Back" where they donate a portion of their sales to environmental and social causes.

I recently purchased a few t-shirts from Pact, and I was impressed by the softness and fit. They are comfortable, breathable, and sustainable, and I appreciate their commitment to giving back.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Amour Vert

Amour Vert is a brand that combines sustainability with French-inspired designs. They use eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton, Tencel, and modal, and they prioritize fair labor and supply chain transparency. They also have a program called "Plant a Tree" where they plant a tree for every t-shirt sold.

I love Amour Vert's aesthetic and their commitment to sustainability. Their clothes are chic, feminine, and comfortable, and I feel good knowing that they are made ethically and sustainably.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Outerknown

Outerknown is a brand that is known for its surf-inspired designs and commitment to sustainability. They use eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton, recycled polyester, and hemp, and they prioritize fair labor and supply chain transparency. They also have a program called "The Low Tide Club" where they offer discounts to customers who donate their used clothing to be recycled.

I appreciate Outerknown's laid-back aesthetic and their commitment to sustainability. Their clothes are comfortable, stylish, and eco-friendly, and I love that they encourage recycling and circular fashion.

Sustainable US Clothing Brands: Nisolo

Nisolo is a brand that specializes in ethical and sustainable shoes and accessories. They use eco-friendly materials such as vegetable-tanned leather and recycled rubber, and they prioritize fair labor and supply chain transparency. They also have a program called "Shoe Reclamation" where they repair and resell used Nisolo shoes.

I recently purchased a pair of boots from Nisolo, and I was impressed by the quality and comfort. They are stylish, durable, and sustainable, and I appreciate their commitment to circular fashion.

Question and Answer

Q: Why should I switch to sustainable US clothing brands?

A: Sustainable US clothing brands prioritize the health of the planet and its people over profit. They use eco-friendly materials, minimize waste, and practice fair labor. By switching to sustainable clothing, you can reduce your carbon footprint, support ethical and eco-friendly practices, and look stylish and comfortable.

Q: How can I find sustainable US clothing brands?

A: You can find sustainable US clothing brands by doing research online, reading reviews, and checking certifications such as GOTS, Fair Trade, and B Corp. You can also look for brands that use sustainable materials such as organic cotton, Tencel, and recycled polyester, and prioritize transparency and accountability.

Q: Are sustainable US clothing brands more expensive?

A: Sustainable US clothing brands can be more expensive than fast fashion brands, but they offer higher quality, durability, and ethics. They also tend to use eco-friendly materials that are more expensive to produce. However, some sustainable brands offer affordable options, and you can also find deals and discounts on their websites and social media.

Q: Can sustainable US clothing brands be stylish?

A: Yes! Sustainable US clothing brands offer a wide range of styles, from basics to trendy designs. They also prioritize aesthetics and comfort, and many of them collaborate with designers and artists to create unique and fashionable collections.

Conclusion of Sustainable US Clothing Brands

Sustainable US clothing brands are making a positive impact on the fashion industry and the planet. They offer ethical and eco-friendly alternatives to fast fashion, prioritize transparency and accountability, and create stylish and comfortable clothing. By supporting sustainable US clothing brands, you can make a difference as a consumer and contribute to a more sustainable and just future.
