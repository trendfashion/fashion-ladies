FabIndia is a brand that is known for its ethnic wear and handcrafted products. The brand was established in 1960 and has been a favorite among the masses ever since. FabIndia's target audience is people who appreciate traditional Indian wear and handcrafted products. The brand's unique selling proposition is its use of natural fabrics and handcrafted techniques, which gives their products a unique and authentic look.

Biba is a women's ethnic wear brand that was established in 1988. The brand's target audience is women who love to dress up in ethnic wear and want to look stylish and trendy. Zara is a men's and women's wear brand that was established in 1975. The brand's target audience is young and modern individuals who want to look stylish and fashionable. Zara's unique selling proposition is its fast fashion collection, which is trendy and affordable.
