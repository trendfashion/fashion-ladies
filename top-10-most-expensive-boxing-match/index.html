The Top 10 Most Expensive Boxing Matches Of All Time

Written by Eveline Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Boxing has always been a sport that has attracted a lot of attention, especially when it comes to high profile matches. Over the years, boxing matches have become increasingly expensive, with some of the most famous fights costing millions of dollars. In this article, we will take a look at the top 10 most expensive boxing matches of all time.

If you're a boxing fan or just someone who's interested in the sport, you might be wondering what makes these matches so expensive. Well, for starters, these fights usually involve some of the best boxers in the world, who have already made a name for themselves in the sport. Additionally, the hype and anticipation surrounding these matches often drive up ticket prices and pay-per-view costs.

Now, let's take a look at the top 10 most expensive boxing matches of all time:

Floyd Mayweather vs. Manny Pacquiao

In 2015, Floyd Mayweather and Manny Pacquiao faced off in what was dubbed "The Fight of the Century." The match was highly anticipated, with millions of people around the world tuning in to watch. The fight generated $623.5 million in revenue, making it the most expensive boxing match of all time.

Floyd Mayweather vs. Conor McGregor

Another one of Floyd Mayweather's fights makes the list. In 2017, he faced off against Conor McGregor, a popular mixed martial artist. The fight generated $600 million in revenue, making it the second most expensive boxing match of all time.

Canelo Alvarez vs. Gennady Golovkin II

In 2018, Canelo Alvarez and Gennady Golovkin faced off for the second time, after their first fight ended in a controversial draw. The rematch was highly anticipated and generated $94 million in revenue, making it the third most expensive boxing match of all time.

Oscar De La Hoya vs. Floyd Mayweather

In 2007, Oscar De La Hoya and Floyd Mayweather faced off in a highly publicized match. The fight generated $136 million in revenue, making it the fourth most expensive boxing match of all time.

Anthony Joshua vs. Wladimir Klitschko

In 2017, Anthony Joshua and Wladimir Klitschko faced off in a match that was highly anticipated by boxing fans. The fight generated $93 million in revenue, making it the fifth most expensive boxing match of all time.

Canelo Alvarez vs. Gennady Golovkin

The first fight between Canelo Alvarez and Gennady Golovkin was held in 2017 and generated $80 million in revenue, making it the sixth most expensive boxing match of all time.

Floyd Mayweather vs. Saul Alvarez

Floyd Mayweather's fight against Saul Alvarez in 2013 generated $150 million in revenue and is the seventh most expensive boxing match of all time.

Floyd Mayweather vs. Conor McGregor II

The rematch between Floyd Mayweather and Conor McGregor in 2020 generated $72 million in revenue, making it the eighth most expensive boxing match of all time.

Evander Holyfield vs. Mike Tyson II

The second fight between Evander Holyfield and Mike Tyson in 1997 generated $100 million in revenue, making it the ninth most expensive boxing match of all time.

Lennox Lewis vs. Mike Tyson

The fight between Lennox Lewis and Mike Tyson in 2002 generated $112 million in revenue, making it the tenth most expensive boxing match of all time.

Conclusion of Top 10 Most Expensive Boxing Matches

These are the top 10 most expensive boxing matches of all time, with the Mayweather-Pacquiao fight taking the top spot. These matches have not only generated millions of dollars in revenue but have also been watched by millions of people around the world.
