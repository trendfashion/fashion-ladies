Are you in the market for a new car and wondering what the top-selling cars in Canada are for 2020? Look no further! In this article, we will provide a detailed breakdown of the top 10 selling cars in Canada for 2020, along with their features and benefits.

With so many options on the market, it can be challenging to choose the right car for your needs. That's why it's essential to have an understanding of the top-selling cars in Canada to make an informed decision.

So, without further ado, let's dive into the top 10 selling cars in Canada for 2020!

Toyota RAV4

As the best-selling SUV in Canada, the Toyota RAV4 offers a spacious and comfortable interior, excellent fuel economy, and a wide range of safety features. Its hybrid version is also available for environmentally conscious drivers.

Honda Civic

The Honda Civic has been a fan favorite for decades, and it's easy to see why. With its sleek design, high safety ratings, and fuel-efficient engine, it's an excellent choice for anyone looking for a reliable and affordable car.

Toyota Corolla

Another Toyota model, the Corolla, offers a comfortable and spacious interior, fuel-efficient engine, and high safety ratings. It's an excellent option for anyone in the market for a reliable and long-lasting car.

Mazda CX-5

The Mazda CX-5 is a popular choice for those seeking a luxury SUV at an affordable price. It offers a stylish design, comfortable interior, and advanced safety features.

Hyundai Kona

The Hyundai Kona is a subcompact SUV that offers a fun and sporty driving experience. It comes with a fuel-efficient engine, spacious interior, and a variety of safety features.

Toyota Highlander

The Toyota Highlander is a midsize SUV that offers a spacious and comfortable interior, advanced safety features, and a fuel-efficient engine. It's an excellent option for anyone in the market for a family-friendly car.

Hyundai Tucson

The Hyundai Tucson is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable and spacious interior, excellent fuel economy, and advanced safety features. It's an excellent option for anyone looking for a reliable and affordable car.

Mazda3

The Mazda3 is a compact car that offers a sleek and stylish design, fuel-efficient engine, and advanced safety features. It's an excellent option for anyone in the market for a reliable and affordable car.

Toyota Camry

The Toyota Camry is a midsize sedan that offers a comfortable and spacious interior, fuel-efficient engine, and advanced safety features. It's an excellent option for anyone in the market for a family-friendly car.

Honda CR-V

The Honda CR-V is a compact SUV that offers a spacious and comfortable interior, fuel-efficient engine, and advanced safety features. Its hybrid version is also available for environmentally conscious drivers.
