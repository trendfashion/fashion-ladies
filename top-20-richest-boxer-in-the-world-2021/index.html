When it comes to making the most money in boxing, Floyd Mayweather Jr. is at the top of the list with a net worth of $450 million. He's followed by other boxing legends like Manny Pacquiao, Oscar De La Hoya, and Lennox Lewis, who have all earned over $100 million throughout their careers.

One of the boxers who has managed to earn a lot of money in a short period is Anthony Joshua. He's currently one of the most popular boxers in the world and has a net worth of over $100 million. The Legacy of Muhammad Ali

Muhammad Ali is one of the most iconic boxers of all time, and he's also one of the richest. He had a net worth of over $80 million at the time of his death in 2016. Ali was a three-time heavyweight champion and won numerous other titles throughout his career. He was also known for his activism and his outspokenness about social issues.

How Do Boxers Make Money?

Boxers make money from various sources, including fight purses, sponsorships, and endorsements. Boxers also earn money from sponsorships and endorsements with companies like Nike, Adidas, and Under Armour.
