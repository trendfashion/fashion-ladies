Top Brands For Plus Size A Guide To Finding The Perfect Fit

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you struggle to find fashionable and comfortable clothing that fits your plus-size body? Are you tired of feeling left out of the fashion world because of your size? You're not alone. Many women face the same struggle, but fortunately, there are top brands that cater to plus-size women. In this guide, we'll explore the best brands for plus size and help you find the perfect fit for your body.

If you're a plus-size woman, you know that shopping for clothes can be a frustrating experience. Many brands don't offer sizes that cater to your body type, leaving you with limited options. This can make you feel like your body isn't valued or appreciated. But with the rise of body positivity movements, more brands are stepping up to create fashionable and comfortable clothing for plus-size women.

So, where can you find the best brands for plus size? There are a few top brands that stand out for their dedication to designing clothing that fits and flatters plus-size bodies. These brands offer a range of sizes, styles, and price points to suit every woman's taste and budget. Let's dive into the top brands for plus size and what makes them so great.

Target's Ava & Viv

Target's Ava & Viv line is a great option for plus-size women who want affordable and trendy clothing. The line offers sizes ranging from 1X to 4X, and the clothing is designed to fit and flatter curves. Whether you're looking for casual wear or something more formal, Ava & Viv has a variety of options to choose from. Personally, I have found many great pieces from this line that are comfortable and stylish.

Torrid

Torrid is a brand that exclusively caters to plus-size women, offering sizes ranging from 10 to 30. The brand is known for its trendy and edgy clothing, with a focus on body positivity and inclusivity. Torrid offers a wide range of styles, from casual wear to evening wear, and even has a swimwear line. Their clothes are designed to fit and flatter curvy bodies, making you look and feel confident. I have found many great pieces from Torrid that fit me perfectly and make me feel amazing.

Universal Standard

Universal Standard is a brand that's all about inclusivity, offering sizes ranging from 00 to 40. The brand's mission is to create clothing that fits and flatters every body type, and they deliver on that promise. Universal Standard offers high-quality basics, as well as on-trend pieces that are designed to last. They focus on using premium materials and innovative designs that make their clothing comfortable and stylish. I have found many great pieces from Universal Standard that fit me perfectly and are versatile enough to wear for any occasion.

Lane Bryant

Lane Bryant is a brand that's been around for over 100 years, and they've been catering to plus-size women since the 1920s. The brand offers sizes ranging from 12 to 28, with a focus on creating clothing that fits and flatters curves. Lane Bryant offers a range of styles, from casual wear to workwear, and even has a line of lingerie. Their clothing is designed to make you feel confident and comfortable, whether you're running errands or attending a formal event. I have found many great pieces from Lane Bryant that fit me perfectly and make me feel amazing.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these brands expensive?

A: These brands offer a range of prices, from affordable to high-end. It really depends on your budget and what you're looking for. Target's Ava & Viv line is very affordable, while Universal Standard tends to be on the higher end.

Q: Can I find these brands in stores?

A: Yes, most of these brands have brick-and-mortar stores, as well as online stores. Check their websites to find a store near you.

Q: Do these brands offer a range of sizes?

A: Yes, these brands offer a range of sizes to cater to every woman's body type. Be sure to check their size charts before making a purchase to ensure you're getting the right fit.

Q: Can I return items if they don't fit?

A: Yes, all of these brands have a return policy that allows you to return items if they don't fit. Be sure to check their policies before making a purchase.

Conclusion of Top Brands for Plus Size

Finding clothing that fits and flatters your plus-size body can be a challenge, but with the rise of body positivity movements, more brands are stepping up to create fashionable and comfortable clothing for plus-size women. Target's Ava & Viv, Torrid, Universal Standard, and Lane Bryant are all top brands that cater to plus-size women, offering a range of sizes, styles, and price points. By shopping at these brands, you can find clothing that makes you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.
