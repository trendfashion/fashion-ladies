Top Footwear Stocks in India: Investing in the Right Pair of Shoes

Are you looking to invest in the Indian footwear industry? With the growing demand for comfortable and stylish shoes, the footwear market in India is expected to reach a value of USD 15.5 billion by 2024. However, with so many options available, it can be difficult to choose the right pair of stocks to invest in. In this post, we will explore the top footwear stocks in India and their potential for growth.

Before we dive into the stocks, let's understand the target market for the Indian footwear industry. With a population of over 1.3 billion, India has a diverse range of consumers with varying purchasing power. The demand for footwear is highest among the middle-class and upper-middle-class population, which accounts for 30-35% of the total market share. Additionally, the rise of e-commerce platforms has made it easier for consumers to purchase shoes online, leading to a surge in online sales for footwear brands.

To invest in the right footwear stocks, you need to consider the financial performance of the company, market trends, and consumer demand. Here are some of the top footwear stocks in India that you should keep an eye on:

Bata India Ltd.

Bata India Ltd. is one of the oldest and most trusted footwear brands in India. The company has over 1,400 stores in India and a strong online presence. Bata India Ltd. reported a revenue of INR 2,892 crore in 2020 and a net profit of INR 211 crore. The company has been investing in product innovation and expanding its retail footprint to capture a larger market share.

Relaxo Footwears Ltd.

Relaxo Footwears Ltd. is a popular footwear brand known for its comfortable and affordable shoes. The company has a strong presence in the northern and eastern regions of India and has been expanding its reach in other parts of the country. Relaxo Footwears Ltd. reported a revenue of INR 2,486 crore in 2020 and a net profit of INR 226 crore. The company has been investing in marketing campaigns to increase brand awareness and drive sales.

Metro Brands Ltd.

Metro Brands Ltd. is a leading retailer of premium footwear brands in India. The company has a portfolio of international brands like Crocs, Florsheim, and Red Tape, and has a strong presence in metro cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore. Metro Brands Ltd. reported a revenue of INR 1,063 crore in 2020 and a net profit of INR 69 crore. The company has been focusing on expanding its retail footprint and increasing its online sales.

How to Invest in Footwear Stocks in India

Investing in footwear stocks in India can be done through a demat account with a brokerage firm. You can choose to invest in individual stocks or invest in a mutual fund that has exposure to the Indian footwear industry. Before investing, it is important to do your research and understand the financial performance of the company, market trends, and consumer demand. It is also recommended to diversify your portfolio to mitigate risk.

Conclusion of Top Footwear Stocks in India

The Indian footwear industry is poised for growth, and investing in the right stocks can lead to significant returns. Bata India Ltd., Relaxo Footwears Ltd., and Metro Brands Ltd. are some of the top footwear stocks in India that have a strong track record of financial performance and are well-positioned to capture the growing demand for footwear in India. Remember to do your research and diversify your portfolio to make informed investment decisions.
