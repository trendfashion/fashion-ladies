Top Luxury Jewellery Brands In India A Guide To The Best

Written by Aprilia Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to luxury jewellery, India is a country that boasts of some of the finest brands in the world. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we will take a look at the top luxury jewellery brands in India and what makes them stand out.

Are you looking for a luxurious piece of jewellery that reflects your style and sophistication? Do you want to invest in a brand that offers the best craftsmanship, quality, and design? If yes, then this guide is for you.

In this article, we will discuss the top luxury jewellery brands in India, their history, and what sets them apart from the competition. We will also explore the latest trends and styles in luxury jewellery and what you can expect from these brands.

Tanishq

Tanishq is one of the most popular jewellery brands in India, known for its exquisite designs and quality craftsmanship. The brand was founded in 1994 and has since then become a household name in India. Tanishq offers a wide range of jewellery collections, including Kundan, Polki, and Diamond. The brand is also known for its innovative designs and collaborations with renowned designers.

My personal experience with Tanishq has been nothing short of exceptional. I recently purchased a diamond necklace from their Rivaah collection, and the attention to detail and quality of the piece were outstanding. The design was contemporary yet timeless, and the diamonds were of the highest quality.

Amrapali

Amrapali is another luxury jewellery brand in India that has gained a lot of popularity in recent years. The brand was founded in 1978 and is known for its traditional and ethnic jewellery designs. Amrapali's collections are inspired by Indian mythology, heritage, and culture. The brand uses a variety of precious stones and metals, including diamonds, gold, and silver, to create stunning pieces of jewellery.

My experience with Amrapali has been wonderful. I purchased a pair of earrings from their Sari collection, and the intricate design and attention to detail were impressive. The earrings were lightweight, comfortable to wear, and complemented my outfit perfectly.

Sabyasachi

Sabyasachi is a luxury designer brand in India that is known for its bridal couture and jewellery. The brand was founded in 1999 and has since then become one of the most sought-after brands in India. Sabyasachi's jewellery collections are inspired by Indian heritage and culture and are known for their intricate designs and use of precious stones and metals.

My personal experience with Sabyasachi has been limited to admiring their jewellery from afar. However, I have seen some of their exquisite pieces on brides and celebrities, and they are truly breathtaking. The brand's attention to detail and craftsmanship are second to none.

Tips for Choosing the Right Luxury Jewellery Brand

When it comes to choosing the right luxury jewellery brand, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Research the brand's history and reputation
  • Check the quality of the materials used
  • Look for unique and innovative designs
  • Consider your personal style and preferences
  • Check the brand's customer service and return policy

Conclusion of Top Luxury Jewellery Brands in India

India is home to some of the finest luxury jewellery brands in the world, and choosing the right one can be a daunting task. However, with our guide to the top luxury jewellery brands in India, you can make an informed decision and invest in a brand that reflects your style and sophistication.

From Tanishq's contemporary designs to Amrapali's traditional collections and Sabyasachi's bridal jewellery, there is something for everyone. So go ahead and indulge in a luxurious piece of jewellery that will be cherished for years to come.
