Top Ten Expensive Jeans Brands In India

Written by Oliver Aug 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Jeans are a staple in every wardrobe, but some brands go a step further by creating high-end, luxury denim that can come with a hefty price tag. In India, there are several brands that cater to the demand for premium jeans, but the top ten expensive jeans brands stand out for their quality, craftsmanship, and design.

When it comes to purchasing high-end jeans, some of the pain points include finding the right fit, ensuring that the quality justifies the price, and deciding which brand to invest in. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one.

This post aims to answer the target of showcasing the top ten expensive jeans brands in India, their unique features, and what makes them worth the investment.

In summary, this article will provide an overview of the top ten expensive jeans brands in India, their price range, and the factors that make them stand out in the market.

1. Diesel

Diesel is a premium Italian brand that has made a name for itself in the Indian market for its high-quality denim. The brand's jeans are known for their innovative designs, unique washes, and attention to detail. Diesel's jeans range from INR 10,000 to INR 30,000, depending on the style and design.

Personally, I have owned a pair of Diesel jeans for over five years, and they still look as good as new. The quality of the denim and the stitching is exceptional, and the fit is perfect for my body type.

2. True Religion

True Religion is an American brand that has gained a cult following for its iconic horseshoe logo and signature stitching. The brand's jeans are made from premium denim and are known for their comfortable fit and unique designs. True Religion's jeans range from INR 15,000 to INR 35,000.

I have always been a fan of True Religion's jeans because of their flattering fit and attention to detail. The brand's signature stitching adds a unique touch to each pair of jeans, making them stand out from other brands.
