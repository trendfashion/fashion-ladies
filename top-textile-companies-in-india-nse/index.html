India is one of the largest textile producers and exporters in the world, and its textile industry is a significant contributor to its economy. Reliance Industries Limited

Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) is a diversified conglomerate with interests in textiles, petrochemicals, refining, and more. Its textile division is one of the largest in India, with a wide range of products, including polyester, nylon, and viscose. RIL's textile business has a strong market presence, with a focus on high-quality products and innovative solutions.

As of September 2021, RIL's market capitalization was over INR 15 trillion, making it one of the most valuable companies in India. Its revenue in FY2021 was over INR 404 billion, and its net profit was INR 53 billion.

Welspun India Limited

Welspun India Limited is a leading textile company in India, specializing in home textiles, such as bed and bath linens. Its products are sold in over 50 countries, and it is a preferred supplier for major retailers in the US, Europe, and Australia. Welspun India's commitment to sustainability and innovation has earned it several awards and recognition.

In FY2021, Welspun India's revenue was over INR 66 billion, and its net profit was INR 4.5 billion.

Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Limited

Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Limited (ABFRL) is a leading fashion and lifestyle company in India, with a portfolio of brands, such as Louis Philippe, Van Heusen, and Allen Solly. Its textile division produces fabrics for both domestic and international markets, with a focus on sustainability and quality.

In FY2021, ABFRL's revenue was over INR 70 billion, and its net profit was INR 3.3 billion.
