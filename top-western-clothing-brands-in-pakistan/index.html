Zara

Zara is a well-known Spanish brand that is popular all over the world. They offer a wide range of western clothing that is both trendy and affordable. Their target audience is young adults who are looking for fashionable outfits at a reasonable price. Zara's price range varies, but most of their outfits fall in the mid-price range.

Personally, I love Zara's clothing because they are always up-to-date with the latest trends. Their outfits are also very comfortable to wear, and the quality is excellent. Whether you are looking for casual wear or a dressy outfit, Zara has got you covered.

Mango

Mango is another Spanish brand that is popular in Pakistan. They offer a variety of western clothing that is both elegant and stylish. Their target audience is women who are looking for high-quality outfits at an affordable price. Mango's price range is similar to Zara's, falling in the mid-price range.

I have personally purchased several outfits from Mango, and I have always been impressed with their quality. Their clothing is also very versatile, which means you can dress it up or down depending on the occasion. If you are looking for timeless and elegant western wear, Mango is the brand for you.

Levi's

Levi's is an American brand that is known for its high-quality denim jeans. They offer a variety of western clothing, but their denim jeans are what they are most famous for. Their target audience is both men and women who are looking for high-quality denim at an affordable price. Levi's price range varies, but their denim jeans fall in the mid to high price range.

Personally, I love Levi's denim jeans because they are very comfortable to wear and last for a long time. Their jeans are also very versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. If you are looking for high-quality denim jeans, Levi's is the brand for you.

Calvin Klein

Calvin Klein is an American brand that is known for its luxury clothing and accessories. They offer a variety of western clothing that is both elegant and sophisticated. Their target audience is both men and women who are looking for high-quality outfits at a premium price. Calvin Klein's price range is high, but their quality justifies the price.
