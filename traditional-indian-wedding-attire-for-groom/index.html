Traditional Indian Wedding Attire For Groom A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel May 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and extravagance. From the food to the decorations, everything is larger than life. One of the most important aspects of an Indian wedding is the attire of the bride and groom. While the bride's attire steals the show, the groom's attire is equally important. Traditional Indian wedding attire for groom can be overwhelming, and many grooms struggle to find the perfect outfit. In this post, we will guide you through everything you need to know about traditional Indian wedding attire for groom.

When it comes to traditional Indian wedding attire for groom, the aim is to look regal and elegant. The attire should reflect the traditions and culture of India. The groom's attire should complement the bride's attire and should be in sync with the overall theme of the wedding. It is crucial to choose the right attire that suits your personality and body type.

In this post, we will cover the different types of traditional Indian wedding attire for groom, the accessories that go with them, and some tips on how to choose the perfect attire. We will also answer some common questions related to traditional Indian wedding attire for groom.

Sherwani – An Elegant Choice

Sherwani is a popular choice for grooms at Indian weddings. It is a long coat-like garment that is buttoned up to the collar and extends below the knee. Sherwani is usually made of silk or brocade and is heavily embroidered with intricate designs. The embroidery can be in gold, silver or any other color that complements the garment. Sherwani is usually paired with churidar pants or dhoti pants.

I wore a beautiful sherwani on my wedding day. It was made of silk and had intricate gold embroidery. I paired it with a turban and a matching dupatta. The sherwani was comfortable and allowed me to move around freely. I felt like a king in it!

Jodhpuri Suit – A Modern Twist

Jodhpuri suit is a modern take on traditional Indian wedding attire for groom. It is a fusion of Indian and Western styles and is perfect for grooms who want to add a contemporary touch to their attire. Jodhpuri suit consists of a jacket and trousers. The jacket is usually made of silk or velvet and has a Nehru collar. The trousers are either churidar pants or formal trousers. Jodhpuri suit is usually paired with a turban or a safa.

My brother wore a Jodhpuri suit on his wedding day. It was a beautiful navy blue suit with gold embroidery. He paired it with a turban and looked like a modern-day prince. The Jodhpuri suit was a perfect choice for his outdoor wedding.

Achkan – A Classic Choice

Achkan is a classic choice for traditional Indian wedding attire for groom. It is a knee-length coat that is usually made of silk or brocade. Achkan is heavily embroidered with intricate designs and is paired with churidar pants. Achkan is a versatile garment that can be worn at formal as well as semi-formal events.

My father wore an Achkan on my wedding day. It was a beautiful ivory-colored achkan with gold embroidery. He paired it with a turban and looked like a true gentleman. The achkan was comfortable and allowed him to move around freely.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Traditional Indian Wedding Attire for Groom

Here are some tips for choosing the perfect traditional Indian wedding attire for groom:

  • Choose a garment that suits your body type.
  • Choose a garment that complements the bride's attire.
  • Choose a garment that reflects your personality.
  • Choose a garment that is comfortable and allows you to move around freely.
  • Choose a garment that is in sync with the overall theme of the wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: What accessories should I wear with traditional Indian wedding attire for groom?

A: You can wear a turban or a safa with your attire. You can also wear a necklace, a bracelet, and a watch.

Q: Can I wear a western suit at an Indian wedding?

A: While it is not traditional, you can wear a western suit at an Indian wedding. However, it is always better to opt for traditional Indian wedding attire for groom.

Q: Can I wear a sherwani at a beach wedding?

A: A sherwani may not be the best choice for a beach wedding. You can opt for a Jodhpuri suit or a linen suit instead.

Q: Can I wear a turban with a Jodhpuri suit?

A: Yes, you can wear a turban or a safa with a Jodhpuri suit.

Conclusion of Traditional Indian Wedding Attire for Groom

Traditional Indian wedding attire for groom is all about looking regal and elegant. It is important to choose the right attire that suits your personality and body type. Sherwani, Jodhpuri suit, and Achkan are some popular choices for traditional Indian wedding attire for groom. When choosing the perfect attire, keep in mind the overall theme of the wedding and the bride's attire. With these tips in mind, you will be able to choose the perfect attire for your big day.
