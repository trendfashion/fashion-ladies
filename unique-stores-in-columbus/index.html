Columbus is a city full of unique stores that offer an unforgettable shopping experience. From vintage clothing shops to artisanal bakeries, there is something for everyone in this vibrant city.

Are you tired of the same old shopping malls and big box stores? Do you crave a more personalized shopping experience where you can find one-of-a-kind items? Look no further than Columbus!

If you are looking for unique stores in Columbus, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will explore some of the best and most interesting stores that Columbus has to offer.

From vintage shops to record stores, Columbus has a wide variety of unique stores to choose from. Whether you are a collector, a music lover, or simply looking for something different, you are sure to find what you are looking for in one of these unique stores.

1. Wholly Craft

Wholly Craft is a unique store in Columbus that specializes in handmade goods. The store features items from over 200 different artists and makers, including jewelry, clothing, and home decor. I visited Wholly Craft last month and was blown away by the quality and creativity of the items for sale. I ended up buying a beautiful handmade necklace that I wear almost every day.

2. Records Per Minute

If you are a music lover, you have to check out Records Per Minute. This unique store in Columbus specializes in vinyl records and has a huge selection of new and used LPs. The store also features live music performances, so you can listen to your favorite bands while you shop. I love coming to Records Per Minute to discover new music and add to my record collection.

3. The Book Loft

The Book Loft is a unique store in Columbus that is a must-visit for book lovers. The store is located in a historic building that used to be a saloon and has 32 rooms filled with books. You can easily spend hours browsing the shelves and discovering new titles. The Book Loft also features a cozy cafe where you can grab a coffee and a pastry while you read.

4. Stauf's Coffee Roasters

Stauf's Coffee Roasters is a unique store in Columbus that is perfect for coffee lovers. The store features a wide variety of coffee beans from around the world, and you can even watch the beans being roasted in-house. Stauf's also has a cozy cafe where you can enjoy a delicious cup of coffee while you work or catch up with friends.

Question and Answer

Q: What makes Columbus's unique stores different from other cities?
A: Columbus's unique stores offer a personalized shopping experience and often feature items that you won't find anywhere else. Many of these stores also support local artists and makers, so you can feel good about supporting the local economy when you shop.

Q: Are there any unique stores in Columbus that specialize in food?
A: Yes! Columbus has a wide variety of unique stores that specialize in food, including artisanal bakeries, specialty cheese shops, and gourmet food stores. Be sure to check out North Market, which features over 30 local vendors selling everything from fresh produce to handmade pasta.

Q: What is the best way to discover new unique stores in Columbus?
A: One of the best ways to discover new unique stores in Columbus is to simply explore different neighborhoods and shopping districts. Some of the best areas for unique stores include the Short North, German Village, and Grandview Heights. You can also check out local publications like Columbus Monthly and Columbus Alive for recommendations.

Q: Are there any unique stores in Columbus that are family-friendly?
A: Yes! Columbus has plenty of unique stores that are family-friendly, including toy stores, candy shops, and children's clothing stores. Be sure to check out Naturally Curious Kids, which specializes in eco-friendly and educational toys for children.

Conclusion of Unique Stores in Columbus

If you are looking for a unique shopping experience, be sure to check out some of the amazing stores in Columbus. From handmade goods to vinyl records to gourmet food, there is something for everyone in this vibrant city. So why settle for boring chain stores when you can discover something truly special in Columbus?
