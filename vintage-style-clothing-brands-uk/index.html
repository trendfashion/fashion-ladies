Discover The Best Vintage Style Clothing Brands Uk

Written by Robby Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fan of vintage style clothing? Do you struggle to find the perfect vintage pieces that suit your taste and style? Look no further, as we have listed the best vintage style clothing brands in the UK that cater to all your vintage fashion needs.

Many people find it difficult to find vintage clothing that fits well and suits their personal style. It can be a daunting task to scour through racks of secondhand clothes or spend hours searching online. But with the rise of vintage style clothing brands, it has become much easier to find unique and stylish vintage-inspired pieces that fit perfectly.

If you are looking for vintage style clothing brands in the UK, we have got you covered. Whether you are into 1920s flapper dresses or 1970s boho chic, there is a vintage style brand for everyone.

Let's take a look at some of the best vintage style clothing brands in the UK that you should know about.

Vivien of Holloway

When it comes to vintage-inspired clothing, Vivien of Holloway is a name that comes to mind. This brand is known for its 1940s and 1950s style dresses and separates that flatter all body types. Their dresses are made from high-quality fabrics and come in a range of sizes to ensure the perfect fit.

I personally love their halterneck dresses that come in a variety of fun prints and colors. They are perfect for summer parties or a day out at the beach.

Beyond Retro

Beyond Retro is a vintage clothing brand that offers a wide range of vintage clothing from different eras. From 1920s flapper dresses to 1980s power suits, they have it all. They also have a sustainable fashion line called Beyond Retro Label that uses recycled fabrics to create unique pieces.

I recently purchased a 1970s boho dress from Beyond Retro and it has quickly become one of my favorite pieces in my wardrobe. The quality of the fabric is amazing and it fits like a glove.

Rokit

Rokit is another vintage clothing brand that has been around for over 30 years. They offer a wide range of vintage clothing and accessories from the 1920s to the 1990s. They also have their own line of vintage-inspired clothing that is made from sustainable fabrics.

One of my favorite pieces from Rokit is a 1980s oversized denim jacket that I found in one of their stores. It's the perfect statement piece and I always receive compliments whenever I wear it.

The House of Foxy

If you are looking for vintage-inspired clothing with a modern twist, The House of Foxy is the brand for you. They specialize in 1930s and 1940s style clothing that is made in the UK using traditional techniques. Their pieces are perfect for vintage enthusiasts who want to incorporate vintage elements into their everyday wardrobe.

I recently purchased a 1930s style blouse from The House of Foxy and I love how versatile it is. It can be dressed up with a pencil skirt or dressed down with jeans and sneakers.

Question and Answer

Q: Are vintage style clothing brands expensive?

A: It depends on the brand and the quality of the clothing. Some vintage style brands can be expensive, but there are also affordable options available.

Q: Can I find vintage style clothing in high street stores?

A: Yes, many high street stores offer vintage-inspired clothing. However, if you want authentic vintage pieces, it's best to check out vintage stores or online vintage retailers.

Q: Is vintage clothing sustainable?

A: Yes, vintage clothing is a sustainable fashion choice as it reduces the amount of clothing that ends up in landfills. It also reduces the demand for new clothing production which has a negative impact on the environment.

Q: How do I care for vintage clothing?

A: Vintage clothing requires special care as the fabrics may be delicate. It's best to hand wash or dry clean vintage clothing and avoid using harsh chemicals or bleach.

Conclusion of Vintage Style Clothing Brands UK

There are plenty of vintage style clothing brands in the UK that offer unique and stylish pieces for vintage enthusiasts. Whether you are into 1920s flapper dresses or 1970s boho chic, there is a vintage style brand for everyone. So, go ahead and explore these brands to add some vintage charm to your wardrobe.
