Why are wetsuit prices in South Africa so expensive?

As someone who loves spending time in the water, I've had my fair share of wetsuit shopping experiences. One thing I've noticed is that wetsuit prices in South Africa can be quite expensive compared to other parts of the world. Where can I find affordable wetsuits in South Africa?

If you're looking for an affordable wetsuit in South Africa, there are a few different options to consider. Firstly, you can try shopping online. What should I consider when buying a wetsuit?

When buying a wetsuit, there are several factors to consider. Firstly, you'll want to think about the thickness of the wetsuit. The thickness of a wetsuit determines how warm it will keep you in the water. Thicker wetsuits are better for colder water temperatures, while thinner wetsuits are better for warmer water. You'll also want to consider the fit of the wetsuit. A wetsuit that is too loose or too tight can be uncomfortable and affect your performance in the water.

How can I make my wetsuit last longer?

To make your wetsuit last longer, there are a few things you can do. Firstly, always rinse your wetsuit with fresh water after each use. This helps to remove any salt or sand that could damage the material. You should also avoid leaving your wetsuit in direct sunlight or hot temperatures, as this can cause the material to break down over time. Finally, be sure to store your wetsuit properly when not in use, hanging it up on a padded hanger or folding it neatly in a drawer.

Where can I find the best deals on wetsuits in South Africa?

As someone who has spent many hours browsing wetsuits in South Africa, I've found that the best deals can often be found at end-of-season sales. Many surf shops and online retailers offer discounts on wetsuits at the end of summer, making it a great time to invest in a new suit. You can also keep an eye out for promo codes and coupons from online retailers, which can help you save even more on your purchase.
