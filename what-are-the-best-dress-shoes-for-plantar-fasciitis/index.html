Plantar fasciitis is a condition that causes pain and inflammation in the heel of your foot. This can make it difficult to walk, stand or even wear shoes. It is often caused by overuse, poor footwear, or an injury to the foot. If you suffer from plantar fasciitis, you know how important it is to find shoes that provide adequate support and cushioning for your feet.

The best dress shoes for plantar fasciitis are those that provide a combination of support, cushioning, and flexibility. They should have a firm heel counter, good arch support, and a cushioned footbed. They should also have a flexible sole that allows your foot to move naturally.
