So, what brands are Australian made? Here are some of our top picks:

1. Ugg Australia

As the name suggests, Ugg Australia is a brand that specialises in Australian made ugg boots. The company was founded in 1978 and has been producing high-quality ugg boots ever since. The boots are made from premium Australian sheepskin and are available in a range of colours and styles. Plus, they're made in Australia, so you can be sure that you're getting an authentic Australian product.

2. Akubra

Akubra is an iconic Australian brand that has been producing hats since 1874. The company is known for its high-quality hats, which are made from pure rabbit fur felt. The hats are available in a range of styles and colours, and are made in Australia. If you're looking for an authentic Australian hat, then Akubra is the way to go.

3. R.M. Williams

R.M. Williams is another iconic Australian brand that has been around since the 1930s. The company is known for its high-quality leather boots, which are made in Australia. The boots are made from premium leather and are available in a range of styles and colours. Plus, they're built to last, so you can be sure that you're investing in a quality product.

4. Breville

Breville is an Australian brand that produces high-quality kitchen appliances. The company was founded in Sydney in 1932 and has been producing innovative products ever since. Breville products are designed and engineered in Australia, so you know that you're getting a product that's been made with Australian know-how.

5. Bonds

Bonds is an Australian clothing brand that produces a range of clothing and underwear. The company was founded in 1915 and has been producing quality products ever since. Bonds products are made in Australia, so you can be sure that you're getting a product that's been made with Australian expertise.

These are just a few of the many Australian made brands out there. By choosing to support these brands, you're not only investing in quality products but also supporting local Australian businesses.

There are many reasons why you should consider buying Australian made products. Firstly, by buying Australian made, you're supporting local businesses and helping to create jobs in Australia.
