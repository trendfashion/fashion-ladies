What Colors Can You Wear To A Spring Wedding

Written by Christine May 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Spring weddings are a beautiful celebration of love, but choosing an outfit can be stressful. You want to look your best, but you also don't want to upstage the bride or groom. Plus, you need to know what colors are appropriate for the season and the occasion.

The right color can make you feel confident and comfortable, while the wrong one can make you feel out of place. You don't want to be that guest who shows up in all black to a spring wedding. So, what colors can you wear to a spring wedding?

When it comes to spring wedding colors, pastels are always a safe bet. Think light pinks, blues, and yellows. You can also opt for floral prints or bright, bold colors like coral or turquoise. However, it's important to consider the dress code and location of the wedding. A garden wedding may call for lighter colors and flowy dresses, while a formal ballroom wedding may require more elegant attire.

In summary, when choosing an outfit for a spring wedding, stick to pastels, bright colors, and floral prints. Consider the dress code and location of the wedding to ensure your outfit is appropriate. Now, let's dive deeper into what colors can you wear to a spring wedding.

Pastels

When it comes to spring weddings, pastels are a classic choice. They're feminine, elegant, and perfect for the season. Think soft pinks, blues, yellows, and greens. These colors are light and airy, which is perfect for a spring wedding. You can choose a pastel dress or incorporate pastel colors into your outfit with accessories like shoes, jewelry, or a clutch.

Personally, I attended a spring wedding wearing a light pink dress with nude heels and a matching clutch. I felt comfortable and confident, and it was appropriate for the occasion. The bride and groom had a garden wedding, and my pastel outfit fit right in with the spring blooms.

Bright Colors

Bright colors are a fun and bold choice for a spring wedding. If you're not a fan of pastels, you can opt for brighter shades like coral, turquoise, or fuchsia. These colors are eye-catching and perfect for a daytime wedding. Just make sure to balance out the brightness with neutral accessories like nude shoes and a clutch.

For example, I attended a spring wedding where the bride requested guests to wear bright colors. I chose a turquoise dress with lace detailing and paired it with nude heels and a clutch. I received many compliments on my outfit, and it was appropriate for the occasion.

Prints

Prints are a great way to add some personality to your outfit while still looking appropriate for a spring wedding. Floral prints are a classic choice for the season, but you can also opt for stripes, polka dots, or abstract patterns. Just make sure the print isn't too busy or overwhelming, so it doesn't upstage the bride.

For instance, I attended a spring wedding where the bride requested guests to wear floral prints. I chose a light blue dress with a small floral print and paired it with nude heels and a clutch. The print added some interest to my outfit, but it wasn't too overpowering.

Accessories

Accessories can make or break your spring wedding outfit. You don't want to wear a beautiful dress with clunky shoes or a mismatched clutch. Stick to neutral accessories like nude or metallic shoes, a small clutch or handbag, and simple jewelry. If you want to add some interest to your outfit, you can opt for a statement earring or bracelet.

For example, I attended a spring wedding where I wore a simple pastel dress with nude heels and a matching clutch. I added a statement earring that had a small floral design, which tied in with the spring theme of the wedding. The earring added some interest to my outfit without being too overpowering.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to a spring wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing black to a spring wedding, as it can be seen as too somber. Stick to lighter colors like pastels, bright colors, or prints.

Q: Can I wear white to a spring wedding?

A: It's not recommended to wear white to a wedding, as it can be seen as trying to upstage the bride. Stick to other colors like pastels, bright colors, or prints.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a spring wedding?

A: No, jeans are not appropriate for a spring wedding. Stick to dresses, skirts, or dress pants.

Q: Can I wear sneakers to a spring wedding?

A: No, sneakers are not appropriate for a spring wedding. Stick to dress shoes or sandals.

Conclusion of What Colors Can You Wear to a Spring Wedding

When it comes to choosing an outfit for a spring wedding, stick to pastels, bright colors, and floral prints. Consider the dress code and location of the wedding to ensure your outfit is appropriate. Accessorize with neutral accessories and avoid wearing black or white. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to find the perfect outfit for a spring wedding.
