What Do Brides Wear In India

Written by Oliver Nov 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

India is a land of diverse cultures and traditions. When it comes to weddings, Indian brides have a plethora of options to choose from. The attire of an Indian bride is a reflection of her culture, tradition, and personal style. In this blog post, we will dive into what do brides wear in India, and explore the different styles and designs that are popular among Indian brides.

The Pain Points of What Do Brides Wear in India

Choosing the perfect bridal attire can be a daunting task, especially for non-Indian brides who are not familiar with the traditional Indian attire. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which style and design to choose. Additionally, the cost of bridal attire can also be a concern as Indian wedding attire can be quite expensive.

Answering the Target of What Do Brides Wear in India

Indian brides wear a variety of traditional attire, depending on their culture and region. One of the most popular choices is the saree, a long piece of cloth draped around the body. The saree can be made of different fabrics, such as silk, cotton, or chiffon, and is often embellished with embroidery, sequins, or beads. Another popular choice is the lehenga, a skirt and blouse set that is often worn with a dupatta (a long scarf). The lehenga can be made of silk, velvet, or chiffon, and is often heavily embellished with intricate embroidery or sequins. The salwar kameez is another popular choice, which consists of a long tunic (kameez) worn over loose-fitting pants (salwar) and a scarf (dupatta).

Indian brides also wear a variety of jewelry to complement their attire, such as necklaces, earrings, bangles, and bracelets. The jewelry is often made of gold, and can be heavily embellished with precious stones such as diamonds, rubies, or emeralds.

Main Points of What Do Brides Wear in India

In summary, Indian brides wear a variety of traditional attire, such as the saree, lehenga, and salwar kameez, and complement their attire with a variety of jewelry. The attire is often heavily embellished with intricate embroidery or sequins, and made of luxurious fabrics such as silk, velvet, or chiffon.

Styles and Designs of What Do Brides Wear in India

As mentioned earlier, Indian brides have a plethora of options to choose from when it comes to their wedding attire. Each region of India has its own unique style and design, and brides often choose attire that reflects their cultural heritage. For example, brides from North India often wear red or maroon lehengas, while brides from South India often wear silk sarees with gold jewelry.

Personally, I am from the state of Gujarat in Western India, and the traditional bridal attire is the Panetar saree, which is a white saree with red and green embroidery. The saree is often paired with a red blouse and gold jewelry, and is considered to be a symbol of purity and innocence.

The Cost of What Do Brides Wear in India

The cost of bridal attire in India can vary greatly depending on the style, design, and fabric. Designer bridal attire can cost upwards of $10,000, while more affordable options can range from $500 to $2,000. Many brides opt to rent their bridal attire to save costs, or choose to wear a family heirloom that has been passed down through generations.

Choosing the Perfect Bridal Attire

Choosing the perfect bridal attire can be a daunting task, but there are a few things that brides can keep in mind to make the process easier. Firstly, it is important to consider your cultural heritage and personal style when choosing your attire. Secondly, it is important to set a budget and stick to it. Finally, it is a good idea to try on different styles and designs to see what looks best on you.

Where to Buy Bridal Attire in India

If you are in India, there are many places where you can buy bridal attire, such as designer boutiques, traditional markets, and online stores. Some popular designer boutiques include Sabyasachi, Manish Malhotra, and Tarun Tahiliani. Traditional markets such as Chandni Chowk in Delhi and Zaveri Bazaar in Mumbai are also popular destinations to buy bridal attire.

Question and Answer

Q: What color is traditional bridal attire in India?

A: The color of traditional bridal attire in India varies depending on the region and culture. Red and maroon are popular colors in North India, while South Indian brides often wear silk sarees in bright colors such as yellow, green, or pink.

Q: What type of jewelry do Indian brides wear?

A: Indian brides wear a variety of jewelry, such as necklaces, earrings, bangles, and bracelets. The jewelry is often made of gold, and can be heavily embellished with precious stones such as diamonds, rubies, or emeralds.

Q: Can non-Indian brides wear traditional Indian attire?

A: Yes, non-Indian brides can wear traditional Indian attire. However, it is important to consider cultural sensitivity and appropriateness when choosing your attire.

Q: Do Indian brides wear a veil?

A: No, Indian brides do not traditionally wear a veil. Instead, they wear a dupatta, which is a long scarf that is draped over the head and shoulders.

Conclusion of What Do Brides Wear in India

Indian bridal attire is a reflection of the rich cultural heritage and traditions of India. From the elaborate embroidery to the intricate jewelry, every aspect of the attire is carefully chosen to make the bride look her best on her special day. Whether you choose a saree, lehenga, or salwar kameez, the most important thing is to choose an attire that reflects your personal style and cultural heritage.
