What Does A Man Wear To An Indian Wedding

Written by Frank Oct 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their lavish celebrations, vibrant colors, and traditional customs. However, attending an Indian wedding can be a daunting task, especially for men who are unsure of what to wear. In this article, we will guide you on what does a man wear to an Indian wedding and help you make a lasting impression.

Attending an Indian wedding can be overwhelming, and not knowing what to wear can add to the stress. You want to look your best and dress appropriately for the occasion, but you also want to make sure you're comfortable throughout the festivities. It can be challenging to strike a balance between style and comfort, but with the right outfit, you can enjoy the wedding without any worries.

The traditional outfit for men at an Indian wedding is a Sherwani, a long coat-like garment that is worn over a Kurta and Churidar. The Sherwani is usually made of silk or other luxurious fabrics and is adorned with intricate embroidery or embellishments. The Kurta is a long shirt that reaches the knees, and the Churidar is a tight-fitting trouser that gathers at the ankles. Men can also wear a Nehru jacket, which is a shorter version of the Sherwani that can be paired with a Kurta and Churidar.

In addition to the Sherwani, men can also wear a Bandhgala suit or a Jodhpuri suit. The Bandhgala suit is a formal suit that features a high-collared jacket, while the Jodhpuri suit is a more casual version of the Bandhgala suit that is often worn for daytime events. Both outfits can be paired with a Kurta and Churidar.

My Experience

As an Indian man, I have attended my fair share of weddings, and dressing up for the occasion is always a highlight. I usually opt for a Sherwani or a Bandhgala suit, depending on the formality of the event. I make sure to choose comfortable fabrics and well-fitted clothes to ensure that I can move around easily and enjoy the festivities. It's also essential to accessorize appropriately, so I always wear a pair of Jutti, traditional Indian footwear, and a turban or a scarf.

What to Keep in Mind

When choosing an outfit for an Indian wedding, keep in mind the formality of the event and the time of day. If the wedding is in the evening, a Sherwani or a Bandhgala suit would be appropriate, while a Jodhpuri suit or a Nehru jacket would be suitable for a daytime event. Make sure to choose comfortable fabrics and well-fitted clothes, so you can enjoy the celebrations without any discomfort. Accessorize appropriately with traditional Indian footwear and headwear, and don't be afraid to add a pop of color to your outfit.

Colors to Avoid

While Indian weddings are known for their vibrant colors, there are some colors that men should avoid wearing. Black and white are considered inauspicious for weddings, so it's best to steer clear of those colors. Red and gold are popular choices for Indian weddings, but it's always a good idea to check with the bride and groom about their color preferences.

How to Accessorize

Accessories are an essential part of an Indian wedding outfit for men. Traditional Indian footwear, such as Jutti or Mojari, are a must-have and can add a touch of elegance to your outfit. Headwear, such as a turban or a scarf, can also complete your look. You can also add a brooch or a pocket square to your outfit for a touch of sophistication.

My Final Thoughts

Attending an Indian wedding is an unforgettable experience, and dressing up for the occasion can be just as exciting. The key is to choose an outfit that is comfortable, well-fitted, and appropriate for the event's formality and time of day. Accessories are also an essential part of the outfit, so don't be afraid to add a touch of tradition and elegance to your look.

Question and Answer

Q. Can men wear a suit to an Indian wedding?

A. Yes, men can wear a Bandhgala suit or a Jodhpuri suit to an Indian wedding. However, it's essential to pair the suit with a traditional Indian outfit, such as a Kurta and Churidar, to maintain the cultural theme of the wedding.

Q. Is it okay to wear jeans to an Indian wedding?

A. Jeans are not appropriate for an Indian wedding as they are considered too casual. It's best to wear traditional Indian attire, such as a Sherwani, a Bandhgala suit, or a Jodhpuri suit.

Q. Can men wear sandals to an Indian wedding?

A. Sandals are not appropriate for an Indian wedding as they are considered too casual. Traditional Indian footwear, such as Jutti or Mojari, are a better option and can add a touch of elegance to your outfit.

Q. What is the traditional color for men's outfits at an Indian wedding?

A. There is no traditional color for men's outfits at an Indian wedding. However, shades of red and gold are popular choices, and it's always a good idea to check with the bride and groom about their color preferences.

Conclusion of What Does a Man Wear to an Indian Wedding

Attending an Indian wedding can be a daunting task, but with the right outfit, you can make a lasting impression. Choose an outfit that is comfortable, well-fitted, and appropriate for the event's formality and time of day. Accessorize appropriately with traditional Indian footwear and headwear, and don't be afraid to add a pop of color to your outfit. By following these tips, you'll be sure to turn heads and make a statement at the wedding.
