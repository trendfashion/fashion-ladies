What Does Slack Huddle Do

Written by Christine Aug 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever found yourself struggling to keep up with your team's progress or feeling like you're out of the loop? Perhaps you're tired of constantly jumping between different communication platforms just to stay connected. These are common pain points that many teams face, but luckily, Slack has a solution: Slack Huddle.

Slack Huddle is a feature within the Slack platform that allows teams to hold quick, impromptu meetings or check-ins with members in different locations, without the need for scheduling or lengthy meetings. With Slack Huddle, teams can stay in sync and keep up with each other's progress in real-time, saving time and increasing productivity.

Slack Huddle is targeted towards remote teams, highly collaborative teams, and teams with members in different time zones. It's a simple and effective way to keep everyone on the same page and ensure that everyone is working towards the same goals.

How Does Slack Huddle Work?

Slack Huddle is a straightforward feature that's easy to use. To initiate a huddle, simply select the members you want to include and click on the Huddle button. This will create a new channel that's only visible to those members. From there, you can start a voice or video call, or simply use the chat function to communicate.

One personal experience I had with Slack Huddle was when my team was working on a project that had a tight deadline. We were all working remotely, and it was tough to keep up with everyone's progress. Slack Huddle allowed us to quickly check-in with each other and ensure that we were all on track. It saved us a lot of time and helped us meet our deadline.

Benefits of Slack Huddle

Slack Huddle offers several benefits to teams, including:

Real-time communication:

Slack Huddle allows teams to communicate in real-time, which is especially important for remote teams or teams with members in different time zones. It ensures that everyone is on the same page and working towards the same goals.

Increased productivity:

Slack Huddle allows teams to quickly check-in with each other and address any issues or roadblocks that may be hindering progress. It saves time and increases productivity by eliminating the need for lengthy meetings or scheduling conflicts.

How to Use Slack Huddle Effectively

To use Slack Huddle effectively, it's important to keep a few things in mind:

Keep it brief:

Slack Huddle is designed to be a quick and efficient way to communicate. Keep the meetings brief and focused on the task at hand.

Set expectations:

Use Cases for Slack Huddle

Slack Huddle can be used in a variety of situations, including:

Project check-ins:

Slack Huddle is a great way to quickly check-in with team members and ensure that everyone is on track with a project.

Remote team collaboration:

Impromptu meetings:

Slack Huddle is perfect for impromptu meetings or check-ins that don't require scheduling or lengthy meetings.
