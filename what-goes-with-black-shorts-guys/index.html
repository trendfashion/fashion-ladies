What Goes With Black Shorts Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Black shorts are a versatile and stylish option for men's summer fashion. However, choosing the right top, shoes, and accessories to wear with black shorts can be a daunting task. It can be challenging to find the right balance between comfort, style, and functionality.

If you're struggling to put together the perfect outfit with black shorts, don't worry. This guide will help you understand what goes with black shorts guys and provide you with some outfit ideas.

When it comes to pairing black shorts, the options are endless. You can create a casual or dressy look, depending on the occasion. The key is to choose complementary colors and textures that work well with black shorts.

What Goes with Black Shorts Guys: Casual Outfit Ideas

For a casual look, you can pair black shorts with a simple t-shirt or polo shirt. White or gray t-shirts are classic options that go well with black shorts. You can also experiment with brighter colors like blue, green, or red.

When it comes to shoes, choose sneakers, loafers, or flip flops, depending on the occasion. White sneakers or loafers can add a touch of sophistication to your casual look. Flip flops are perfect for a day at the beach or a relaxed weekend.

Accessories like sunglasses, hats, and watches can also elevate your casual outfit. Aviator sunglasses or a baseball cap can add a sporty touch to your look. A leather or metal watch can make your outfit look more polished.

What Goes with Black Shorts Guys: Dressy Outfit Ideas

To create a dressier look with black shorts, you can pair them with a button-down shirt or a blazer. White or light blue shirts are classic options that work well with black shorts. You can also choose a patterned shirt or a bold color like pink or purple.

When it comes to shoes, choose dress shoes like oxfords or loafers. Brown or black shoes can add a touch of elegance to your outfit.

Accessories like a leather belt, a tie, or cufflinks can also make your outfit look more formal. A leather belt can add a touch of sophistication to your look. A tie can make your outfit look more formal, especially if you're attending a wedding or a business event.

What Goes with Black Shorts Guys: The Bottom Line

Pairing black shorts with the right top, shoes, and accessories can create a stylish and versatile look. Whether you're going for a casual or dressy look, the key is to choose complementary colors and textures that work well with black shorts.

How to Care for Black Shorts: Tips and Tricks

To keep your black shorts looking their best, follow these tips:

- Read the care instructions on the label and follow them closely.

- Wash your black shorts in cold water to prevent fading.

- Use a mild detergent and avoid bleach or fabric softeners.

- Air dry your black shorts instead of using a dryer to prevent shrinking and damage.

FAQs About What Goes with Black Shorts Guys

Q: Can I wear black shorts with black shoes?

A: Yes, you can wear black shorts with black shoes, but make sure to add some color or texture to your outfit to avoid looking too monochromatic.

Q: Can I wear a black shirt with black shorts?

A: It's best to avoid wearing a black shirt with black shorts, as it can create a too monochromatic look. Instead, choose a complementary color like white, gray, or blue.

Q: Can I wear sandals with black shorts?

A: Yes, you can wear sandals with black shorts, but make sure to choose a pair of sandals that complements your outfit and the occasion.

Q: Can I wear a hat with black shorts?

A: Yes, you can wear a hat with black shorts, but make sure to choose a hat that complements your outfit and the occasion. A baseball cap is perfect for a casual look, while a fedora or a Panama hat can add a touch of sophistication to your outfit.

Conclusion of What Goes with Black Shorts Guys

Pairing black shorts can be a fun and creative process. Whether you're going for a casual or dressy look, choose complementary colors and textures that work well with black shorts. Don't be afraid to experiment with different outfits and accessories to find your perfect style.
