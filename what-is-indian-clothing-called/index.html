What Is Indian Clothing Called

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you curious about the traditional clothing of India but don't know what it's called? Perhaps you're planning to travel to India or attend an Indian wedding and want to know what type of attire is appropriate. Whatever your reason, understanding what is Indian clothing called can be helpful in many situations.

Indian clothing is often associated with vibrant colors, intricate designs, and unique styles. However, for those unfamiliar with Indian culture, it can be challenging to navigate the different types of Indian garments and their names.

The traditional clothing of India varies by region, religion, and occasion. However, some of the most common types of Indian clothing include sarees, salwar kameez, lehenga choli, and kurta pajama.

In summary, Indian clothing is the traditional attire worn by people in India. It includes a variety of garments such as sarees, salwar kameez, lehenga choli, and kurta pajama, and varies by region, religion, and occasion.

What is a Saree?

A saree is a long piece of cloth draped around the body, typically worn by women in India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and Nepal. The saree is usually made of silk, cotton, or chiffon and comes in a wide range of colors and designs. It can be draped in different ways, depending on the region and occasion.

As an Indian woman, I have worn sarees for various occasions, such as weddings, festivals, and family gatherings. One of my favorite sarees is a vibrant pink and gold silk saree that I wore to my sister's wedding. It was adorned with intricate embroidery and made me feel elegant and beautiful.

What is a Salwar Kameez?

A salwar kameez is a two-piece outfit consisting of a long tunic (kameez) and loose-fitting pants (salwar). The kameez can be short or long and is often paired with a dupatta (scarf). Salwar kameez is commonly worn by women in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.

During my college days, I used to wear salwar kameez to class. It was comfortable and practical, and I could easily move around without feeling restricted. I still wear salwar kameez occasionally for casual outings or when visiting family.

What is a Lehenga Choli?

A lehenga choli is a three-piece outfit consisting of a long skirt (lehenga), a short blouse (choli), and a dupatta (scarf). It is commonly worn by women in India, especially for weddings and other formal occasions.

I remember wearing a beautiful red and gold lehenga choli for my cousin's wedding. It was heavily embroidered and had intricate details that made it stand out. I received many compliments on my outfit and felt like a princess.

How to Choose the Right Indian Clothing?

If you're unsure about what type of Indian clothing to wear for a specific occasion, it's always best to ask someone who is familiar with Indian culture. Alternatively, you can do some research online and look for inspiration from Bollywood movies or fashion bloggers.

When choosing Indian clothing, consider the occasion and your personal style. If you're attending a wedding or a formal event, opt for a saree or lehenga choli. If you prefer something more comfortable, go for a salwar kameez or kurta pajama.

Conclusion of What is Indian Clothing Called

In summary, Indian clothing is the traditional attire worn by people in India and varies by region, religion, and occasion. Some of the most common types of Indian clothing include sarees, salwar kameez, lehenga choli, and kurta pajama. When choosing Indian clothing, consider the occasion and your personal style, and don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and designs.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it appropriate to wear Indian clothing to a Western wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code and the preferences of the bride and groom. If the wedding is formal, it's best to opt for a Western outfit. However, if the dress code is casual or if the bride and groom have specifically requested Indian attire, it's acceptable to wear Indian clothing.

Q: Can men wear sarees or lehenga choli?

A: Sarees and lehenga choli are traditionally worn by women, but there are no hard and fast rules. Men can experiment with different types of Indian clothing such as kurta pajama or dhoti-kurta.

Q: What is the significance of the color red in Indian clothing?

A: In Indian culture, red is considered a symbol of love and marriage. It's common for brides to wear red or shades of red on their wedding day. However, red is also a popular color for other occasions such as festivals and religious ceremonies.

Q: How do I take care of my Indian clothing?

A: Indian clothing is often made of delicate fabrics such as silk or chiffon and requires special care. It's best to follow the care instructions on the label or consult a professional dry cleaner. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, and store your Indian clothing in a cool, dry place.
