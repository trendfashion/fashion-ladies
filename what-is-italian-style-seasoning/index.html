What Is Italian Style Seasoning

Written by Frank Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever tried making Italian dishes at home but found that they just don't taste quite right? Or maybe you're just looking to add some extra flavor to your meals but don't know where to start. Whatever the case may be, Italian style seasoning could be the solution you're looking for.

Italian style seasoning is a blend of herbs and spices that is commonly used in Italian cuisine. It typically includes a mixture of basil, oregano, rosemary, thyme, garlic powder, and onion powder. This combination of flavors is what gives Italian dishes their distinct taste and aroma.

If you're new to cooking with Italian seasonings, don't worry! It's an easy way to elevate the flavor of your meals without having to spend hours in the kitchen. In this post, we'll take a closer look at Italian style seasoning and how you can use it to enhance your cooking.

Why Use Italian Style Seasoning?

Italian style seasoning can be used in a variety of dishes, from pasta sauces to soups and stews. It adds depth and complexity to the flavors in your food, making even the simplest dishes taste more delicious. Plus, using a pre-made seasoning blend saves you time and effort compared to measuring out individual herbs and spices.

Personally, I love using Italian style seasoning when making homemade pizza. It gives the crust and toppings an extra boost of flavor that takes it to the next level. I also like to sprinkle it on roasted vegetables or grilled meats for an easy weeknight meal.

How to Use Italian Style Seasoning

The easiest way to use Italian style seasoning is to simply sprinkle it on your food before or after cooking. You can also mix it into sauces or marinades for an extra punch of flavor. Keep in mind that a little goes a long way, so start with a small amount and adjust to your liking.

If you're feeling adventurous, you can also try making your own Italian seasoning blend at home. All you need is a combination of dried herbs and spices, such as basil, oregano, thyme, rosemary, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix them together in a jar and store in a cool, dry place for up to six months.

Benefits of Using Italian Style Seasoning

In addition to its delicious flavor, Italian style seasoning also has some health benefits. Many of the herbs and spices used in the blend have anti-inflammatory properties and can help boost your immune system. Plus, using seasoning blends instead of salt can help reduce your sodium intake.

How to Make Italian Style Seasoning

If you want to make your own Italian style seasoning blend, here's a simple recipe to try:

  • 2 tablespoons dried basil
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoons dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons dried rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons onion powder

Mix all of the ingredients together in a jar and store in a cool, dry place for up to six months.

FAQs About Italian Style Seasoning

Q: Can I use Italian style seasoning in non-Italian dishes?

A: Absolutely! Italian style seasoning can be used in a variety of dishes, from roasted vegetables to grilled meats and even scrambled eggs.

Q: What's the difference between Italian seasoning and Italian style seasoning?

A: Italian seasoning typically includes a blend of dried herbs such as basil, oregano, and thyme. Italian style seasoning, on the other hand, usually includes additional spices like garlic powder and onion powder for extra flavor.

Q: Can I use fresh herbs instead of dried?

A: Yes, you can use fresh herbs in place of dried. Just keep in mind that the flavor may be slightly different and you'll need to adjust the amounts accordingly.

Q: Is Italian style seasoning gluten-free?

A: As long as the individual herbs and spices used in the blend are gluten-free, then the seasoning blend should be as well. However, it's always a good idea to double-check the ingredients before using.

Conclusion of What is Italian Style Seasoning

Italian style seasoning is a versatile blend of herbs and spices that can elevate the flavor of your meals. Whether you're making pasta sauce, roasted vegetables, or grilled meats, Italian style seasoning is a quick and easy way to add extra flavor. You can buy pre-made blends at the store or make your own at home using a combination of dried herbs and spices. With its delicious flavor and potential health benefits, Italian style seasoning is a must-have for any home cook.
