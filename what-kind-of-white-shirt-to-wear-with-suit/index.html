What Kind Of White Shirt To Wear With Suit A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 28, 2023 · 5 min read

When it comes to dressing up for a formal event, nothing beats a classic suit. However, choosing the right shirt to go with it can be a bit of a challenge. After all, the wrong choice of shirt can ruin the entire look. In this post, we’ll be discussing what kind of white shirt to wear with suit, and how to make sure you look your best.

It’s not uncommon to feel overwhelmed when trying to choose the right shirt to wear with your suit. There are so many different styles and fabrics to choose from, and it can be difficult to know where to start. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered.

The short answer to the question of what kind of white shirt to wear with suit is: it depends. The type of shirt you choose will depend on a number of factors, including the type of suit you’re wearing, the occasion, and your personal style.

So, what are some of the things you should consider when choosing a white shirt to wear with your suit? First and foremost, you’ll want to consider the fit. Your shirt should fit you well, without being too baggy or too tight. You’ll also want to consider the fabric – a high-quality cotton shirt is always a good choice.

Personal Experience: Classic White Dress Shirt

When it comes to dressing up for a formal event, my go-to choice is always a classic white dress shirt. It’s versatile, timeless, and it goes with just about any suit. I like to choose a shirt with a pointed collar and a slim fit – this gives me a clean and polished look without being too formal.

Personal Experience: Button-Down White Shirt

If I’m going for a slightly more casual look, I’ll opt for a button-down white shirt. This style of shirt is less formal than a classic dress shirt, but it still looks great with a suit. I like to choose a shirt with a button-down collar and a slightly looser fit – this gives me a more relaxed and comfortable look.

Types of White Shirts

There are several different types of white shirts to choose from, including classic dress shirts, button-down shirts, and even t-shirts. Here’s a rundown of each:

Classic Dress Shirts: These are the most formal type of white shirt, and they’re typically worn with a suit and tie. They come in a variety of styles and fabrics, and they’re a great choice for formal occasions.

Button-Down Shirts: These shirts have buttons on the collar, and they’re typically less formal than classic dress shirts. They’re a great choice for more casual events, and they can be worn with or without a tie.

T-Shirts: While not typically worn with a suit, a plain white t-shirt can be a good choice for a more casual look. Just make sure the t-shirt fits well and is made from a high-quality fabric.

How to Choose the Right White Shirt for Your Suit

When choosing a white shirt to wear with your suit, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider the occasion – a classic dress shirt is always a safe choice for formal events, while a button-down shirt can be a good choice for more casual events.

Next, consider the fit – your shirt should fit well without being too tight or too baggy. You’ll also want to consider the fabric – a high-quality cotton shirt is always a good choice.

Personal Experience: Textured White Shirt

One white shirt that I’ve found works particularly well with a suit is a textured white shirt. This type of shirt adds some subtle visual interest to the outfit, and it looks great with a solid-colored suit. I like to pair a textured white shirt with a navy or charcoal suit for a classic look.

Personal Experience: White Shirt with French Cuffs

If I’m looking to add a bit of extra style to my outfit, I’ll opt for a white shirt with French cuffs. These cuffs are folded back and fastened with cufflinks, which adds a bit of visual interest to the outfit. I like to wear a white shirt with French cuffs with a black or gray suit for a more formal look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can you wear a white shirt with a patterned suit?

A: Yes, you can! However, you’ll want to choose a white shirt with a simple pattern or no pattern at all. A patterned shirt can clash with a patterned suit, so it’s best to keep it simple.

Q: Can you wear a white t-shirt with a suit?

A: While a white t-shirt can be a good choice for a more casual look, it’s not typically worn with a suit. Stick to a classic dress shirt or a button-down shirt for a more formal look.

Q: Should you tuck your shirt in when wearing a suit?

A: Yes, you should always tuck your shirt in when wearing a suit. This gives you a clean and polished look, and it helps to show off your suit to its best advantage.

Q: What color tie should you wear with a white shirt and suit?

A: A white shirt and suit is a classic combination that goes well with just about any tie color. However, if you’re looking for a safe choice, you can’t go wrong with a navy or burgundy tie.

Conclusion of What Kind of White Shirt to Wear with Suit

Choosing the right white shirt to wear with your suit can be a bit of a challenge, but it doesn’t have to be. By considering the occasion, the fit, and the fabric, you can choose a shirt that looks great and makes you feel confident. Whether you opt for a classic dress shirt or a more casual button-down, make sure to choose a shirt that fits well and complements your personal style.
