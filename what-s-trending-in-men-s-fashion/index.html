What s Trending In Men s Fashion A Beginner s Guide

Written by Christine Aug 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you ever wonder how to keep up with fashion trends as a man? With so many styles and options out there, it can be challenging to know where to start. However, with this beginner's guide to what's trending in men's fashion, you'll be able to navigate the world of fashion with ease and confidence.

As a man, you may have experienced the struggle of finding clothes that fit well or look stylish. It can be frustrating to see all the latest trends and not know how to incorporate them into your wardrobe. Additionally, there's the pressure to keep up with the latest trends while still staying true to your personal style and budget.

So, what's trending in men's fashion? The answer is a mix of old classics and new takes on familiar styles. Think oversized blazers, bold prints, and vintage-inspired pieces.

Overall, the main trend in men's fashion is the focus on individualism and self-expression. Rather than following strict fashion rules, men are encouraged to experiment with different styles and make their own statement.

Oversized Blazers

Oversized blazers are a staple of men's fashion in 2021. This trend is all about wearing a blazer that is a few sizes too big for you. The result is a relaxed and effortless look that can be dressed up or down. To pull off this trend, pair your oversized blazer with slim-fit pants or jeans and a simple t-shirt or sweater.

Personally, I love the oversized blazer trend because it's so versatile. I can wear it to work with dress pants and a button-up shirt, or I can dress it down with jeans and sneakers for a casual weekend look.

Bold Prints

Bold prints are another trend that's taking over men's fashion in 2021. From animal prints to floral patterns, there's no shortage of options when it comes to this trend. To make this trend work for you, start with a simple piece of clothing like a t-shirt or sweater with a bold print. Pair it with neutral-colored pants or shorts to keep the focus on the print.

I recently purchased a floral-print shirt and was a bit hesitant to wear it at first. However, once I paired it with black jeans and boots, I felt confident and stylish.

Vintage-Inspired Pieces

Vintage-inspired pieces are a trend that's been growing in popularity in recent years. This trend is all about incorporating classic styles from the past into your modern wardrobe. Think leather jackets, denim shirts, and retro sneakers.

The great thing about this trend is that you can find vintage-inspired pieces at a variety of price points. Whether you're on a budget or looking to splurge, there's a vintage-inspired piece out there for you.

How to Incorporate Trends into Your Wardrobe

If you're new to fashion trends, it can be overwhelming to figure out how to incorporate them into your wardrobe. However, there are a few simple tips that can help.

First, start small. Rather than buying an entire outfit based on a trend, try incorporating one trend piece into your existing wardrobe. This can be something as simple as a printed t-shirt or a pair of sneakers.

Second, don't be afraid to mix and match. Trends don't have to be worn head-to-toe. Instead, try pairing a trend piece with something classic or neutral to balance it out.

Third, focus on fit. No matter how stylish a piece is, if it doesn't fit well, it won't look good. Make sure to try on clothes before buying them and pay attention to how they fit your body.

Question and Answer

Q: Are there any trends that are not recommended for men over 40?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to fashion, some trends may not be as age-appropriate for men over 40. For example, overly distressed jeans or flashy logos may look out of place on an older man. However, ultimately it comes down to personal style and comfort.

Q: Can I still wear classic pieces even if they're not trending?

A: Absolutely! Classic pieces like a well-fitted suit or a leather jacket will never go out of style. In fact, incorporating classic pieces into your wardrobe is a great way to balance out trendy pieces.

Q: How do I know which trends will work for me?

A: It's all about trial and error. Don't be afraid to try new things and see what works for you. However, it's important to also consider your body type, personal style, and comfort level when trying out new trends.

Q: Can I still be fashionable without breaking the bank?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of affordable options out there when it comes to fashion. Look for sales, shop at thrift stores, or invest in a few key pieces that can be mixed and matched with other items in your wardrobe.

Conclusion of What's Trending in Men's Fashion

Overall, the key to staying on top of what's trending in men's fashion is to focus on individualism and self-expression. Whether you're rocking an oversized blazer or a bold print, the most important thing is that you feel confident and comfortable in what you're wearing. By incorporating trends into your wardrobe in a way that works for you, you'll be able to stay stylish and on-trend without sacrificing your personal style or budget.
