Cool What Shoes To Wear With Dresses In The Summer For New Ideas

Written by William Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Summer is here, and it's time to break out the dresses! But what shoes should you wear with them to stay comfortable and stylish? Don't worry, we've got you covered with this guide to what shoes to wear with dresses in the summer.

It can be frustrating trying to find the perfect shoes to wear with your summer dresses. You want something that looks good, feels comfortable, and won't leave your feet sweaty and blistered. But with so many options out there, it can be hard to know where to start.

The best shoes to wear with summer dresses are ones that are comfortable, breathable, and versatile. You want shoes that can take you from a day at the beach to a night out with friends without missing a beat.

In summary, when it comes to what shoes to wear with dresses in the summer, look for options that are comfortable, breathable, and versatile. Now, let's take a closer look at some specific shoe styles that fit the bill.

1. Sandals

Sandals are a classic choice for summer dresses. They're comfortable, breathable, and come in a variety of styles to suit any dress. Whether you prefer strappy sandals, slides, or gladiator sandals, there's a pair out there for you.

Personally, I love pairing a flowy sundress with some strappy leather sandals. It's the perfect combination of comfort and style.

2. Sneakers

Sneakers might not be the first thing that comes to mind when you think of summer dresses, but hear me out. A pair of white sneakers can look amazing with a summer dress, especially if you're going for a more casual look.

I love wearing my white Converse sneakers with a simple t-shirt dress for a laid-back, but still put-together look.

3. Espadrilles

Espadrilles are a summer staple for a reason. They're comfortable, stylish, and come in a variety of colors and styles. Whether you prefer wedge espadrilles or flat ones, they're a great option for pairing with summer dresses.

I love wearing my black wedge espadrilles with a floral maxi dress for a boho vibe.

4. Mules

Mules are another great option for summer dresses. They're easy to slip on and off, and come in a variety of styles to suit any dress. Whether you prefer heeled mules or flat ones, they're a stylish and comfortable option.

I love pairing my heeled mules with a midi dress for a chic and sophisticated look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear boots with summer dresses?

A: While it's not the most traditional choice, you can definitely wear boots with summer dresses. Just make sure to choose a lighter material, like suede or canvas, and pair them with a shorter dress to balance out the look.

Q: What kind of sandals should I wear with a maxi dress?

A: For a maxi dress, you can't go wrong with a pair of strappy sandals. Look for ones with a low heel if you want a bit of height, or go for flat sandals if you prefer something more comfortable.

Q: Can I wear flip flops with a summer dress?

A: While flip flops are comfortable, they're not the best choice for pairing with summer dresses. They can make your feet look sloppy and unpolished. Instead, opt for a pair of sandals or espadrilles for a more put-together look.

Q: Are there any colors I should avoid when it comes to shoes and summer dresses?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, it's generally best to avoid black shoes with light-colored dresses. They can look too heavy and clash with the summery vibe. Instead, opt for lighter colors like white, tan, or metallics.

Conclusion of what shoes to wear with dresses in the summer

When it comes to what shoes to wear with dresses in the summer, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you prefer sandals, sneakers, espadrilles, or mules, there's a pair out there that will work for you. Just make sure to choose shoes that are comfortable, breathable, and versatile, and you'll be ready to take on the summer in style!
