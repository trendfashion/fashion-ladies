What Should A White Person Wear To An Indian Wedding

Written by Bardi Jul 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Attending an Indian wedding as a white person can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to choosing the right outfit. You don't want to offend anyone by wearing something inappropriate, but you also don't want to stick out like a sore thumb. In this post, we will guide you through what you should wear to an Indian wedding as a white person.

Indian weddings are known for their vibrant colors, intricate designs, and rich culture. It is important to respect the customs and traditions of the wedding by dressing appropriately. Not only will this show your appreciation for the culture, but it will also help you blend in with the rest of the guests.

The ideal outfit for a white person attending an Indian wedding would be a traditional Indian attire such as a saree or a salwar kameez. However, if you are not comfortable wearing these outfits, you can opt for a western-style outfit that is in line with Indian wedding attire. For women, this could be a long flowy dress with a dupatta (scarf) or a lehenga (long skirt) with a crop top. Men can wear a sherwani (long coat) with a kurta (long shirt) and churidar (tight pants) or a suit with a Nehru jacket.

To ensure that your outfit is appropriate, it is best to check with the bride or groom or someone in the wedding party before deciding on what to wear. They will be able to guide you on the dress code and help you choose an outfit that is suitable for the occasion. It is also important to keep in mind the venue and the time of the wedding. If the wedding is in a temple or a traditional venue, it is best to dress conservatively. If the wedding is in a hotel or a modern venue, you can opt for a more modern outfit.

In summary, the key to dressing appropriately for an Indian wedding as a white person is to respect the culture and traditions while also feeling comfortable in your outfit. Choose an outfit that is in line with Indian wedding attire, check with the bride or groom for the dress code, and keep in mind the venue and time of the wedding.

What Should a White Person Wear to an Indian Wedding - Personal Experience

As a white person attending an Indian wedding, I was nervous about what to wear. I did not want to offend anyone by wearing something inappropriate, so I consulted with the bride's sister. What Should a White Person Wear to an Indian Wedding - Tips for Men

As a man attending an Indian wedding, it is important to dress appropriately while also feeling comfortable in your outfit. If the wedding is in a hotel or a modern venue, you can opt for a more modern outfit.

What Should a White Person Wear to an Indian Wedding - Understanding Indian Attire

Indian attire is known for its vibrant colors, intricate designs, and rich culture. Sarees and salwar kameezes are traditional Indian outfits for women, while sherwanis and kurtas are traditional Indian outfits for men. Women can also opt for a lehenga or a long flowy dress with a dupatta, while men can wear a suit with a Nehru jacket. It is important to choose an outfit that is in line with Indian wedding attire while also feeling comfortable and respecting the culture and traditions of the wedding.

What Should a White Person Wear to an Indian Wedding - Accessories

Accessories are an important part of Indian attire. Women can wear bangles, earrings, and a necklace to complete their outfit. They can also wear a bindi (a decorative dot on the forehead) and apply henna on their hands. Men can wear a turban or a safa (a decorative headgear) and carry a stole or a dupatta. It is important to keep in mind the dress code and the venue when choosing your accessories.
