The History of PG Tips

PG Tips was first introduced in the UK in the 1930s by Brooke Bond, a tea company that was established in 1869. The brand was named after its founder, Arthur Brooke, and the "PG" stands for "Pre-Gestee," a digestive aid that was added to the tea in its early days. The brand quickly gained popularity among the people, and it has remained a favorite ever since. Today, PG Tips is owned by Unilever, a multinational consumer goods company.
