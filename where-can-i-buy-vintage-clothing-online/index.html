Where Can I Buy Vintage Clothing Online

Written by Oliver Aug 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Have you ever wanted to add vintage clothing to your wardrobe but didn't know where to start looking? Vintage clothing can be hard to find in physical stores and it can be time-consuming to search through thrift stores. Plus, there's no guarantee that you'll find something you like in your size. This is where buying vintage clothing online comes in handy. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

If you're wondering, "where can I buy vintage clothing online?" you're in luck. There are many online stores that specialize in selling vintage clothing. These stores offer a variety of styles, sizes, and eras to choose from. You can easily find unique and one-of-a-kind pieces that you won't find anywhere else.

So, let's dive into the world of vintage clothing and explore the best places to buy them online.

Asos Marketplace

Asos Marketplace is a great place to start your search for vintage clothing. It's a platform that allows independent boutiques and vintage sellers to sell their items online. You can find a wide variety of vintage clothing on this site, from 1920s flapper dresses to 1990s grunge pieces. Asos Marketplace offers sizes for everyone, so you're sure to find something that fits you.

Personally, I have bought a vintage 1970s maxi dress from Asos Marketplace and it has become one of my favorite pieces in my wardrobe. The dress was in great condition and looked just as it did in the photos on the website.

Etsy

Etsy is another great option for buying vintage clothing online. It's a marketplace that allows independent sellers to sell vintage and handmade items online. You can find vintage clothing from all eras and styles on Etsy. The site also offers a wide range of sizes, so everyone can find something they love.

I have bought several vintage pieces from different sellers on Etsy and have always been satisfied with my purchases. The sellers on Etsy are usually very communicative and happy to answer any questions you may have about the item you're interested in buying.

Beyond Retro

Beyond Retro is a vintage clothing store based in the UK, but they ship worldwide. They offer a wide selection of vintage clothing from the 1950s to the 1990s. You can find everything from vintage band t-shirts to vintage Levi's jeans on their website. Beyond Retro also offers a range of sizes, so everyone can find something they love.

If you're looking for something specific, Beyond Retro has a great search function on their website that allows you to search by era, style, and even color. This makes it easy to find exactly what you're looking for.

How to Buy Vintage Clothing Online

Buying vintage clothing online can be a bit daunting, especially if you're not used to shopping online. Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Check the measurements: Vintage clothing sizes can be different from modern sizes, so it's important to check the measurements of the garment before you buy it.
  • Read the description: Make sure to read the description of the item carefully. This will give you information about the condition of the garment and any flaws it may have.
  • Look at the photos: Make sure to look at all the photos of the item. This will give you a good idea of what the garment looks like from all angles.
  • Check the return policy: Make sure to check the return policy before you buy the item. If the item doesn't fit or isn't what you expected, you want to be able to return it.

Conclusion of Where Can I Buy Vintage Clothing Online

Buying vintage clothing online is a great way to add unique pieces to your wardrobe. There are many online stores that specialize in selling vintage clothing, from Asos Marketplace to Etsy to Beyond Retro. When buying vintage clothing online, make sure to check the measurements, read the description, look at the photos, and check the return policy. Happy shopping!

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if the vintage clothing I'm buying online is authentic?

A: It can be hard to tell if vintage clothing is authentic just by looking at photos online. However, reputable sellers will usually provide information about the era and brand of the garment. If you're unsure, you can always ask the seller for more information.

Q: What should I do if the vintage clothing I buy online doesn't fit?

A: Make sure to check the return policy before you buy the item. If the item doesn't fit, you should be able to return it for a refund or exchange.

Q: How do I care for vintage clothing?

A: Vintage clothing can be delicate, so it's important to care for it properly. Always follow the care instructions on the label, if there is one. If there isn't a label, hand wash the garment in cold water and lay it flat to dry.

Q: Can I find vintage clothing for men online?

A: Yes, there are many online stores that specialize in selling vintage clothing for men. Asos Marketplace and Beyond Retro both offer a wide selection of vintage clothing for men.
