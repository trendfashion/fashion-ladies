Where To Buy Old Western Clothing Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a fan of old western movies or just love the vintage style? Do you want to dress up like a cowboy or a cowgirl for a themed party or Halloween? If so, you might be wondering where to buy old western clothing that fits your taste, budget, and location.

Searching for old western clothing can be a daunting task, especially if you don't know where to start. You might struggle to find authentic pieces, the right size or style, or affordable prices. Moreover, you might have to deal with limited options, long shipping times, or poor customer service.

But don't worry, we got you covered! In this article, we will show you the best places to buy old western clothing online and offline. Whether you're looking for cowboy hats, boots, shirts, jackets, skirts, or dresses, we have got something for you!

Online Stores for Old Western Clothing

One of the easiest ways to buy old western clothing is to browse online stores that specialize in vintage or retro fashion. Here are some of the most popular options:

Etsy: This online marketplace is a treasure trove for vintage lovers, including those who are into old western clothing. You can find a wide variety of items from different eras, styles, and prices, sold by independent sellers worldwide. Some of the most popular categories are cowboy boots, hats, jackets, and dresses. You can also customize your search by using keywords such as "western," "cowboy," "ranch," or "rodeo."

Amazon: This giant e-commerce platform has a vast selection of old western clothing, ranging from classic to modern styles. You can find both brand new and secondhand items, depending on your preference and budget. Some of the most popular brands include Wrangler, Stetson, Levi's, and Ariat. You can also filter your search by size, color, price, and customer ratings.

eBay: This online auction site is another great option for buying old western clothing, especially if you're looking for rare or collectible items. You can bid on items or buy them outright, depending on the seller's preferences. Some of the most popular categories are vintage cowboy boots, hats, and belts. You can also check the seller's feedback and ratings before making a purchase.

Offline Stores for Old Western Clothing

If you prefer to see and touch the old western clothing before buying them, you can visit local stores that specialize in vintage or western wear. Here are some of the best options:

Thrift Stores: These stores sell secondhand items that are donated by individuals or organizations. You can find a wide variety of old western clothing at thrift stores, ranging from cowboy hats to leather jackets. The prices are usually lower than in online stores, but the selection might be limited or unpredictable. Some of the most popular thrift stores are Goodwill, Salvation Army, and Value Village.

Vintage Stores: These stores sell curated collections of old western clothing that are sourced from various places. You can find unique and high-quality pieces at vintage stores, but the prices might be higher than in thrift stores. Some of the most popular vintage stores are Beyond Retro, Rokit, and Vintage King.

Western Wear Stores: These stores sell new and old western clothing that are designed for practical and fashion purposes. You can find a wide variety of items at western wear stores, ranging from cowboy boots to rodeo shirts. The prices are usually higher than in thrift or vintage stores, but the quality and authenticity are guaranteed. Some of the most popular western wear stores are Sheplers, Cavender's, and Boot Barn.

How to Choose the Best Old Western Clothing

Now that you know where to buy old western clothing, you might wonder how to choose the best items that suit your style, budget, and occasion. Here are some tips to help you:

Know Your Size: Old western clothing might have different sizing standards than modern clothing, so make sure to measure yourself and compare the sizes with the seller's charts. If in doubt, ask the seller for more information or try the item on if possible.

Check the Condition: Old western clothing might have some wear and tear, stains, or odors, depending on their age and usage. Make sure to check the item's description, photos, and condition before buying it. If you're not satisfied with the item's condition, don't hesitate to contact the seller and ask for a refund or return.

Consider Your Style: Old western clothing comes in various styles, from classic to modern, from rugged to elegant. Make sure to choose the items that reflect your personality and taste, and that match the occasion you're dressing up for. You can also mix and match old western clothing with modern clothing to create a unique and stylish outfit.

How to Care for Your Old Western Clothing

Now that you have your old western clothing, you might wonder how to take care of them and make them last longer. Here are some tips to help you:

Follow the Care Instructions: Old western clothing might have different care instructions than modern clothing, so make sure to read and follow the label's recommendations. Some items might need to be hand-washed, air-dried, or ironed at a low temperature. Avoid using bleach, harsh detergents, or dry-cleaning unless necessary.

Store Them Properly: Old western clothing might be sensitive to light, moisture, or pests, so make sure to store them in a cool, dry, and dark place. You can use garment bags, hangers, or boxes to protect them from dust, wrinkles, or creases. Avoid storing them in plastic bags or in direct sunlight.

Repair Them if Needed: Old western clothing might develop some damages or flaws over time, such as holes, tears, or loose stitches. Make sure to repair them as soon as possible to prevent further damage. You can use a sewing kit, a tailor, or a specialized repair shop to fix them.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Old Western Clothing

Buying old western clothing can be a fun and rewarding experience, as long as you know where to look and what to expect. Whether you prefer to shop online or offline, make sure to choose the best options that suit your needs and preferences. Remember to check the size, condition, and style of each item before buying it, and to take care of them properly once you have them. With these tips, you can dress up like a cowboy or a cowgirl and show off your western spirit!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I find old western clothing at a flea market?

A: Yes, you might find old western clothing at a flea market, but the selection and quality might vary. Make sure to check the item's condition and authenticity before buying it, and to negotiate the price if possible.

Q: What is the difference between vintage and retro clothing?

A: Vintage clothing refers to items that are at least 20 years old and have historical or cultural significance. Retro clothing refers to items that are inspired by or imitate vintage styles, but are not necessarily old. Both vintage and retro clothing can be valuable and stylish, depending on their quality and rarity.

Q: How do I know if an online seller is trustworthy?

A: You can check the seller's feedback, ratings, and reviews on the platform or on third-party websites. You can also contact the seller and ask for more information or photos about the item, and see how they respond. If you're not satisfied with the seller's communication or service, don't hesitate to look for another seller.

Q: How can I tell if an old western clothing item is authentic?

A: The authenticity of an old western clothing item depends on various factors, such as the brand, the age, the materials, and the design. Some signs of authenticity might include the label, the stitching, the hardware, and the wear and tear. You can also do some research on the item and the brand to verify its authenticity.
