Find Your Perfect Vintage Dress Online

Written by Aprilia Oct 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of scouring thrift stores for the perfect vintage dress, only to come up empty-handed? Do you want to add some vintage flair to your wardrobe, but don't know where to start? Look no further, because we have the answer to your problem: buying vintage dresses online.

As vintage clothing becomes increasingly popular, more and more people are turning to the internet to find unique and one-of-a-kind pieces. However, the vast selection of online stores can be overwhelming, and it can be hard to know where to start your search.

If you're wondering where to buy vintage dresses online, fear not. There are plenty of reputable online stores that specialize in vintage clothing, and we've rounded up some of the best options for you.

When it comes to buying vintage dresses online, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, make sure you do your research on the seller before making a purchase. Look for reviews from other customers, and check to see if the seller has a return policy in case the dress doesn't fit or isn't what you expected.

Another important factor to consider is sizing. Vintage clothing often runs smaller than modern sizes, so be sure to check the measurements provided by the seller and compare them to your own measurements.

Etsy

One of the best places to buy vintage dresses online is Etsy. With a wide selection of vintage clothing from sellers all over the world, you're sure to find something that fits your style and budget. Plus, Etsy has a great review system that allows you to see what other customers have to say about a seller before making a purchase.

Personally, I've had great luck finding unique vintage dresses on Etsy. I love that I can communicate directly with the seller and ask any questions I have before making a purchase. Plus, many sellers offer custom sizing options to ensure a perfect fit.

ASOS Marketplace

ASOS Marketplace is another great option for buying vintage dresses online. The site features a curated selection of vintage clothing from independent sellers, so you can trust that the quality is high and the styles are on-trend.

What I love about ASOS Marketplace is the variety of styles available. Whether you're looking for a 1920s flapper dress or a 1970s bohemian maxi, you're sure to find something that catches your eye.

Vintage Shops on Instagram

Many vintage shops have taken to Instagram to showcase their wares and make sales. This is a great option if you're looking for a specific style or piece, as many shops will post new arrivals on their Instagram page before adding them to their online store.

One of my favorite vintage shops on Instagram is @shopmoonage. They have a great selection of vintage dresses, and I love how they style their pieces in a modern way.

Avoiding Fakes

One thing to keep in mind when buying vintage dresses online is the risk of purchasing a fake. Some sellers may try to pass off a modern dress as a vintage piece, or may sell a dress with fake vintage labels. To avoid this, make sure to do your research on the brand and label of the dress before making a purchase.

Conclusion of where to buy vintage dresses online

When it comes to buying vintage dresses online, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you prefer the wide selection of Etsy, the curated styles of ASOS Marketplace, or the unique finds on Instagram, there's something for everyone.

Just remember to do your research on the seller, check the sizing carefully, and be on the lookout for fakes. With a little bit of patience and perseverance, you're sure to find the vintage dress of your dreams.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a vintage dress will fit me?

A: Be sure to check the measurements provided by the seller and compare them to your own measurements. Vintage clothing often runs smaller than modern sizes, so it's important to double-check before making a purchase.

Q: What should I look for when buying a vintage dress?

A: Look for high-quality fabrics, unique details, and classic silhouettes that will never go out of style.

Q: How can I avoid buying a fake vintage dress?

A: Do your research on the brand and label of the dress before making a purchase. Look for signs of wear and tear that would be expected in a vintage garment, and be wary of dresses that look brand new.

Q: Can I return a vintage dress if it doesn't fit?

A: It depends on the seller. Be sure to check their return policy before making a purchase.
