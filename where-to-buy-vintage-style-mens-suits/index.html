Where To Buy Vintage Style Mens Suits Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Oct 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a man who loves vintage style? Do you want to add some classic pieces to your wardrobe? If so, you might be wondering where to buy vintage style mens suits. It can be a challenging task to find the right stores that offer quality vintage suits at affordable prices. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll help you find the best places to buy vintage style suits for men.

When it comes to buying vintage style mens suits, there are a few pain points that you might face. First, it can be challenging to find the right size and fit. Vintage suits are often made differently from modern suits, and you might need to spend some time searching for the perfect fit. Second, it can be challenging to find quality suits that are still in good condition. Many vintage suits have wear and tear, and you don't want to spend money on something that won't last. Finally, it can be challenging to find vintage style suits that are affordable. Some stores charge a premium for vintage clothing, making it difficult to find a good deal.

Where to Buy Vintage Style Mens Suits: Our Top Picks

There are many places to buy vintage style mens suits, but we've narrowed down the list to our top picks. These stores offer quality suits, reasonable prices, and a wide selection of sizes and styles.

Etsy

If you're looking for a unique vintage style suit, Etsy is the perfect place to start. This online marketplace offers a wide variety of vintage suits from different eras and styles. You can find everything from 1920s gangster suits to 1970s disco suits. The sellers on Etsy are usually small vintage shops or individuals, so you can find some great deals. However, you should always read the reviews and check the measurements before making a purchase.

ASOS Marketplace

ASOS Marketplace is another great place to buy vintage style mens suits. This online marketplace offers a curated selection of vintage clothing from different sellers. You can find some unique and stylish suits on this platform, and the prices are reasonable. ASOS Marketplace has a system for verifying the authenticity and condition of the vintage items, so you can be sure that you're getting a quality suit.

Thrift Stores

If you're on a budget, thrift stores can be a great place to find vintage style mens suits. Thrift stores usually offer a wide selection of vintage clothing, including suits. You might need to spend some time searching through the racks, but you can find some great deals. However, keep in mind that the suits might not be in the best condition, and you might need to spend some money on alterations.

Vintage Shops

If you want to see the vintage suits in person, you can visit vintage shops in your area. These shops specialize in vintage clothing and offer a wide selection of suits from different eras and styles. However, vintage shops can be expensive, and you might need to spend some time searching for the right suit.

My Personal Experience

As a vintage style enthusiast, I've bought many vintage mens suits over the years. My go-to place for buying vintage suits is Etsy. I love browsing through the different shops and finding unique suits that fit my style. I've also bought suits from thrift stores, but I usually need to spend some money on alterations to get the right fit. Overall, I think Etsy is the best place to buy vintage style mens suits because of the wide selection, reasonable prices, and quality sellers.

Tips for Buying Vintage Style Mens Suits

Check the Measurements

When buying vintage style mens suits, it's essential to check the measurements. Vintage suits are often made differently than modern suits, and the sizes might not match up. Make sure to measure yourself and compare it to the measurements provided by the seller.

Inspect the Condition

Before buying a vintage suit, inspect the condition carefully. Look for any signs of wear and tear, such as stains or holes. If possible, try the suit on and check for any fit issues.

Consider Alterations

If you find a vintage suit that you love but doesn't fit perfectly, consider getting it altered. A good tailor can make adjustments to the suit to make it fit better. Keep in mind that alterations can add to the cost, so factor that into your budget.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I find vintage style mens suits at department stores?

A: It's possible to find vintage style suits at some department stores, but the selection might be limited. You might have better luck at vintage shops or online marketplaces like Etsy.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a vintage style mens suit?

A: The price of a vintage style mens suit can vary widely depending on the era, style, and condition. You can find some great deals for under $100, but some suits can cost several hundred dollars.

Q: Can I wear a vintage style suit to a modern event?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear a vintage style suit to a modern event. Just make sure that the suit fits the dress code and the occasion.

Q: How do I take care of a vintage style mens suit?

A: It's important to take care of your vintage style mens suit to ensure that it lasts. Make sure to store it properly, avoid wearing it too often, and have it dry cleaned when necessary.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Vintage Style Mens Suits

Buying vintage style mens suits can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following our tips and checking out our top picks, you can find the perfect vintage suit for your style and budget. Whether you're shopping on Etsy, ASOS Marketplace, or in-person vintage shops, make sure to check the measurements, inspect the condition, and consider alterations. With a little bit of patience and effort, you can add some classic pieces to your wardrobe that will never go out of style.
