Where To Buy Vintage Tees Online A Guide For Retro Fashion Lovers

Written by Oliver Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a fan of vintage fashion, you know that finding the perfect vintage tee can be a challenge. Whether you're looking for a band tee from the 70s or a graphic tee from the 90s, it can be tough to know where to start your search. Fortunately, the internet has made it easier than ever to find vintage tees online. In this guide, we'll show you where to buy vintage tees online and give you tips for finding the best deals.

Are you tired of scouring thrift stores and vintage shops in search of the perfect vintage tee? Do you want to know where to buy vintage tees online? Look no further! We've compiled a list of the top online stores for vintage tees, so you can shop from the comfort of your own home.

Overall, there are plenty of options for buying vintage tees online. From popular marketplaces like Etsy and eBay to specialized vintage clothing stores like Beyond Retro and Rokit, there are plenty of places to find unique and authentic vintage tees. Some popular online stores include ASOS Marketplace, Depop, and Etsy.

ASOS Marketplace

ASOS Marketplace is a popular online marketplace where you can buy vintage and retro clothing from a variety of independent boutiques and vintage sellers. With a wide range of vintage tees from the 80s and 90s, you're sure to find something you love.

Personally, I've had great experiences buying vintage tees on ASOS Marketplace. The prices are reasonable and the selection is great. Plus, the site makes it easy to search by size, color, and era.

Beyond Retro

Beyond Retro is a UK-based vintage clothing store with a great selection of vintage tees. They have a range of tees from different eras, including band tees, graphic tees, and sportswear. The prices can be a bit higher than other online stores, but the quality is excellent.

I recently bought a vintage Rolling Stones tee from Beyond Retro and was impressed with the quality of the shirt. It looked and felt like it was from the 70s, but was in great condition.

Etsy

Etsy is a popular online marketplace for handmade and vintage items. You can find a wide range of vintage tees on Etsy, from band tees to graphic tees to vintage sports tees. The selection is vast, so you're sure to find something you love.

I've bought several vintage tees on Etsy over the years and have always been happy with my purchases. The sellers are usually very responsive and will answer any questions you have about the item before you buy.

Rokit

Rokit is a UK-based vintage clothing store with a great selection of vintage tees. They have a range of tees from different eras, including band tees, graphic tees, and vintage sports tees. The prices are reasonable and the quality is excellent.

I recently bought a vintage Nirvana tee from Rokit and was impressed with the quality of the shirt. It looked and felt like it was from the 90s, but was in great condition.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a vintage tee is authentic?

A: Look for tags or labels that indicate the era the tee is from. You can also check for signs of wear and tear that would be consistent with the age of the tee.

Q: Can I return a vintage tee if it doesn't fit?

A: It depends on the store's return policy. Be sure to read the return policy before you buy.

Q: How do I care for a vintage tee?

A: Wash your vintage tee by hand or on a delicate cycle. Hang dry or lay flat to dry.

Q: Are vintage tees expensive?

A: It depends on the store and the tee. Some vintage tees can be expensive, but you can also find great deals if you know where to look.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Vintage Tees Online

Overall, there are plenty of options for buying vintage tees online. Whether you're looking for a band tee from the 70s or a graphic tee from the 90s, there's a store out there for you. Just be sure to read the return policy and check for signs of authenticity before you buy. Happy shopping!
