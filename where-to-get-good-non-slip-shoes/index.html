Avoid slips and falls with good non slip shoes. Whether you work in a restaurant, hospital, or any other industry where spills and wet floors are common, finding the right non slip shoes is crucial.

Have you ever slipped on a wet floor and felt embarrassed or injured yourself in the process? Slippery floors can be dangerous, especially if you are wearing regular shoes. That's why you need to invest in a good pair of non slip shoes that offer maximum traction and grip.

If you're wondering where to get good non slip shoes, there are several options to consider. Here are some of the best places to buy non slip shoes:

1. Online retailers

You can find a vast selection of non slip shoes on online retailers such as Amazon, Zappos, and Shoes for Crews. These websites offer a wide range of styles, colors, and sizes to suit your needs. The best part is that you can read reviews from other customers to help you make an informed decision.

2. Shoe stores

If you prefer trying on shoes before buying them, you can visit a local shoe store. Most shoe stores have a section dedicated to non slip shoes, and the staff can help you find the right pair. Plus, you can try on different sizes and styles to see what feels most comfortable.

3. Workwear stores

If you work in an industry that requires non slip shoes, you can check out workwear stores such as Dickies or Red Kap. These stores specialize in workwear and safety gear, including non slip shoes. You can find durable and reliable shoes that meet industry standards.

4. Manufacturer websites

Some brands sell their non slip shoes directly on their websites. For instance, Skechers has a line of non slip shoes for both men and women that you can purchase on their website. By buying from the manufacturer, you can ensure that you're getting a genuine product.

Now that you know where to get good non slip shoes, you can choose the best option for your needs. Remember to prioritize comfort and durability, and don't forget to read reviews before making a purchase.

What to look for in non slip shoes

When buying non slip shoes, there are a few factors to consider:

1. Traction

The most important feature of non slip shoes is their traction. Look for shoes with rubber soles that provide excellent grip on wet or oily surfaces. Some shoes also have special tread patterns that increase traction.

2. Comfort

You'll be wearing your non slip shoes for several hours a day, so comfort is essential. Look for shoes with cushioned insoles and breathable materials. Also, make sure the shoes fit well and don't rub against your feet.

3. Durability

Non slip shoes should be durable enough to withstand daily wear and tear. Look for shoes made from high-quality materials that won't fall apart after a few months. Some shoes also have reinforced toe caps or slip-resistant coatings that increase durability.

FAQs about where to get good non slip shoes

Q: How much do non slip shoes cost?

A: Non slip shoes can range from $30 to $150, depending on the brand and style.

Q: Do I need to buy special socks to wear with non slip shoes?

A: No, you can wear regular socks with non slip shoes. However, some brands sell socks that are designed to work with their shoes.

Q: Can I wear non slip shoes outside of work?

A: Yes, non slip shoes can be worn for any activity where traction is essential, such as hiking or walking on slippery surfaces.

Q: How do I know if the shoes I'm buying are genuinely non slip?

A: Look for shoes that meet industry standards for slip resistance, such as ASTM or SATRA. You can also check reviews from other customers to see if they have had issues with slipping.

Conclusion of where to get good non slip shoes

Investing in a good pair of non slip shoes is essential if you work in an industry where falls and slips are common. By buying from reputable retailers and prioritizing comfort, traction, and durability, you can find the perfect pair of non slip shoes for your needs.
