How to Use a Hot Water Bag Safely?

When using a hot water bag, it is crucial to follow these safety guidelines:

  • Fill the hot water bag with hot, not boiling water
  • Check for leaks before use
  • Use a cover or towel to prevent burns
  • Do not sleep with the hot water bag
  • Do not use the hot water bag for more than 15-20 minutes at a time

FAQs
