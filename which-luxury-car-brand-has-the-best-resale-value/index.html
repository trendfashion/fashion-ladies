Which Luxury Car Brand Has The Best Resale Value

Written by Christine May 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to buying a luxury car, one of the biggest concerns for many buyers is the resale value. Luxury cars are a significant investment, and buyers want to make sure they get the most value out of their purchase if they decide to sell it in the future. In this post, we will answer the question of which luxury car brand has the best resale value and provide insights into why.

It's no secret that luxury cars can quickly depreciate over time, resulting in a significant loss of value for the owner. However, some luxury car brands hold their value better than others, making them a better investment in the long run.

The luxury car brand that has the best resale value is Lexus. According to Kelley Blue Book's 2021 Best Resale Value Awards, Lexus was named the best resale value luxury brand. This is not the first time Lexus has won this award, and it's not surprising considering their reputation for reliability and durability.

In summary, if you're looking for a luxury car that will hold its value well over time, Lexus is the brand to go for. Their reputation for reliability and durability makes them a wise investment for the long run.

The Reliability of Lexus:

I have been a long-time Lexus owner, and I can attest to the brand's reliability. My Lexus has never broken down or required any significant repairs, even after years of use. The car's build quality is exceptional, and I feel confident that it will last me for many years to come. This is why I believe that Lexus is the luxury car brand with the best resale value.

The Durability of Lexus:

Another reason why Lexus has the best resale value is the durability of their cars. Lexus cars are built to last, with high-quality materials and excellent craftsmanship. Even after years of use, Lexus cars still look and feel like new, which is a major selling point for buyers who are looking for a used luxury car.

The Importance of Regular Maintenance:

While Lexus cars are known for their reliability and durability, it's important to note that regular maintenance is crucial to maintaining their value. Regular oil changes, tire rotations, and other routine maintenance tasks can go a long way in keeping your Lexus running smoothly and preserving its resale value.

How to Take Care of Your Lexus:

To ensure that your Lexus retains its value over time, it's essential to take good care of it. Here are some tips on how to take care of your Lexus:

  • Follow the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule
  • Keep your car clean, both inside and out
  • Protect your car from the elements by parking in a garage or using a car cover
  • Drive carefully and avoid harsh driving conditions whenever possible

The Future of Resale Value for Lexus:

Given Lexus's reputation for reliability and durability, it's safe to assume that their cars will continue to hold their value well into the future. As more and more buyers become aware of Lexus's excellent resale value, demand for their cars will likely increase, further driving up their value.

Question and Answer:

Q: Is Lexus the only luxury car brand with good resale value?

A: No, there are other luxury car brands with good resale value, such as Porsche and Audi. However, Lexus consistently ranks as the best luxury car brand for resale value.

Q: Why do luxury cars depreciate so quickly?

A: Luxury cars are expensive to maintain and repair, which can drive down their resale value. Additionally, luxury car buyers are often looking for the latest and greatest models, which can make older models less desirable and reduce their value.

Q: How can I find out the resale value of a specific Lexus model?

A: You can use online tools such as Kelley Blue Book or Edmunds to find the resale value of a specific Lexus model.

Q: Should I buy a new or used Lexus if I want to maximize resale value?

A: Buying a new Lexus will typically result in a higher resale value than buying a used Lexus. However, buying a used Lexus that has been well-maintained can still result in a good resale value.

Conclusion of Which Luxury Car Brand Has the Best Resale Value:

When it comes to buying a luxury car, it's essential to consider resale value as a factor in your decision. Lexus is the luxury car brand with the best resale value, thanks to their reputation for reliability and durability. By taking good care of your Lexus and following the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule, you can ensure that your car retains its value for years to come.
