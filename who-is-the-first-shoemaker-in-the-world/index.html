What Was The Neolithic Period?

The Neolithic period, also known as the New Stone Age, was a time when humans shifted from hunting and gathering to agriculture and domestication of animals. During this period, humans also developed new technologies and tools, such as pottery, weaving, and metalworking. The Neolithic period began around 10,000 BCE and ended around 4,500 BCE.
